Puerto Rico under state of emergency due to Zika virus by AFP, Juliet de Loza-Cudia August 14, 2016 the United States declared the declared Puerto Rico under state of emergency due to the widespread transmission of the Zika virus.
it was told to the United States (US) Secretary of Health and Human Services, the spread of the Zika virus is a big threat for the public health in Puerto Rico.
the declaration is based on the request of Rico Governor Alejandro Garcia Padilla. this was allegedly for the allotment of additional funds for Puerto Rico in tightening the campaign against the Zika virus.
it was said that Puerto Rico is the commonwealth of United States.
currently, the confirmed case of Zika virus in Puerto Rico has reached 10,960 wherein 1,035 are pregnant women.
