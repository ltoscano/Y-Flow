
The case of Zitto Kabwe, April first.

August 24, 2014.

The high court in Dar es Salaam, yesterday postponed the case of the former deputy secretary general of the Party of Democracy and Development (CHADEMA), Mister Zitto Kabwe, to take any decision on his membership because that decision was presented yesterday to April first this year by the registrar of parliament of that party, Mister Peter Kibatala and his request, Mister Msepwa.
