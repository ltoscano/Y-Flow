
Kenya is accused of attackers.

November 10, 2013.

Kenya has released the names of four men who did the attack against the West Brom area in Nairobi two weeks ago where more than 60 people were killed by those men were seen in the pictures of the security camera that was found in that area being attacked by four days.
According to BBC, the army's spokesperson has mentioned the men to be Abu Dhabi on South Sudan, Omar Naburhan Mlau, and the army spokesperson explained that all the attackers were killed, where one was a citizen of Kenya.
