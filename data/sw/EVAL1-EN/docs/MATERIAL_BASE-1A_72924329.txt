
Karume Stadium in Pemba will start giving service.

It has been written by Makame Suleiman, Zanzibar.

It has been published in September 2016.

The Karume Memorial Stadium in Pemba has now started providing services to land and taking off at all times including night.

That situation is as a result of the major renovations that were done by putting efforts to lead the aircraft and that came to land at night, the project caused the Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar more than 1 billion shillings.
The Minister of State, Office of the President and the chairperson of Revolutions Council, Issa Haji Ussi (Gogo) said that the step is part of the efforts that were taken by the President of Zanzibar, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein to see the Island of Pemba makes a step in economic development and in the tourism sector.
Gwagilo says that the project is the deputy minister of communication and infrastructure, he said the completion of that project is another big opportunity for the growth of the economy in Pemba.
He said this during the tour of the president of the United Republic of Tanzania, John Magufuli at the end of the week in Pemba Island.
After finishing to address the citizens, Magufuli left the plane through that stadium to Unguja together with Doctor Shein and other leaders of Zanzibar and the union.
