
President Magufuli to launch a new plane.

It has been written by Lilian Liundi.

I was published today 27th September 2016.

President Doctor John Magufuli tomorrow is expected to launch two planes of Tanzania Air Limited Company (ATC) that brought him using taxes of citizens.

Speaking to journalists in Dar es Salaam city about the launch of the plane, the Secretary General of the Ministry of Works, Transport and Communications that deals with transport, Doctor Leonard Lutegama Maboko said the launch will be held at the Julius Nyerere International Airport (Terminal 1).
Doctor Silencer Mapuranga, said the first plane arrived in the country on September 20 and the second plane was expected to arrive in the country today at 6 o'clock in the afternoon.
That secretary general has also said that those planes have been bought by the government in order to serve the public and outside Tanzania.
"These type of 8 Bombardier Q400 type which are made by the Bombardier Q400 model in Canada, which every one has a capacity to carry 76 passengers and will be used in serving the Haniya Tanzania market and the neighboring country," explained Doctor Chamwamba.
In addition, he said the government of Tanzania invites all citizens to witness the launch of those planes that are expected to start at two o'clock in the morning.
