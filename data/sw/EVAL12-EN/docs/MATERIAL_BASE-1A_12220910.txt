RC, Dodoma did not want jokes.

It has been written by Swaliatu Msuya of Dodoma.

It has been released on 09 / 2016.

Dodoma Provincial Commissioner Jordan Rugimbana, has urged the Capital Development Authority (CDA) to stop the purchase of land for the citizens because it started fighting conflicts and many complaints on land grabbing.

Rugimbana, said that today while entertaining members of the land council of Dodoma town and Kondoa provinces.
The topic took place on September 10 for more news.
