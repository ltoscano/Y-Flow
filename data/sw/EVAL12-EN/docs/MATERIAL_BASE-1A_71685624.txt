President John Magufuli has been swearing in Doctor Ramadhani Shauri to be the ambassador of Tanzania in Malaysia.

Before being appointed to be Ambassador, Doctor Mwinyimvua was the executive director of the National Social Security Fund (NSSF).

Doctor Mwinyimvua was appointed as the ambassador of February 15 this year, together with the ministers in the fourth government, Mathias Chikawe and Doctor Asha-Rose Migiro.
On April 13th this year, Chikawe was appointed as the ambassador of Tanzania to Japan, while Doctor was appointed before being the ambassador of Tanzania to England.

Those two have been taking place with Benedict Ole Ntimama that took place at Benedict Liwenga.
