
The reports of drug effects on the internet.

It has been written by Hellen Mlenda.

I was published today, 13th October, 2016.

Tanzania Food and Drugs Authority (TFDA), has launched a electronic system of giving information on drugs, the system will help to protect the community health by using huge effects, deaths or disabilities that will be caused by drugs.

The Executive Director of TFDA, Hiiti Sillo said in Dar es Salaam city that the system that was organized by the experts of information and communication technology (ICT) of Dodoma University (UDOM), has been tested in 10 provinces.
He mentioned those provinces to be Dar es Salaam, Manyara, Arusha, Iringa, Tanga, Kagera, Ruvuma, Mwanza, Mtwara and Morogoro and that the system seems to be able to ease the provision of information on the effects of drugs.
"This system will enable the directors of health, patients, and citizens to give information on human effects, by using mobile phones and also computers connected to the internet through our website which is in the past," said Sillo.
Sillo said that in the sender of the information, there are a special form that if they are yellow in blood is special for health practitioners and the form that are special to patients, brothers, friend or close relatives and a patient.
He has said that in the period of trials, June up to August this year, they have received a total of reports on the effects of 36 drugs and among those 32 were forms for yellow and four of the fourth form.
In addition, he said TFDA will continue with other electronic systems by using their foreign experts so as to increase efficiency in serving their customers and citizens.
The minister for health, community development, gender, the elderly and children, Ummy Mwalimu has ordered TFDA for improving the system so that it can be used by every person that they have at their phones even if they are advising them by sending a short message of words (VVU).
