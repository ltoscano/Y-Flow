More than 800 million shillings have been collected from public organizations and government institutions through special fundraising, set aside for victims of the earthquake in Kagera province that was done by the registrar of treasury, Lawrence Mafuru.

Speaking during the fundraising, Mafuru said that institutions and public organizations are the responsibility of contributing to the state and assets that arise in the country.
Among the organizations and institutions that contribute to the government's newspapers agency (TBC), Tanzania Broadcasting Corporation (TBC), Tanzania Revenue Authority (TANAPA), Tanzania Ports Authority (TPA) and the Tanzania Ports Authority (TPA) and the Ngorongoro Conservation Authority.
