
Magufuli has ordered TPA to save the port.

It has been written by Hanscana.

It has been published today, 26th of September.

President Doctor John Magufuli has urged the Tanzania Ports Authority (TPA) to prepare to build land port (TD) Ruvu in Pwani province.

President Magufuli has urged TPA to stop depending on the port that were started by private people in Dar es Salaam city because they are used to evade taxes.
He made those instructions today when he toured Dar es Salaam port. During the tour, President Magufuli has spoken to TPA employees.
He has explained that those employees and others in the government are saying that the exercise of reviewing certificates is ongoing in the whole country has no aim to chase them but to deal with ghost workers including those who use certificates.
