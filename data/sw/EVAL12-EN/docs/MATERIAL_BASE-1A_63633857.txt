
SMZ being built to build a modern hospital in heaven.

It has been written by Makame Suleiman, Zanzibar.

It has been published today, September 2016.

The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar has said the new modern hospital will be built in heaven and Central Africa with the status of the Referral Hospital that has the ability to provide all medical services to the citizens of Unguja and Pemba.

That was said by the minister for health, Mahmoud Thabit Kombo, while answering a question by the representative of Tunguu, Simai Mohamed who wanted to know how the construction of the hospital will start.
Kombo said the step of the construction of the modern hospital in heaven is part of the implementation of the revolutionary party manifesto (CCM) and the promises that were issued by the Zanzibar presidential candidate on the party ticket in the general elections, last year.
He said that the implementation of that promise is that and will be implemented in actions with the aim of improving health services to reach all citizens at the required quality, giving an example, he said in a new hospitals in heaven and Central Africa, all services of health centers will be realized including cancer treatment.
"Heaven hospital will have status and reputation of the Referral Hospital and therefore meeting the medical services to the citizens of Unguja and Pemba," he said.
He said that the decision of the construction of heaven hospital has come after the current hospital at Mnazi Mmoja from being affected by a large number of people, including patients who come from all the two areas of Unguja and Pemba.
