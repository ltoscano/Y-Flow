Majaliwa told Serengeti Boys.

It has been written by Angela Semwenda, Dodoma.

I was heard of 16th September 2016.

The Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa has congratulated the youth team of the youth under the age of 17, Serengeti Boys in a match to score a large soccer team in South Africa in the games to look for a chance to qualify for the African Cup competition to youths of that age.

He made those congratulations today in parliament in Dodoma town when he addressed the fourth meeting of the 11 parliament.
He has said that the action of that team can be well prepared and urged the players to continue self-employed so as to bring success to the nation.
"I would like to congratulate the players of this team, they have done good work and now it is in the camp to climb with the game against Congo Brazzavile, I have faith that they will do well and participate in those finals," said the Prime Minister.
The finals of the tournament aiming to take place in Madagascar next year. Minister Majaliwa gave advice to stakeholders and lovers of soccer in the country in general to support the team so that it can fulfill its goal.
That time, parliamentarians and lovers of Simba and Yanga teams are expected to meet on the pitch in a friendly game that will take place at the National Stadium in Dar es Salaam.
