
Doctor Shein wanted to improve the security of South Africa.

It has been written by special writer, Zanzibar.

I was revealed at 5 October, 2016.

The president of Zanzibar and the chairperson of Revolutions Council, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein has called for the ministry of internal affairs and the police force in the country to strengthen security in the southern region of Unguja Island where many tourism activities are being held.

He made that call yesterday when he spoke to the Minister of Internal Affairs of the country, Mwigulu Nchemba who visited him in his office at State House in Zanzibar.
"You have run it well on the issue of security of our country, but in Zanzibar we have a lot of tourism and the region of East Africa where there are many services of that business is clear," Doctor Shein told the minister.
Previously, Mwigulu told President Shein that during this period of future future, his ministry explains the issue of security of South Africa to be important especially considering the issue of improving the gas and oil sector in the northern region of our country.
In another step, Doctor Shein told that minister about the increase of deaths arising from road accidents together with bribery actions done by some traffic police who are against the ethics of their work.
