
DAWASCO aims at Ruvu Shooting at 4 o'clock.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published in 1st September 2016.

The machine for producing water from Ruvu Shooting will be aired on the 14th average to allow purification of the new pipeline and to do the trials for the new plant.

Dar es Salaam Water Utilities Regulatory Authority in Dar es Salaam (DAWASCO), has said that exercise is taking the final stages of expansion of the plant in order to eliminate the problem of availability of water services in the areas that are served by Ruvu Shooting plant.
DAWASCO has said that the machine will take place from 3 o'clock in the morning until 5 o'clock in the morning up to 5 trilion shillings night, on Friday 12 / 09 / 2016.
According to the report, the areas that missed water due to the discovery of that machine is Mlandizi town, Ruvu Darajani, Vikuruti, Maasais', Maasais', Zekij, Zekij and Maasais.
Others are Msakup, Salim Mbonde, group photo, Surely, Lamu, Kibamba, Kibamba, Kibamba, Kibamba, Kibamba, Kibamba, and Lugome.
