
Kabila to land for a three day tour.

It has been written by Sophia Kaduma.

I was published (03 / 2016.

President Joseph Kabila of the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), is expected to arrive in the country today for a three day tour.

The Provincial Commissioner of Dar es Salaam, Paul Makonda, said President Kabila will be received by President John Magufuli at the Julius Nyerere International Airport (JNIA).
Makonda told the journalists yesterday that during that tour, Kabila will talk about different issues with his host and tomorrow he will visit the port of Dar es Salaam.
Despite President Kabila is also said the vice president of Cuba, Salvador Memba will come into the country for a two day tour and will be received by the Vice President, Samia Suluhu Hassan.
He also said that in that province, there was a visitor of people who live in 30,000 in which they are doing a general meeting in the country after accepting the request of President Magufuli of doing their meeting in the country.
