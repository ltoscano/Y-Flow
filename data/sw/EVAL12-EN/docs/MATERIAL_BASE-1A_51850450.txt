DRC to strengthen business and Tanzania.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published in October, 2016.

President Joseph Kabila of the Democratic Republic of Congo has said his country will improve business and Tanzania in order to improve the economy of those countries.

President Kabila has said that while laying the foundation stone of the Tanzania Ports Authority (TPA) in Dar es Salaam city.
Previously President John Magufuli has said that anyone who will be responsible for DRC to be unable to do business through the port of Dar es Salaam, will be taken against him and requested the DRC ambassador in the country, Jean Pierre Mutta to give his information about any discomfort that will get the business people in the country.
