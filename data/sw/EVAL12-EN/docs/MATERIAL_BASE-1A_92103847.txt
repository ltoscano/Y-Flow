By Bashir Yakub.
The residents of Mtwala residents have requested them to leave the area of Kilale who have complained to have stuck for the project of making machines for removing water into the area.
Being led by the representative of Muembe Makundi Ward, Mister Bakari Ali said one of the projects that were stuck on is one of the construction of illegal water tools that despite starting to be implemented two years ago, you have no any information about it.
"It is clear that because this projects was implemented by public funds but how it was done now is a great issue," said Bakari.
The representative of Muembe Makundi Ward said that there must be transparency in an amount of projects that are implemented by using public funds.
