
Finally, January 16, 2013.

The commission of constitution bans me in Mkapa.

The top leadership of the Constitutional Review Commission today has thrown out his home to the retired president, Benjamin Mkapa, Hotel, in Dar es Salaam city to get his views on the new constitution.
The ones that went to Mkapa (in the picture) are delegates of the commission and the Secretariat of the Constitutional Review Commission in Dar es Salaam led by the chairman Judge Joseph Warioba (left).
Others are the deputy chairperson of the Retired Chief Justice Augustino Ramadhani (second on the right), a member of the commission Doctor Salim Ahmed Salim (right) and the secretary of Commission Asser Rashid (third from right).
