More than 70 children with heart problems today they are starting to undergo a huge operation of heart at the Jakaya Kikwete Cardiac Institute (JKCI) in Dar es Salaam city.

Previously there were reports that those children are in danger of lack of surgery services due to the shortage of blood in the Muhimbili National Hospital (MNH).
Speaking with this newspaper, the Relations Officer of that institution, Maulid Mohammed, has said the operation will start today because the blood is from there and another one will continue to be paid.
He has said that in the operation of blood pressure for a long time it was not known, "said there are different groups that have come out to contribute towards while others are going to come out and including the workers of the electricity organization (TANESCO) from Kigamboni, in the outskirts of Dar es Salaam.
We need a lot of blood because the need is not only children but people of all of us who are supposed to be researched on, "he said.
He said there is need to build a culture of producing blood every time so as to help save the lives of others with blood needs, the operation is expected to continue until September 19 this year and more than 300 members are needed to resolve it.
