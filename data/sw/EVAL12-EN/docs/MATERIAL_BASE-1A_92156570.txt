Nape to be handed over to the victims.

It has been written by Halima Kimwana.

It has been published in 21st September 2016.

Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye has received a donation of shillings.

10 million from the construction company pension fund has been building for the purpose of helping victims of the earthquake that happened on September 10, in Kagera Province.
That earthquake caused the deaths of 17 people and several injured while the residence of people and other buildings were destroyed and were destroyed, equivalent to various infrastructure.
Speaking after receiving that donation in Dar es Salaam yesterday, the minister said that the donation is part of the fruits of the efforts that are ongoing done by the government in conjunction with its institutions including the National Bureau of Standards (TBC).
He said that the organization has organized a fundraising which will enable various development stakeholders to help them the victims of that disaster.
"This is just the beginning because we are expecting that this evening (yesterday) we will be able to receive more donations from other stakeholders through the TBC program," he insisted.
He called for private individuals, organizations and various institutions to continue coming out in large numbers to help those victims.
