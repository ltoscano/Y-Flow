We need to learn language in Germany.

By Agnes Chaki.

The German culture institution in the country of GeitaMGODI of Tanzania has said that it has a plan to take advantage of how to take advantage of the German language to Germany, so that they can learn more language.
The Head of Language in that institution, Samuel Gavyole said that in Dar es Salaam yesterday at a ceremony to give them certificates to the secondary school students of Zanibar and Chang'ombe, who passed the German examinations.

He said at the moment we shall reach the students of secondary schools to be able to have a big awareness to study in Germany different from when the institution starts work in the country.

"When we start with a few students, but at the moment we use that awareness, we have more than 250 students now, but 15 are not students of secondary schools," he said.

He added that the institution has been taking two students in Germany every year to learn so as to encourage others to study in Germany.

He urged Tanzanians to come out to learn in Germany because there is a great importance to learn different languages in order to know different languages and work in office where the institution expects to start taking their students to go to Germany at their cost.
