
In the office of the vice president opens an evaluation of the earthquake.

It has been written by Angela Kizigha, Bukoba.

It has been reported in 23 September 2016.

The office of the Vice President will announce the work of being charged with environmental destruction in areas that were affected by the earthquake in Kagera province.

Minister of State, Office of the Vice President, United Republic and Environment, January Makamba gave the statement while speaking to the chairman of the disaster committee who is also the provincial commissioner of Kagera, Major General, Salum Kijuu in Bukoba town when he made a work tour of the areas that were affected by the earthquake in Missouri.
Minister Makamba instructed one of the expert from the National Social Security Council (UNHCR) to join with the team of experts who are in Ihanga village, who are doing the effects of the earthquake so that the system can be taken by the government in improving the infrastructure that was affected by the earthquake.
The Provincial Commissioner of Kagera, Salum Kijuu said that they have been asked experts to pass through every area that were affected by the religious institutions and the schools of those institutions so as to see the effects that happened.
