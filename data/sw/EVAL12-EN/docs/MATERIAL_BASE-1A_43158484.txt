
Doctor Kabila is the new secretary general of Tamisemi.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been published today, September 2016.

President Doctor John Magufuli has appointed Doctor Zainab Chaula to be deputy secretary general, office of the president, regional administration and local government (TAMISEMI) from today.

The state house Dar es Salaam city has said that before that appointment, Doctor Zainab Chaula was chief physician in charge of Dodoma Provincial Referral Hospital.
Doctor Zainab Chaula taking the position that was left vacant by who was the deputy secretary general, TAMISEMI, Doctor Deodatus Balile who has retired.
Doctor Zainab Chaula will be sworn in September 26th, 2016, at State House in Dar es Salaam.
