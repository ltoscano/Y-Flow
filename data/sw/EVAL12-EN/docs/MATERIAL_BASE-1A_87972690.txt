By George Mshenga.
The business people in Narok Kenya have complained to the cost of electricity power from time to time, a situation that has mentioned they have caused a loss of more than 80 million shillings.
According to the chairman of the union of traders in Narok Central Region (NCBA) Mister David Santa, company of electricity services in the country, Kenya Power, is supposed to compensate the business people from that loss.
Moreover, he added that business that were affected are those of selling food and provision of health services like the clinic.
"Disappearance of electricity every time we increase the cost of doing business so that it may reduce the level of investment," he said.
