The government gets the act of 159.

It has been written by Angela Semwenda, Dodoma.

I was heard of 16th September 2016.

A total of 159 students have been taken to study outside the country and the government in the financial year of 2015 / 2016.

The Deputy Minister of Education Science, Technology and Vocational Training, Stella Manyanya said those students went to study in China, Algeria, Russia, Egypt and England through the African Community.
Manyanya noted that while responding to a question by the nominated member of parliament, Taiyo (CCM) who wanted to know the budget of the year 2015 / 2016, the government took many students to study outside the country and areas which were given priority.
Responding to the question, Manyanya said in that financial year the government took 159 students and mentioned those countries with the number of students being China (84), Russia (4), Russia (4), Russia (4) and England through the African Community (12).
Manyanya said the government has been taking the students outside the country by considering the priorities of the nation which include science, technology, and communication technology (ICT).
He said students who are taken out of the country depend on the first degree, masters, and doctorate.
