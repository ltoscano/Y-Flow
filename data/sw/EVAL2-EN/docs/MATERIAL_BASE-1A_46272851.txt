
Tanzania is best in the world services and money.

It has been written by Halima Kimwana.

It has been published by 19th September 2016.

Tanzania has been mentioned to be one of the countries that are developed in the world in the use of mobile phones and money through the internet.

That was noted at the six international meeting of discussing the way to improve more services and money through the internet, that was organized by the Central Bank of Tanzania (BoT) in collaboration with Tanzania Communications Regulatory Authority (TCRA).
Opening the meeting in Dar es Salaam city today, the Governor of BoT Professor Benno Ndulu said that since the start of the use of phone services and money through the network of the year 2008, Tanzania has taken a big step in that area.
