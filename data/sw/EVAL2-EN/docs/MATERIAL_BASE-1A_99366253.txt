
Minister Kindamba and the new executive officer of TTCL.

Has been written by our correspondent.

It has been reported in 23 September 2016.

President Doctor John Magufuli has appointed Minister Kindamba to be the acting executive officer of TTCL.

The State House in Dar es Salaam city has said that the appointment was following the step by the government to buy all shares of Bharti Airtel who was the running mate of Tanzania Telecommunications Company Limited (TTCL) and therefore the government uses shares of that company by 100 percent.
Kindamba has been appointed to take the position from today May 23, 2016.
According to that report following that appointment, the former chief executive officer of TTCL, Doctor Kamugisha Kazaura will be given another job.
