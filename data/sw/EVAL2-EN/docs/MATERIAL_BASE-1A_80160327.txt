A new coach of Azam FC to land.

July 31, 2016.
The new coach of Azam FC is expected to land in that squad earlier today, before the players of the team going back to the camp yesterday, while speaking to journalists in the first round match against Mbeya City that ended in a draw of 3-3 goals at the Azam Complex Stadium, Chamazi, Dar es Salaam.
