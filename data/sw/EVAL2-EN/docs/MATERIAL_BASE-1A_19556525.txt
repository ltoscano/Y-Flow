
KBony has announced to increase the teams that are participating in the English Premier League.
By Bashir Yakub.
Tanzania Football Federation (KBN) has added the number of teams that will participate in the premier league matches to reach 20 this season.
Announcing those remarks yesterday, the Secretary General of the Federation of KFm Joseph Amen said that 20 teams will qualify to compete in two East and East African groups.
Every group will have 10 teams where four best teams in every group are the ones that will qualify for the quarter-finals of 2015.
Zuniga scored against 3-1 goals in the semi-finals of the new champions of 2014.
Amen announced that the team (Junction) has been eliminated from the league of men in the first level by getting to reach the stadium for five consecutive matches.
