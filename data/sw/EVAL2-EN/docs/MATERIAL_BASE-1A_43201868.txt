
Professor Mbarawa makes changes in leadership.

It has been written by us.

It has been published by 19th September 2016.

The minister of works, transport and communication, Professor Makame Mbarawa has made changes of leadership by putting the former managing director of Tanzania Railways Company (MARI), Doctor Musa Mgwatu to give him to the Technical Electrical Agency (TEMESA).

Minister Mbarawa has made those changes after President Doctor John Magufuli giving the changes in improving performance.
Moreover, Mbarawa has appointed the executive director of the National Housing Corporation (TRL), Masanja Kadogosa to be the Executive Director of RCO and he will continue with his position of the director of TRL.
