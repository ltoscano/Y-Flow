
Children under the heart.

It has been written by Hanscana.

It has been published yesterday / 03 / 2016.

The Jakaya Kikwete Cardiac Institute (JKCI), will do a heart operation of one day for children under the age of 10 years.

Following that operation, the institution has urged parents and guardians of children with heart problem to take their children on Monday starting from two o'clock in the morning at the Muhimbili National Hospital, Dar es Salaam.
The public information that was released yesterday by the relations officer of that institution, Maulid Mwita, explained that the operation will be done for one day and it will make them get more than 10 years.
"We will reduce all children who will visit that investigation will be conducted by specialist doctors of our institution," said Mgaka in that report.
He said together with the operation to take place in Dar es Salaam, that day also at the Bugando Referral Hospital in Mwanza town, will run that investigation and has requested the parents of the Lake Region to take children for investigation.
