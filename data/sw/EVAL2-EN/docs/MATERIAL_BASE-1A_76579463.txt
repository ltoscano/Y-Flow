Samia leads the tree planting campaign.

It has been written by me.

I was published in 1st October, 2016.

The Vice President, Samia Suluhu Hassan today will lead the residents of Dar es Salaam city at the launch of a tree planting campaign that will start at 12 o'clock in the morning at the Julius Nyerere International Airport (ACTL).

In line with a tree, Samia will inspect water infrastructure that was built to ensure that the trees that will be planted, and grow well.
Speaking to journalists yesterday, the head of that province, Paul Makonda said the preparation of a tree planting exercise is complete and that they are ready to receive the vice president.
He said that the launch of "my tree 'campaign will take place from NIC International Airport in Ilala district and later in Temeke District where trees will be planted on Kilwa road, Uhuru road up to Gymkhana area and Kinondoni.
