The tennis game of youth under the age of 18 years is expected to take place on November 19 and 20 at Gymkhana grounds in Dar es Salaam.

The coach of the tennis team of the disabled, has told this newspaper yesterday that those competitions will involve women with disabilities and those who do not have.
He said that with disabilities will play their colleagues in Kenya while those who do not face others from the provinces of Dar es Salaam, Morogoro and Arusha.
"On our part we are continuing with exercises for allowing us because we want to do well and continue with our record," he said and added that they are expecting to get competition from their neighbors Kenya who have a better team.
Salum said the competition has an importance for youths to continue improving themselves in the international competition to donate.
That team with disabilities has been putting efforts to participate in many internal and foreign competitions and to get great success, recently participated in the international competition that was organized by South Africa and also participated in the open competition of Kenya that brought together different countries and held the second position.
