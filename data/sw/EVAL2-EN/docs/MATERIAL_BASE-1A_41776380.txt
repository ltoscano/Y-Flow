
Norway brings us Tanzania.

It has been written by Shadrack Sagna.

It has been witnessed, 03 / 2016.

The Ambassador of Norway in the country Hanne-Marie Le Pen has congratulated Tanzania for his decision to start the process of giving the contract of Paris.

He said that yesterday when he was speaking at the environment and climate change organized by Mwalimu Nyerere at the University of Dar es Salaam.
He said the agreement aims at improving the effects of climate change and start with the process of the side of Tanzania is a big step.
The qualifications have been issued when tomorrow Friday at Marrakech in Morocco to take place in the first meeting which every country is heading to explaining how it organized to implement that agreement whose aim is to reduce the effects arising from the climate change in the country.
"The meeting will look more strategies of every country in addressing the priorities of the Paris contract, especially in areas to reduce debts, availability of money, transparency, relocating the technology, immigrants," said the United Nations.
While launching the conference that was attended by participants from different countries, the Vice President Samia Suluhu Hassan said that Tanzania has started the process of giving them that declaration.
