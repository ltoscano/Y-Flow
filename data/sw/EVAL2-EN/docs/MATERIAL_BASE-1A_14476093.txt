
Body of Doctor Mvungi to go to Doctor Shein today.

July 31, 2016.

The president of Zanzibar and the chairperson of Revolutions Council, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein, has sent condolences to the chairman of the commission on constitutional changes, retired Judge Joseph Masikitiko who was sworn in today November 12, this year, at the National Housing Corporation (MOI), he said that the body will arrive at Karimjee stadium tomorrow evening and to the late Doctor Shein has explained his disappointment in his home, Kibamba, in Kinondoni Province for the burial which was held on November 18th, 2013, in Dar es Salaam city, where he was arriving at Karimjee Grounds in Dar es Salaam city.
