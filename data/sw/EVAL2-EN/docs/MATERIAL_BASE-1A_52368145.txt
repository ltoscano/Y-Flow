
The sitting for Mama Samia and Mitawi has been put on Saturday in Njombe Province, when the police force wanted the citizens to be calm while waiting for the results.

A fire whose cause is not yet known until now it has burnt down some of the mothers stalls with hand close to the bus station in Njombe Province.

However, the fire squad in that province has succeeded to put off the fire night where police have said that the investigation of the incident is going on.

According to that squad, other stalls have already been destroyed in that accident.

However, that squad has urged citizens to be calm while this time waiting for the election results which is expected to be released any time in that province.

According to Clouds TV television station, police in that province have said the elections in the province took place with calmness and spreading it on the fire that was done, many citizens were in their homes.
