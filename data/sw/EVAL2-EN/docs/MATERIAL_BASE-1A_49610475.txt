
Open tournament in Arusha.

It has been written by Zubeida Malick.

It has been published in 1st September 2016.

Open competition of basketball known as "Kilimanjaro Marathon contract is expected to kick off in the city on September 10 to 17 this year.

Already 13 provinces in the country has confirmed to participate in the competition that was organized by the basketball party in Arusha province (on the right) in collaboration with the Tanzania Football Federation (T).
Provinces that confirmed their participation in Dar es Salaam, Mbeya, Shinyanga, Mwanza, Morogoro, Iringa, Kilimanjaro, Mtwara, Rukwa, Tanga, Singida and host Arusha.
Moreover, there is a team from Zanzibar and Mombasa, Kenya are expected to participate.
While speaking yesterday, a delegate on the tour, Bartez had said that the competition is taking place for the third season since they were started officially in the year 2014 with the aim of announcing the Mount Kilimanjaro and various tourist attractions available in the northern region as well as to maintain the relationship between the basketball players.
