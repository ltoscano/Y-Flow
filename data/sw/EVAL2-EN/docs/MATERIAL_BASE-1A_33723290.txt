The 29th September 2016

The investigation of a case involving three parliamentarians and two councilors of the Party of Democracy and Development (CHADEMA) in a case of allegedly stealing the former administrative secretary of Dar es Salaam Province, Theresia Mbamba, has been completed.
The Republic being led by Attorney General, Flora Molel, told the Kisutu Resident Magistrate Court, Dar es Salaam yesterday that investigation of that case is complete.
