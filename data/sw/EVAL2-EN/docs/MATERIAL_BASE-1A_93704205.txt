Babati has decided to buy taxes of buildings.

Babati has said the council of councilors of Bahati district council in Manyara province, has arranged strategies to increase the income by collecting taxes for buildings.

That council has begun the implementation of the strategy in six wards of Magoza village, Ballidum and Nuzulack Dausen and Darcoboa, because it is the areas that are facing many people to build residential and business houses.

The chairperson of the council of that district Nicodemus Thom said that yesterday at a session of the councilors' council.

He said that in order to get the real value of buildings, it must be an increase in the first before starting from tax payment.

He said that work will be made by an increase in charge and the urban planning officer.
