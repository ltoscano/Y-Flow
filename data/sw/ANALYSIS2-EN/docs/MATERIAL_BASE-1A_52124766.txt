<1>	Puma Energy kudhamini Rock City Marathon	Puma Energy to sponsor the Rock City Marathon
<2>	Imeandikwa na Mwandishi Wetu	written by our author
<3>	Imechapishwa: 21 Septemba 2016	published: 21st September 2016
<4>	KAMPUNI ya mafuta ya Puma Energies Tanzania imejitosa kudhamini mashindano ya Rock City Marathon ambayo kilele chake kinatarajiwa kuwa Septemba 25 mwaka huu kwenye Uwanja wa CCM Kirumba jijini Mwanza.	Puma Energies Oil Company Tanzania has come out to sponsor the Rock City Marathon races, whose final race is expected to be on September 25th this year at the CCM grounds, Kirumba in Mwanza.
<5>	Akikabidhi hundi yenye thamani ya Sh milioni 15 kwa msemaji wa Kampuni ya Capital Plus International Mathew Kasonta ambaye kampuni yake kwa kushirikiana na mkoa wa Mwanza ndio wanaandaa mbio hizo, Meneja Mkuu wa Kampuni ya Puma Energies, Phillipe Corsaletti alisema udhamini huo unalenga kuunga mkono juhudi za Serikali ya Awamu Tano katika kuendeleza na kuhamasisha michezo nchini.	handing a check worth 15 million shillings to the spokesperson of the Capital Plus International, Mathew Kasonta, whose company, in partnership with Mwanza province, is organizing the races, the General Manager of Puma Energies Company, Phillipe Corsaletti, said that the sponsorship aims to support the efforts of the fifth term government to bolster and encourage sports in the country.
<6>	“Lakini pia tumeguswa na malengo ya mbio hizi ambazo pamoja na mambo mengine zinalenga kupambana na ujangili pamoja na kutangaza utalii katika katika Kanda ya Ziwa,’’ aliongeza.	"but we have also been touched by the goals of these races which, together with other things, seek to address poaching as well as market tourism in the Lake Region," he added.
