<4>	Shirika la Umeme Tanzania (Tanesco) Mkoa wa Mbeya linawadai wateja wake zikiwamo taasisi za Serikali Sh7.3 bilioni.	the Tanzania Electric Supply Company Limited (TANESCO) in Mbeya Region is owed 7.3 billion shillings from its customers, including government institutions.
<5>	Meneja wa Tanesco mkoani hapa, Francis Maze alisema hayo alipowasilisha taarifa kwa Mkuu wa Mkoa, Amos Makala aliyefanya ziara ya ghafla katika ofisi za shirika hilo juzi jioni.	TANESCO's manager in the region here, Francis Maze, said this when he submitted a statement to the Regional Head, Amos Makala, who made a sudden visit to the agency's offices in the evening.
<6>	“Pamoja na madeni shirika hilo pia linakabiliwa na changamoto ya makandarasi wa ufungaji wa nyaya majumbani kutoza gharama kubwa za kusuka waya kwenye nyumba ikiwamo kupiga mihuri kwa Sh80,000 na kuwafanya wananchi kushindwa kumudu kupata fedha za kulipia huduma ya laini,” alisema.	"in addition to the debts, the organization is also faced with the challenge from contractors to install cables in houses of charging high costs for installing cables in houses and putting seals for 80,000 shillings and making people unable to afford paying for line services," she said.
<7>	Makala aliwaonya watumishi wa Tanesco kuacha kushirikiana na vishoka kutoa huduma ya umeme na kwamba kwa kufanya hivyo ni kukiuka taratibu na kanuni za kazi.	Makala warned TANESCO staff to stop cooperating with brokers to provide electricity services and that, doing so, will violate the rules and regulations of work.
