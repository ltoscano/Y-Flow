
Wapenya interview Arusha four

It has been written by Yasinta Amos, Arusha

Imechapishwa: 15 December 2016

The election committee of the party in Arusha (ARFA) has been put in public the names of the candidates who passed the interview that happened on December 10th this year under the supervision of a member of the Tanzania Football Federation (TFF).

The candidates who were in that interview and wanazowania position in brackets is Omary Walii and Peter Temu (chairman), Zakayo Mjema (the secretary general), Mwarizo Nasorro (assistant general secretary), Omary Nyambuka (the treasurer).
Others are Athumani Mhando, a general meeting Hamiss Issa (TFF), Soud Maohamed (a member of the executive committee ARFA).
Who were after failing to interview is James Rugangila, Fredrick Lyimo, Gerald Munisi, Maiki Warioba and Victor Mwigila.
The secretary of the committee of the election ARFA, Selemani Kilua said some of the candidates hawakukidhi the criteria and the reputation that among the criteria, therefore, it is a certificate of form four and five years experience in the leadership.
"Tuliwatangazia all the candidates that the day of the audition must come with real certificate in the education of form four and not copies kulizingatia and that is the reason he didn't come to the candidate who is the real and the certificate alienguliwa with him so that the elections can be fair," said Kilua.
Previously 13 candidates came out to vie for various positions which is the chairman, the vice chairman, the secretary general, the assistant secretary, the treasurer, member of the general assembly, a member the representative of the club and three members of the executive committee.
The election ARFA has been planned to take place on January 8 next year.
