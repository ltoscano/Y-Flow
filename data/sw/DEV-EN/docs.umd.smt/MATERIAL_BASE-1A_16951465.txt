President RT not to contest the leadership

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: On October 25, 2016

Former president of the Athletics Tanzania (RT) Francis John has said that he does not plan to contest again for the leadership in the elections that will be held on November 27th in Dar es Salaam.

John he led RT for six years from the year 2006 until 2012, and is the former head coach of the national team of iliyoshiriki Athletics Olympic Games 31 that took place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, 2016.
Speaking by phone yesterday that coach who finished his mwanariadha Felix Simbu of five at the Olympic Rio said yesterday on the phone that, because he had already alishaongoza hatarajii vying for a position in the RT.
"Don't have the plan because he had already I nilishaongoza a high position of the athletics leadership in the country, thus nitagombea which position during the presidency is the highest position, sigombei," said Francis.
He wanted the president atakayechaguliwa in that election responsibilities to his members of the executive committee if he wants to work well and to develop the game in the country.
He said many members of the executive committee of the RT after being elected they are not getting involved with the activities of the game and only sitting president and their only secretary wakimuachia wakijiweka on the side.
