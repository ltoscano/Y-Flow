
"Action can be taken against them" child marriages

It has been written with Jacquiline Mrisho, Maelezo- Kilombero

Imechapishwa: 12th October, 2016

The head of Kilombero District, Morogoro Province, James Ihunyo has ordered directors to take disciplinary measures against actors who will be proven to have child marriages in their areas.

Those executives kuchulikiwa required step is of the villages, local and county as a way of putting an end to that problem.
That order has been issued by the head of the district, when he was reading a speech during the commemoration of the international day of the girl child, which was organized by the Plan International Organization and held in the village of Ihenga.
Also, wants the head of the police force and the executive director to ensure that all those wanaowaoa or kuwaoza girls under the age of 18 years wanakamatwa and being taken to court.
