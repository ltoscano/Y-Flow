
IGP ASISITIZA discipline in the police

It has been written by Francisca Emmanuel

Imechapishwa: 12th November 2016

The head of the police force in the country (IGP), Ernest Mangu all urged the police to ensure they are maintaining discipline, love, unity and peace in the country so as to protect the citizens and their property.

IGP Mangu said that after the exercise made between the police and soldiers of the region.
And the police commander of the special unit of Dar es Salaam, Simon Sirro has said the exercise is for the purpose of building stability Rangers together with their duty to remind all the time while at work. "" IGP saw the importance of these exercises where he decided to participate in the us and kutuasa and urge all police Tanzania to ensure that they are maintaining discipline all the time while at work, "" said Sirro.
Insisted that the exercise will continue in order to protect their health because exercise for those police is something linalotakiwa done every day in their lives.
