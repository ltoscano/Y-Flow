
Every Serengeti goal 1 million.

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: September 20, 2016

Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye has commended the national youth team under the age of 17, Serengeti Boys in the home to win the game for the qualification of African Cup against Congo Brazzaville, promising to buy every goal 1 million shillings in the second leg.

Previously, before the game, the minister gave 200,000 shillings for every player the Serengeti as motivation and promising them Serengeti as wataifunga Congo in the game, then every goal atalilipia 500,000 shillings aliitekeleza promise day after that game.
In that game that was played at the National Stadium, Dar es Salaam the day before yesterday, Serengeti emerged with a victory of 3-2 against Congo Brazzaville therefore, walijinyakulia 1.5 million shillings in three goals waliyofunga.
"Niwapongeze Serengeti Boys imewatendea the rights of Tanzania to win the match at home, we have faith that they do well also, in the final game of away," said and added that the government will continue to support them.
Still the team has the journey to play the final match of the away after two weeks, where it as the team scoring itafuzu to participate in the tournament that will be held Madagascar next year.
Nnauye said his ability to Serengeti imejitahidi waliyokutana team because it is good too, and promised to prayed to perform much better in the next match.
