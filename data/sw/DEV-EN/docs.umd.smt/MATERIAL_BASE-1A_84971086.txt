
Simba wamkana Mbuyu Twite

It has been written by Rahel Pallangyo

Imechapishwa: 13th December 2016

President of Simba, Evans Aveva has denied the reports to sign the defender Mbuyu Twite aliyeachwa Yanga.

Mbuyu Twite was left and Yanga this during the transfer window and rumors kuzagaa that the club's rivals, Simba are in the process of signing up the player anayemudu various positions in the field.
However, speaking to this newspaper, the president of the club of Simba, Evans Aveva, denied those news and said that the defender, from the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) has no chance in the squad.
"Twite has no chance in the lineup of Simba now because we have many players aged boys and talented high in his position," said Aveva.
Said anasikia rumors that Mbuyu Twite ameachwa and Yanga and that Simba it needs and said that rumor has no any facts.
He said that the report of their coach Joseph Omog shows that the club should the goalkeeper and ready wamemsajili door goalkeeper Daniel Agyei from Madeama.
Apart from signing up that goalkeeper, Simba has signed also attacking mid-fielder James Kotei but he added that he also need one striker atakayesaidiana and Laudit Mavugo and Ibrahim Ajib.
Twite alitemwa and Yanga after the club to sign him up the mid-fielder Justin Zullu Zesco from Zambia and completing the number of foreign seven players.
Four years ago Twite alisaini the previous contract with Simba, but later aliigeuka that club and register which aliisaidia Yanga various titles to win the Premier League of Tanzania Mainland, the Kagame Cup and enable it to get into the group stage of the of Confederation of African Football (CAF).
