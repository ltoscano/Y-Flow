
League board yaonya coaches

It has been written by Rahel Pallangyo

Imechapishwa: 16 December 2016

All the club managed by the league to participate in Tanzania Football Federation (TFF) zimeagizwa to employ qualified coaches in accordance with the rules.

The report of the chief executive officer of the board of the league, Boniface Wambura makatinu to go to the heads of the Premier League club, the first and the second one inawakumbusha to employ qualified coaches because it has been revealed some zimeajiri or continuing to employ coaches who don't have the reputation.
The report says the rules of the league has identified 72 for all the qualifications of coaches who are supposed to lead the technical bench so to go against the regulations is violating professional guidance in the area.
According to the regulations, the coach of the team that participates in the Premier League is required to have a license from the bridge B of the Confederation of African Football (CAF) and the assistant coach is required to have a license from the bridge C of the CAF.
For the league and the second class league reputation zinalingana coaches as the head coach is required to have a bridge C of CAF and the assistant is required to have a certificate of the level of between coaching.
According to the rules, those coaches who don't have a reputation to coach the team, they will be removed.
The regulations did work at the end of the round one of the Premier League where the coach of Toto Africans, John Tegete in he was removed from the bench due to the reputation.
