
Leaders yanayoingiza areas smuggling ones held responsible

It has been written by Anna Makange, Tanga

Imechapishwa: 09 September 2016

The governor of Tanga, Martin Shigella ordered to government leaders and executives of the villages in yatakayobainika areas for the importation of goods into the country through smuggling.

Gave the order while speaking with some of the workers of Revenue (TRA) when he visited the office of the province of Tanga yesterday.
Said the role of the first by the leaders of the government and the practitioners in the villages from the grassroots, is to oversee the defense and security, therefore, it is their responsibility to put the strategies to fight and to stop acts of crime.
Previously giving a report on the performance to the head of the province, the manager of TRA Tanga province, Swalehe Byarugaba said that in the period of the year 2015 / 16, the authority has succeeded in collecting more than 110 billion shillings .5 equivalent to 86 percent aimed at collecting 128.7.7 billion shillings.
"Along with that success, we have the problems smuggling products where in the financial year of 2015 / 16 ulikamata province products worth shillings 2,331,752,626 and 351,693,036 shillings, which is a tax and", he said.
