
General Brigedi of bees JWTZ died

It has been written by Khatib Suleiman, Zanzibar

Imechapishwa: 16 December 2016

Brigedi commander of the bees of the Defense Force of Tanzania (JWTZ), Brigadier General Cyril Mhaiki, died in the Army hospital, Dar es Salaam, where treated.

In his greetings rambirambi by the head of the defense and security Tanzania, Davis Mwamunyange, the president of Zanzibar and the chairman of the Revolutionary Council, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein, said yesterday that, has received a huge sadness to the news of the death of aliyefikwa and Brigadier General Mhaiki mauti the day before yesterday.
Doctor Shein on his behalf and all citizens in Zanzibar, said they have received the news in shock, the death of the commander active who makes a big contribution in the activities of defense and security and the development of the country.
Moreover, those messages of rambirambi Brigadier General Mhaiki was found explained that has left a big gap, not only to his family, but by Brigedi of bees, JWTZ and the country in general, while insisting that his contribution hautosahaulika.
He asked Mwamunyange kuzifikisha rambirambi those greetings to the family, the officers and the fighters of all forces defense and security.
Together with that, those messages zilimuomba Almighty God give you the heart patient family, relatives and friends of the deceased during this period tough requesting the Almighty God funeral while kumlaza soul in the commander.
