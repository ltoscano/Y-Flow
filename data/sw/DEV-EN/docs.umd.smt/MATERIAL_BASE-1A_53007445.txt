
Breeders wakanusha farmer beat

It has been written by our writer, Chalinze

Imechapishwa: 11 September 2016

Breeders of the village of Kimange Kwaruhombo Division Chalinze Bagamoyo district, wamekanusha beat the farmer, Ally Muya, then kumnyang'anya gobole.

While speaking to journalists, the breeders said that the incident linakuzwa and some people to breeders waonekane is people with violence, something that is not true.
The breeder Athuman Kideshi said what happened is a two'ombe into the farm of the old man and they were wakijaribu replace those'ombe cow to wasifanye on destruction.
"Is a true'ombe entered for two on old Muya unfortunately tulipokuwa tukijaribu replace then yakatokea conflicts, but hatukumnyang'anya his gun or beating her," said Kideshi.
Habonji Kuhanga for said that for several years, have been living well and farmers and even happening wanalimaliza problem, but for this year the situation has been bad, a step that has brought conflicts between them to farmers, during their harvest that resulted from poor shortage of rain.
"Tunachoomba is well kukatengwa the areas between farmers and breeders so as to eliminate these conflicts that have been coming up from time to time to each side ukae his part, I think this will be medicine than it is now," said Kuhanga for.
The chairman of the village of Kimange, dirty Ayubu said that the issue of breeders has been difficult especially due to a small area of the village that and there is no part of the kuchungia livestock.
Dirty said in order to deal with the challenges, wameunda the committee of farmers and breeders where wanaoingiza livestock herders on the farms have been wakipigwa a fine.
