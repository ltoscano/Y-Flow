
Quality Group, Sonalika start linking tractors in the country

It has been written by Hellen Mlacky

Imechapishwa: On October 25, 2016

The company of Quality Group Limited (QGL), in collaboration with the company Sonalika International Tractors Limited have signed the contract to start linking the machines and agricultural equipment including tractors in the country as part of supporting the efforts of President Doctor.
John Magufuli to make Tanzania a manufacturing country.

Speaking after signing the agreement in Dar es Salaam, the marketing manager of QGL, Timoth Shuma said already they have already completed the initial stages of starting well will be built in the factory that in Morogoro Province and its investments will be among the 50 billion shillings and 100 billion.
He said that for now, the cost of buying a tractor is big due to the cost of transporting linapoingia until the country thus watakapoanza incorporate and linking equipment at the factory will reduce the cost and therefore the farmers to do modern agriculture and business.
