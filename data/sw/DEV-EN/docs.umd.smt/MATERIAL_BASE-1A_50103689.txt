
Director Madaba orders inspection chemists

It has been written by Muhidin Amri, Madaba

Imechapishwa: 14 December 2016

The executive director of the District Council of Madaba Songea district, Shafi Mpenda has directed the chief physician of the council, Doctor Mustafa minister to conduct inspections from time to time in the chemists to identify the individuals shops that drugs in the government.

Moreover, wants kuyafungia all the shops that sell yatakayobainika zilizokwisha a period of drug use and to take action against its owners to stop the behavior of those traders who are collaborating with the health department staff of stealing drugs in the government.
Mpenda gave the order while inspecting various cargo of drugs zilizonunuliwa and the department of health, zitakazosambazwa at the clinic and all the stations of health and renovation of the special store room at Madaba Health Center.
According to his, the health department has the legal responsibility to ensure that every the owner of the shop anazingatia drugs laws, rules and regulations that are in place for the right to sell medicine for human consumption, and avoid selling drugs that are required to be in a clinic, health centers and the government hospitals.
He said that the step will help save the lives of the citizens who is a great pillar in the economy, because the work without good health, there is no wealth creation that can take place, something that is dangerous to the economy of the council.
He said, some of the owners of the shops have a dirty game to sell its time of zilizokwisha drug use, something that can contribute to the poverty because citizens buying those drugs zinashindwa kuponya and caused the citizen to use part of the money to buy drugs which could to take in another service.
Head physician of the council, Doctor Mustafa minister said that for now he is proud to see at all health clinic and the region of Madaba there is no shortage of medicine.
Madaba District Council has 14 dispensaries, government and the 10 four religious institutions together with three health centers, between so two Madaba and Ndelenyuma vinamilikiwa by the government and is owned by one of the Catholic Church.
