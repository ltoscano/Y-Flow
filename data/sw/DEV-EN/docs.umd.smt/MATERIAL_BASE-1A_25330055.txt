
TAMWA yapongeza JPM, Doctor Shein

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: 29 September 2016

Tanzania Media Women's Association (TAMWA) kinampongeza President John Magufuli and the president of Zanzibar, Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein for the appointment of the leaders and especially women.

In that took place recently appointed several members of TAMWA got a chance.
TAMWA iliwataja its members and their positions is Mahfoudha Alley Hamid (Ummie) who has been the chairman of the board of directors of the government's Zanzibar, Maryam Hamdan (the chairman of the Council of Arts and the census film and culture Zanzibar), Nasra Mohamed Juma (the deputy director of the Zanzibar Broadcasting Corporation (ZBC) and Rahel Mhando (the administrative secretary of the new district Kigamboni).
The executive director of TAMWA, Eddah Sanga said the institution believes that, in bringing change in the country, the appointment of women in high positions itakayowezesha is an opportunity for the removal of the weaknesses that are leadership so as to the presence of equity and justice to all Tanzanians.
