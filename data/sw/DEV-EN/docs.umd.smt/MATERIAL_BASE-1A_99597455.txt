The schedule of the female circumcision yamkera DC

It has been written by Mugini Jacob, Tarime

Imechapishwa: 17 November 2016

The schedule of the season of circumcision that is expected to take place at the end of this year imevuja in Tarime district Mara province.

District commissioner of Tarime, Glorious Luoga has said that while he was speaking at a special meeting organized by the Forum for Dignity of the Child (CDF) through funding from the Foundation for Civil Society in Tarime the day before yesterday.
Kuoga has urged all stakeholders to participate in the end the problem of female genital mutilation in that district.
"Is a shame to see that the people they provide the schedule of female genital mutilation Tarime.
Every stakeholder police fulfilling his responsibility tutamaliza the problem of female genital mutilation, Tarime "said Kuoga in that meeting, which was attended by the religious leaders, the traditional elders and officials of the government that they were involved in the issues of the protection of the child.
Said he has instructed the police force to be in a state of precaution to prevent acts of female genital mutilation and urged the citizens to give enough cooperation.
"After getting reports that the schedule coming from, we tumewashirikisha session with the traditional elders, but also to ensure nimemuagiza OCD tunazuia female children kukeketwa," said Kuoga.
The executive director of the CDF, Koshuma Mtengeti used that opportunity to introduce the new project which is aimed at strengthening the security of the girl child in three wards of Tarime district which is Turwa, Bomani and Nyamisangura.
The project of three years will be funded by the Foundation for Civil Society, according to the Koshuma.
