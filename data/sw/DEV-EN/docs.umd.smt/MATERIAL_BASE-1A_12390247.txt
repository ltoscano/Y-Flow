
ZFA using local coaches national teams

September 28, 2016

ZanzibarCHAMA of Zanzibar Football Federation (ZFA) National kitawatumia said that teachers kuzifundisha patriotic their national team of Zanzibar.
The statement was made yesterday by the president of the party, Ravia Idarous during the event of the certificates for the license B 26 coaches who have completed the training last month.
He said the national teams zitafundishwa and Zanzibar people themselves who have licenses of the Confederation of African Football (CAF) present.
"We have many teachers qualified to kuzifundisha our team, so we cannot and we need to make use of teachers from outside to come and kuzifundisha" our team, he said.
However, he urged the teachers have already work on their licenses, which wamezipata so as to reach those who walikusudiwa.
"Working well and getting a certificate is one, but that it is the second which mmeipata profession and to targeted" waliokusudiwa, he said.
Moreover, he said they have many teachers with licenses B Zanzibar is not a bit and others have already licenses A for that reason fanyeni work as required.
He said according to the regulations which wameiweka, any teacher who hatafuata regulations will not with her.
