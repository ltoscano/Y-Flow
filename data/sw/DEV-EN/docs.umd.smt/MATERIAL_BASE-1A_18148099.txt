Imechapishwa: 28 September 2016

The leader of the world, Doctor denominations BOHORA Seyyidna Mohamed Burahanuddin, has arrived in the country for the purpose of the new year festival to celebrate the Muslim believers of those denominations.

Leader arrived at the International Airport of Julius Nyerere and kulakiwa and hundreds of believers of those members who live in the country and various leaders of the government and of the Supreme Council of Muslims Tanzania (Bakwata).
In the reception was also the Minister of Defense and National Service, Doctor Hussein Mwinyi, the Sheikh of Dar es Salaam, Alhad Mussa Salum and the secretary general of Bakwata, Suleiman Lolila.
After the reception, the convoy of the leader ulikwenda directly on the mosque BOHORA where the guest alijumuika and various believers for a celebration of the reception MSIKITINI there.
Speaking at the event, Doctor Mwinyi who represented the Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa, said the government values the contribution of the people of the community of BOHORA Tanzania for the way walivyowekeza and they are running their activities in peace and stability and promised that the government will continue to cooperate with them.
And the representative of Bakwata, Shehe Lolila said President John Magufuli is expected to participate at the opening of the festival which will be held recently at the mosque BOHORA.
"BAKWATA is together and you and nawaahidi for all day 10 mtakazofanya this festival us we will and nyinyi hand in hand to ensure all your activities are mlizopanga" effectively, he said.
