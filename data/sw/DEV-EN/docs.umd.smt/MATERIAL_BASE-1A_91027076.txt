
DAWASCO finishing yatakiwa water problem in the year 2020

It has been written by our writer, Coast

Imechapishwa: 10th October, 2016

Heads of districts in Dar es Salaam and Coastal region wamelitaka the water and sewerage Dar es Salaam (Dawasco) to ensure the problems severe water services in the city of Dar es Salaam inaisha by the year 2020.

That was said by the head of the district of Ilala, Sophia Mjema during a tour of the heads of the districts of Dar es Salaam and Coastal in water production machinery that are of Dawasco.
Speaking on behalf of the heads of his fellow district, Mjema said that the tour has helped to give the light activities done by Dawasco and even to see major projects that was done by the government to ensure citizens get the services of water and safe.
"We have seen a great investment that is done by the government through the machinery of Water Ruvu above and Ruvu lower, therefore we as leaders and representatives of the citizens we must requested Dawasco waongeze strength in ensuring the water yanawafikia many citizens so that the water problem iishe in time," said Mjema.
Kinondoni District Commissioner, aliyekuwapo on that tour, Ally Happi called for Dawasco kuunganishia water services to customers on time and also dealing with water thieves wanaokwamisha the efforts of the authority in the water and destroyed water infrastructure.
Chief executive officer of Dawasco, Cyprian Luhemeja explained that the authority continues with the campaign to connect new customers and explained that until now the will is now since the start of the work under the campaign of Mrs Tua buckets on the head.
