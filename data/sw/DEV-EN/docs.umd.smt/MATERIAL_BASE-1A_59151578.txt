
Business people to relocate the Darajani Saateni

It has been written by Basil

Imechapishwa: 21 September 2016

The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar (SMZ) has set aside Saateni area which is in the area of the city of Unguja for use by small business people where all important infrastructure is complete.

That was said by the minister of the country, the office of the president, regional administration and local government, Haji Omar Kheir when he was answering a question from the constituency representative of the Mwera, Mihayo Juma Nhunga small business people who wanted to know why they feel like they are oppressed in doing their work in the areas of Darajani.
Responding to the question, Kheir said there is no oppression of any kind done by the government to business people until now, but the problem is to some of the traders being unable to follow procedures and policies.
He said the government imelitenga Saateni area for the purpose of the small business people to do their work well and transferred Mwanakwerekwe Darajani.
However, he said there are business people wanaokaidi that order and to continue doing business in those areas in the map of the city yametengwa for the purpose of building a modern buildings in the business.
"The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar insists that there is no kind of oppression is being done by small traders Zanzibar, Darajani tumewahamisha Mwanakwerekwe and we've set aside the area in Saateni", he said.
Said Darajani area for now is in the map of business enhancement including the construction of a modern shops.
