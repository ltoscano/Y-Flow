
From in parliament: President Magufuli moving to Dodoma of Honor, the year 2020

Today, September 16, the Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa reading a speech to postpone the fourth meeting for the 11 parliament that lasted for a period of two weeks, amesoma the schedule of the government moving to Dodoma.

"So as to achieve the government's commitment to move to Dodoma, already itakayowezesha schedule has been organized government moving to Dodoma in phases, starting from the month of September, 2016 until the year 2020, without affecting our budget for the year 2016 / 2017 as follows" -

1\.
September 2016 - February 2017.

The prime minister and all ministers, all the permanent secretaries, deputy ministers all the permanent secretaries they will move to Dodoma in the first phase.
Moreover, every ministry will be required to transfer the servants of the department one or two, and at the same time to continue to put the procedure of taking the servants of another department to Dodoma;

2\.
March 2017 - August 2017.

This period will give opportunity to the directors of different ministries to put in their budgets of the year 2017 / 2018 costs of continuing to relocate its employees to come to Dodoma.

3\.
September 2017 - February 2018.

In this phase, the ministry will continue with relocating the servants of the departments that are inside the ministry.

4\.
March 2018 - August 2018.

During this period, the ministries are needed to continue to relocate its workers.

5\.
September 2018 - February 2020.

During this period, the ministries are needed to continue to relocate its workers.

\ 6.
March 2020 - June 2020.

During this period, the office of the president led by the President of the United Republic of Tanzania and the Vice President of the United Republic of Tanzania, will be relocated to Dodoma.

The Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa used this time to the ministry research all systems handling electronic records, instead of shifting and mafaili from Dar es Salaam to Dodoma, we want to be full of electronic Dodoma, insisted the Prime Minister.
