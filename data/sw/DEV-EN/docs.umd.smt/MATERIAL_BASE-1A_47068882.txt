Ivo opens a soccer station

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: 24th November 2016

Soccer center of advanced for young people under the age of five to 18 is expected to be opened on November 26 in Mwanang Kitunda'ati, Ilala Dar es Salaam.

Speaking with this newspaper the owner of the station, the former goalkeeper of Simba and Yanga teams, Ivo Mapunda said he has seen the importance of soccer to teach children the basis of small-scale still.
"The station called the Ivo Mapunda Sports Center, the aim is to teach children the basis of soccer small a group from the national team in order to build a strong later," said Ivo.
Also Ivo has cooperate with all the parents Kitunda to allow and bringing their children to learn soccer.
Procedures of joining the center are available on the launch in collaboration with the local government.
Ivo Mapunda is among the players of Tanzania who wamecheza soccer in great success.
