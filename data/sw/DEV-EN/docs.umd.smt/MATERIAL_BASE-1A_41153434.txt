
TBS Kutenga sports budget

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: 03 December 2016

Tanzania Bureau of Standards (TBS), has said it will continue to allocate adequate budget to ensure gets and representation in sports organizations, involving the public and private institutions (SHIMMUTA), so as well as building relations with other directors, lipenyeze education about the quality of products and services, to the participants of the games.

According to the marketing officer, TBS, Rhoda Mayugu, they used the games which began on November 22, this year, educating the sportsmen in different ways, as well as other citizens and the areas where the games is taking place, in the province of Tanga.
Mayungu said due to the fact that the games hushirikisha many people from different organizations and institutions of the public and private, they are well and were in the process of preparing to early in the budget, so that the participants and to use the opportunities kuujuza public various issues about its quality standards.
Mayugu said in the games of that kind, TBS hulenga more linaloshiriki, while a group of youths ikiamini that has people with unaooweza influence to help the education to others in their areas of work, at home and they elsewhere easily.
The competition yalizinduliwa of honor on November 24th, this year and the Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye, alishirikisha public agencies and private taasiis budget to set aside for the purpose of developing the competitions, thus raising the games.
Nango organizations and institutions who participated in the competition and promised to make sure employees of public agencies wanayashiriki every year.
