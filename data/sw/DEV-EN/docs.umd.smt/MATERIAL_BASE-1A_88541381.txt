People of watakiwa markets in soccer

It has been written by Rahel Pallangyo

Imechapishwa: 03 September 2016

Soccer Forum has said the issue of markets in football is very important and inclusion on supposed because it has been a big soccer business in the world that can create a lot of money.

Speaking at the forum Soccer which is happening at the National Stadium, the chairman of the platform's Henry Tandau, said if Tanzania should move forward in soccer arena must then kupatikane people with experience in the marketing department.
"In the whole world soccer is business, so as to get the profit is a must to do a good job markets people to look for markets," said Tandau.
Tandau gave an example billionaire Roman Abromovich during akinunua Chelsea took her Peter Kenyon in the department of marketing, a situation that until today the team to do well in business.
"Sheikh Mansour took her from Bruno Soriano Barcelona and if you look at it, now they are doing well and they can register any player in the world," said Tandau.
The forum litahitimishwa today linajadili different issues about the development of soccer in the country.
