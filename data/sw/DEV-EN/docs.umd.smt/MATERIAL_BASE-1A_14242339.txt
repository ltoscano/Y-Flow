
The biggest ship yatinga Bandari Dar

It has been written by our writer

Imechapishwa: 30 September 2016

Tanzania Port Authority (TPA) has said that now is open doors to the largest ship permission to dock at the port of Dar es Salaam, because it allows the ship of modern technology.

Apart from the technology that was used to make the ship has improved, the skills of his coaches after taking in South African countries, Denmark and France to get training on how to bring the type of ship at the port.
The executive director of the authority, Deusdedit Kakoko said that after the coaches of the authorities managed to bring in the biggest ship in the history of the Port of Dar es Salaam 261 meters in length.
That ship called MV Hammonia Grenada which has the ability to carry the makasha 4,256, imetia dock at the port of Dar es Salaam and reducing makasha 483 and loading 656 makasha before heading to Thailand.
Kakoko said the port of Dar es Salaam has the ability to serve the size of ships that its four at the dock, which is eight gate number nine, 10, 11 and which have a thorough 11 meters in length.
He said the arrival of the ship is an enough evidence that the port has the ability to receive a big ship of that kind, which were up only port of Mombasa.
Previously, TPA ilishawahi to bring in another big ships with a length of 250 meters and later ikaingiza another ship 255 meters in length.
According to the Captain Mwingamno, with a length of 261 meters huishia often port of Mombasa due to the UDOGO entrance but the arrival of this ship umefungua officially the doors in business.
