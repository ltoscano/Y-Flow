
A young 27 years in prison for theft

It has been written by Kareny Masasy, Maswa

Imechapishwa: 13th September 2016

Maswa District Court has sentenced a resident of Simiyu province along the new arena in Maswa, Jilala Masele (18) to go to prison for 27 years after being found guilty of breaking into houses at night to steal.

Reading that while at the end of the week, the Magistrate Court, Tumain Marwa said that was pleased with the evidence that was given in front of the court and the prosecution that was led by the inspector of police, Nassib Swedy and therefore anamtia offense, the defendant that sentence in prison.
"Imeridhishwa without doubt this court to stop and the evidence that was given by witnesses for the prosecution who umethibitisha that, the defendant has committed an all these crimes breaking into houses and stealing, therefore inamtia guilty to go to prison for 27 years so it should be a lesson to other people who want to make a mistake like that," has said.
Previously it was alleged in court and Mwendesha Public Prosecution, Swedy that accused alitenda that offense 11 August this year around 9: 00 at night at the new arena in Maswa and contrary to section (1), 256 and 294 (1) (2) of the law of the rules of the shape of a 16 changes in 2002.
He explained to the court that just after breaking the house Leonard John came in and steal shillings 98,000 and different clothes worth 300,000 shillings.
