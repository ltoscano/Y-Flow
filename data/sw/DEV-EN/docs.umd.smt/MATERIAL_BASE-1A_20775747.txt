Kally ajifariji Majimaji

It has been written by Grace Mkojera

Imechapishwa: 14 October 2016

The coach of Majimaji Kalimangonga "Kally" Ongalla said lost its game against Kagera Sugar at home was bad luck and that the procedures watarekebisha their mistakes.

In that game that was played at the Majimaji Songea Stadium, the hosts walikula 1-0.
Speaking with this newspaper yesterday Ongalla said his team ilitawala the ball by a huge percentage in that game but unfortunately walijisahau and scored that goal.
"If you check the whole game, we tulicheza well sema tulishindwa using tulizopata position, our colleagues in the closing kutufunga" and got a chance, he said.
Ongalla said they are continuing to correct the mistakes and procedures penye yatabadilika things to the fans patience is needed because of the team.
Majimaji this season things have been bad, after losing eight games out of nine iliyocheza.
Since Ongalla join and the team lost two games and winning one, while akijipa yatabadilika things heart that is not a long time.
He said anything is possible because there are many games is ahead and that they are continuing with the preparation solidification in order to get good results.
That team inayoshika last expects to play next game against Toto African that will be played at the CCM Stadium, Kirumba Mwanza the day after tomorrow.
