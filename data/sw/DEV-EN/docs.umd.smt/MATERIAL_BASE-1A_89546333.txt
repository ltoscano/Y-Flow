Morogoro province police are holding a citizen of China, Guo you Yuan (48), a resident of in Ifakara Kibaoni Kilombero district in the province of Morogoro, after being found with 10 machines to conduct a lottery, zilizosambazwa in that town, alleging are substandard.

The Police Commander of the province of Morogoro, Ulrich Matei, confirmed yesterday arrested for the citizens of China after carrying out a search and experts of the authority of a lottery and found her with 10 machines zilizofungwa Ifakara town.
Therefore, he said the suspect was arrested at around 4 o'clock in the morning on December 13 this year, following a search ulioendeshwa and experts draw from the headquarters in Dar es Salaam in collaboration with other.
According to the commander of Matei, these experts after assessing the machines, they discovered that were not and the quality that is required and there are also at a low level.
