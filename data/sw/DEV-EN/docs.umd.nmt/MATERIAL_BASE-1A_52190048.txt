
12 to beat Uganda soccer of ufukweni.

It was written by Grace Mkojera.

It has been published: 30 November 2016.

The coach of the national soccer team of Ufukweni, John Mwansansu has announced the 12 players squad that will enter the camp to prepare for international friendly game against Uganda which will be played on December 9th this year.

The Manager of ufukweni soccer, Deo Lucas announced that squad yesterday on behalf of Mwansansu who is not there as a result of urgency.
He mentioned the players to be Ally Rabby, Rajab Ghaba, Khalifa Mgaya, Mwalimu Akida, Mohamed Rajabu, Juma Ibrahim, Kashiru Salum, Talib Ally, Ahmad Ally, Kassim Kilango, Mohamed Rajab and Samwel John.
"The squad will come in camp on December first this year to prepare for that friendly game as part of testing the power," he said.
He said the goalkeeper Juma Kaseja was among them but he was married from problems, but later there is a possibility of an increase in others to increase the strength.
