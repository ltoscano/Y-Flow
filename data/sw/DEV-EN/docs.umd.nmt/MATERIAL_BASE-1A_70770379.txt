
The workers are tested for breast cancer.

It was written by our writer Wetu.

It has been published: 24 October 2016.

Employees of TBL Group Company and their families in its industries in the country, at the end of the week commemorating the breast cancer month.

They were tested for breast cancer and being given education to feed the uu of this disease which has been increasing fast in the country.
The manager of the Rasilimaliwatu of that company, Jonathan Koshuma said the commemorations have happened in order to implement the company's plan to strengthen the health of workers and their families known as health Kwanza.
"The response of all our employees to participate in this program is good as many that have come to be measured and given advice on how to avoid breast cancer and to know the symptoms of this disease earlier when you come out, so as to have been tested and get treatment," he said.
The coordinator of the first health program of TBL Group, Julieth Mgani said apart from workers being given education and advice on various diseases, they also get an opportunity to participate in training together that involves their families and they have been getting education of the importance of good nutrition.
One of the doctors who run cancer testing to those workers in Dar es Salaam city, Doctor Walter Kweka said Tanzania is one of the countries with a huge increase in cancer patients especially in recent years, especially breast cancer that affects many women.
