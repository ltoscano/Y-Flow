District Commissioner of Kilombero in Morogoro province, James Ihunyo has ordered directors to take disciplinary action against directors who will be proven to have early marriages in their areas.

The executives needed to be dealt with the action is of the villages, streets and county as a way to eradicate the problem.
That order has been issued by the district commissioner, when he was reading a speech during the commemoration of the World Child Day that was organized by Plan International organization and held in Ihenga village.
Also, he has wished the head of the police force and the executive director to ensure that all those who are marrying or kuwaoza girls under the age of 18 are being arrested and kufikishwa in court.
