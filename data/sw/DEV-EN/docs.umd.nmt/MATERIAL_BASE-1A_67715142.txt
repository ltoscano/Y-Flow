
Every goal Serengeti million 1 / - -

It was written by our writer Wetu.

It has been published: 20 September 2016.

The Minister of Information, Culture, Arts and Sports, Nape Nnauye has congratulated the national team of youth under the age of 17, Serengeti Boys by winning the home game to qualify for the African Cup Cup against Congo Brazzaville, promising to buy every 1 million shillings in the repeat game.

Previously, before the game, the minister gave 200,000 shillings to every player of Serengeti as the motivation and promised Serengeti if they will score Congo in that game, then every goal will also be 500,000 shillings that he will implement the day before yesterday after the game.
In that game that was played at the National Stadium, Dar es Salaam the day before yesterday, Serengeti emerged with a win of 3-2 goals against Congo Brazzaville thus, they won a 15 million shillings in three goals that they scored.
"Niwapongeze Serengeti Boys has been doing justice of Tanzania by winning a home match, we have faith in them they will do well, in the final game away," he said and added that the government will continue to support them.
Still the team has a trip to play the last game away after two weeks, where if it will succeed against the team will qualify to participate in the tournament that will take place in Madagascar next year.
Nnauye said Serengeti has struggled with his ability because the team that they met is good too, and promised to pray in order to do better in the next game.
