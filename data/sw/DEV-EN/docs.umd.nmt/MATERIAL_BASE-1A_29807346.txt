
TBS will be in control of poor products.

It was written by the editor editor.

It has been published: 30 September 2016.

The Provincial Commissioner of Arusha, Mrisho Gambo has wanted the Tanzania Bureau of Standards (TBS) and other stakeholders to cooperate with the state and property to ensure they are controlling the poor and poor products that are imported and made in the country.

In the speech read on his behalf by the head of Ngorongoro District, Rashid Mtaka at the fourth meeting of the stakeholders of controlling standards that is taking place here, Gambo said the country is facing a huge disaster of spread in poor products and poor without standards and significantly contributes poverty to its citizens.
He said that it's supposed to be controlled by all the strength to stakeholders of those sectors together with the citizens by exposing those involved, and TBS and other stakeholders, they should cooperate with the state and property to deal with the situation so that Tanzania should not be dampo of non-standards.
In addition to called TBS and other stakeholders, Gambo urged Tanzania to boycott the poor and poor products, because that is the only way to kill the market of those products.
