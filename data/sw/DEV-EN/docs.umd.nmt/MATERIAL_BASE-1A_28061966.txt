The deputy minister of health, community development, gender, elderly and children, Doctor Hamis Kigwangalla said the government is required to prepare to deal with the challenges in the health sector resulting from the increase of people where every year more than one million children are born.

Doctor Kigwangalla said that yesterday in the debate of the system of provision of health services in the country that was organized by the Swiss Embassy in the country as part of the commemoration of 50 years of cooperation between that government and Tanzania.
"There is a large number of people increasing every year where Tanzania every year when we receive more than one million children so the next 50 years in Tanzania we will have more than 100 million people and this is a very big challenge health in the health sector and our economy," said the Deputy Minister and added: "We must prepare for the issues of the environment and how to get food, employment and how to serve our people in health and sectors of the sector." The other, for that every day we see challenges are increasing in the health sector and the budget is increasing but we still see no improvements due to various challenges. "
