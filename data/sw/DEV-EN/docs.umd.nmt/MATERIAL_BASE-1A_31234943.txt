It was written by our writer, Kahama Kahama.

It has been published: 30 September 2016.

The stakeholders and various businessmen in the district, Shinyanga province, have requested the Tanzania Air Company Limited (ATCL) that will have planned for the new plane schedule to inform Kahama district.

They have requested the exclusion from that district to be the hub of business that joins the East African Community.
The request was issued one day after President John Magufuli, launching two types of Bombardier Dash Q400 which are manufactured in Canada that will make the journey in the country and outside the country.
Those businessmen, they said that the existence of a trip at least one of the aircraft in the city will help in promoting business and to local business people and outside they will use the opportunity to do their activities in time.
One of those businessmen, candy Mtembei, who owns the KenCompany Company Limited that deals with the cut of air tickets of the Lake region, asked the government to give priority to the district which is growing fast.
"The city of Kahama is in the middle of the five Great Lakes countries and has also been surrounded by great investors especially those of the minerals and agriculture of tobacco.
There is a trip at least one of the week, to please get them to do their business at a time, "said Mtembei.
He said right now, they are travelling a distance of more than 400 kilometers from Kahama to Mwanza to look for that service and others travel from Ngara, Rusumo to Kagera distance of 600 kilometers.
