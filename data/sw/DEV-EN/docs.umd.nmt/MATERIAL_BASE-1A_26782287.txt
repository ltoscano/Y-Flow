Today is the end of taking and returning forms for those who want to vie for various positions of leadership in the general elections of the Olympic Committee (TOC) which will take place on December 10th Dodoma.

According to the press that was married yesterday by the secretary general of TOC, Filbert Bayi who is attending the general meeting of the Olympic Committee (Anoca), today at 10 o'clock in the evening is the end of returning the form.

Bayi has already taken and returning the form to defend his position.
