
IGP insists on the discipline to the police officers.

It has been written by the penalty in Emmanuel.

It has been published: 12 November 2016.

The head of police force in the country (IGP), Ernest Mangu urged all soldiers to ensure they are maintaining discipline, love, unity and peace in the country in order to protect the citizens and their properties.

IGP Mangu said that after the exercises that were done between the police head and the police officer of that region.
And the police commander of the special unit of Dar es Salaam, Simon Sirro has said that exercise is for empowering the soldiers together with reminding their responsibility when they are not at work. "IGP saw the importance of this practice where he decided to participate with us and urged all the soldiers in Tanzania to ensure that they are maintaining discipline all the time when they are not at work," said Sirro.
He insisted that the exercise will be substantial in order to protect their health because the practice for those policemen is an issue that is being done every day in their lives.
