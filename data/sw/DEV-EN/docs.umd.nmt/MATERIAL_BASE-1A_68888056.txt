Analysts or statisticians involved in various research which are coordinated by the National Bureau of Statistics (NBS) have been urged to have graduated from the training of official statistics collection at least a certificate.

The report given by the executive director of the National Bureau of Statistics, Doctor Albina Chuwa said the requirements are according to the statistics law with.
9 of the year 2015 that pays the authority to coordinate and manage the collection of the official data in the country.
It said that the use of analysts who have been through the training of data collection at least level of the certificate will help improve the availability of official data in the country.
In addition, the report stated that the training of official statistics collection is being given by the East African Statistics College, "Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC) which is in Changanyikeni in Dar es Salaam city.
The National Bureau of Statistics has the responsibility of collecting, analysis and spreading official data in the country according to the statistics law of the year 2015.
