
TAMWA yapongeza JPM, Doctor Shein Shein.

It was written by our writer Wetu.

It has been published: 29 September 2016.

The Tanzania Women Women Association (TAMWA) congratulate President John Magufuli and the president of Zanzibar Doctor Ali Mohamed Shein for the appointment of leaders and especially women.

In the appointment that took place recently several members of the Tamgot a chance.
TAMWA mentioned its members and their positions are Mahfoudha, Hamid (Ummie) who has been the chairman of the board of directors of the Tanzania newspaper newspapers, Maryam Hamdan (the chairperson of the Council of Arts and Culture of Zanzibar), Nasra Mohamed Juma (deputy director of the Zanzibar Broadcasting Corporation (ZBC) and Rahel Mhando (administrative secretary of the new district of Kigamboni).
The executive director of Tamdah, Eddah Sanga said that the institution believes that in bringing development change in the country, the selection of women in the above positions is an opportunity that will enable the removal of limitations that are in leadership so as to be equality and justice to all Tanzanians.
