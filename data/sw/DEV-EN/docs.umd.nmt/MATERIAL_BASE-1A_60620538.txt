
Quality Group, Sonalika starting to unite tractors in the country in the country.

It has been written by Hellen Mlacky.

It has been published: 25 October 2016.

The Quality Group Limited company (QGL) in collaboration with the Sonalika International Tractors limited company have signed a contract of starting with machines and agricultural equipment in the country including tractors as part of supporting the efforts of President Doctor.
John Magufuli of making Tanzania to be an industrialized country.

Speaking after signing the contract in Dar es Salaam city, the Marketing Manager of QGL, Timoth Shuma said they have already completed the initial stages of starting that factory which will be built in Morogoro province and its investment will be between 50 billion shillings and 100 billion shillings.
He said at the moment, the cost of buying tractor is huge due to the cost of transportation until it comes in the country, therefore when they start to bring in the equipment and connect with the factory will reduce the cost and therefore farmers do modern farming and business farming.
