Police, Chwaka zang'ara in Zanzibar league in Zanzibar.

It was written by Mwajuma Juma, Zanzibar Zanzibar.

It has been published: 13 October 2016.

The police soccer teams, Chwaka Stars and Black Sailor has won its premier league matches played at different stadiums.

The police played at the Amaan Stadium to play with Malindi where it won 1-0.
The winners goal was scored by Mohammed Othman Mmanga in the seventh minute.
Another match that was played at the stadium was between Black Sailor that closed down Miembeni 1-0.
The goal of winners scored by Mustafa Vuai the 55 minute.
At Fuoni Stadium, Chwaka Stars team has won 1-0 against Chuoni the scorer being Bakar Thani in the 33 minute.
Those results are making Chwaka iongoze stand with 7 points equivalent to Black Sailor that is the second position for a different goals.
Mundu is with six points in the fourth position in the standings, the police are following with five points, JKU holds a sixth position with four points.
That league will continue tomorrow by played three games where the nation Jang'ombe will battle with KVZ, Kipanga will play with the training and at Fuoni Stadium, KMKM will fight with Kilimani City.
