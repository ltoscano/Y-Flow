Players who don't have a license will not be allowed to play in the first league from the fifth round that begins today.

Speaking to journalists yesterday, information officer of Tanzania Football Federation (TFF), Alfred Lucas said the board of the Tanzania Football Federation has ordered commissioners and managers of stations to ensure the player who has no license does not chezi.
"According to the legislation of 14 (16)," all players will be identified by using their licenses to be provided and approval by TFF.
Any player who will not have a license will not be allowed to play in the respective game, "said Lucas.
Lucas said the federation gave an emergency period to use temporary licenses when the licenses of the season are organized, therefore, the licenses are ready but the leaders of the team have not been going to take them to TFF.
