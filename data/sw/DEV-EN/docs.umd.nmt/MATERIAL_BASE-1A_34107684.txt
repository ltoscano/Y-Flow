
Pepsi providers 20 million / - contributing desks to the desks.

It has been written by Kareny Masasy, Shinyanga.

It has been published: 31 August 2016.

The Pepsi cold drinks company (SBC Tanzania Limited) has given 20 million shillings to contribute desks to the primary schools of Shinyanga municipality.

While handing over the money yesterday to his worship the mayor of that municipality, Gullam Mukadam, who is also the chairman of Alat the National, the Regional Manager of the Lake Region, Nicklass Coetzer has said that the company has decided to give that contribution as their responsibility to help social projects that are being implemented here in the country.
Coetzer said, the donation they gave is in support and implementing the objectives of President Magufuli that he wanted of Tanzania in the country to cooperate and make sure all students in the country are learning seated in the desks.
He said they have decided to give that amount of money for the purpose of buying desks, that will be distributed to the schools that are in the municipality, as a step to strengthen the profession in the municipality.
His Excellency the Mayor of Shinyanga Municipality, Gulam Mukadam thanked the leadership of that company, due to the contribution they gave which will help, in reducing the gap of desks shortage in primary schools that are in the municipality.
The Provincial Commissioner of Shinyanga, Josephine Matiro, despite thanking the company, said the contribution that was given will reduce a huge amount of desks in primary schools that are in the municipality.
