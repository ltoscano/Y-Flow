
Upelelezi the case of "terrorism" student UDOM is still yet.

It was written by Sifa Lubasi, Dodoma, Dodoma.

It has been published: 17 December 2016.

The case of terrorism offenses facing the student of the second year of Dodoma University (Udom) Faculty of Health, Azan Abubakar (23) has postponed until December 29 this year when it will be mentioned again due to its investigation incomplete.

The accused was brought back to court for the first time on November 17th, this year after being arrested in Nzega province of Tabora after the discovery of joining the terrorism network of the group of "Al Shabaab" leye his stronghold in Somalia.
He was arrested by the police force due to the reports of the intelijensia in the issues of terrorism.
The accused, the day before yesterday was brought back before the magistrate, Mwajuma Lukindo of the court of Dodoma district and the case was postponed until December 29 this year when it will be mentioned again, while prosecution through the government lawyer Kezilafouled Miyango saying the investigation is not yet completed.
Abubakar is accused of the mistake of sponsoring terrorism under section 13 of the criminal law of control terrorism number 21 of the year 2002.
It was claimed that on May 15, this year, the accaccused used money Mohammed Ibrahim through M-Pesa amounting to 265,000 shillings in number + 254 708104109 for doing terrorism in the country.
