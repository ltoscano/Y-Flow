Yanga striker, Simon Msuva has been elected as the best player of the Tanzania Mainland Premier League in October.

Speaking to journalists in Dar es Salaam yesterday, information officer of Tanzania Football Federation (TFF), Alfred Lucas said Msuva has defeated the players Shiza Kichuya and Muzamir Yassin of Simba.
"Msuva had two close competitors in the race of October from Simba, which is Shiza Kichuya and Muzamir Yassin and defeating them," said Lucas.
In October which had six rounds, Msuva helped his team to get 14 points after scoring four goals, and gave the last five pass.
By winning the best player of the month, Msuva will be awarded a sum of one million shillings from the sponsors of that league of Vodacom Tanzania.
Last season Msuva emerged the best player of the Premier League and is among the players who were called on the national team squad that will play with Zimbabwe on November 13.
