
The organization involves school to strengthen the upanzi of trees with KU the celebrations of the environment in the world being organized today, the Forest Department (KFS) has launched plans to strengthen the efforts of the anzi trees so as to reach ten percent of the areas with the forests in the country.
This is through the program of upanzi trees in the school.
The project was launched by the government in order to give the inspiration to participate in the planting exercise.
"Some schools have been selected to start that project including primary schools," said Mister Peter Kirigua of KFS.
Mister Kirigua added that his organization has collaborated with the government of the Machakos's government to start a project of upanzi trees which will benefit the projects of unyer water farms. "The government of Machakos will recognize the areas where the environment have been destroyed in order to climb trees there," said Mister Kirigua.
He urged other governors to start the conservation committee of forests in order to protect and conserve forest based on the law of the forests of the year 2014.
