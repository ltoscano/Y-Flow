The graduates of two years of scout in Mbeya district have been urged to use two years training that they received by protecting peace and stability of our country as well as self-laws in crime groups.

"" Training you are forced to help the community and use the methods that you get in order to bring their progress themselves, "said the councilor of Songwe Ward, has been Njalambaha.

Njalambaha also urged those graduates to put God in their performance in the community so as to build for the trust of the Almighty God and to be forever every one with faith you're going to right.

"" Mtangulizeni God at your work in front of the community, when you ask God you will have faith, with faith that you will go right, when we go back to our areas we help to maintain peace and harmony, "said Njalambaha.

Moreover, Njalambaha urged those graduates to avoid discrimination involving groups of political ideology or religious ideology and that they are required to put patriotism and help the development of our country without discrimination.

On his side, the acting head of prison of Songwe prison, Salehe Hassan Saleh urged parents to cooperate with youths joining the scout training because those lessons and empower them and understanding them giving the courage and ukakamavu.

And the instructor of the training Deodatus Mazengo said that the training involve 47 and got a two-year training that have happened in the villages that are in the village of songwe in Mbeya district.
