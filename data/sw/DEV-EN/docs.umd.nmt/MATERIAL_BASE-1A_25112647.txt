
A new company to prepare Ngorongoro Marathon.

It was written by our writer Wetu.

It has been published: 21 November 2016.

Ngorongoro race will take place next year on April 22 under the new organizer, Meta Sports Promotions Company has been said.

Speaking on the phone from Karatu in Arusha province, the director of the company Meta Petro said that from next year the request are under the company of sports.
Meta who is the former international player of Tanzania said that the race are now under the athletics people, therefore, will have great changes.
He said that for now, they have started to communicate with different companies in order to ensure that the race are made by enough sponsors and winners to get a big gift.
Petro said that the people that played the game are killing his pain, so they are not ready to see the game if you dropped or the participants to be violated a gift.
He urged various stakeholders of the athletics game together with companies to come out in large numbers to have been given the chance to sponsor the race, which are expected to have the excitement of its kind.
