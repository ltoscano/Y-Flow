Semi-finals of the premier league of youths under 20 years is expected to kick off the day after tomorrow where Simba will battle with Stand United at the Azam Complex stadium Chamazi.

That league involves the youth teams of all teams participating in the Tanzania Mainland Premier League.
The game has been scheduled to do 10 o'clock in the evening being preceded by the Azam against Mtibwa Sugar.
Those teams have been opposed to the semi-finals after taking part of the groups where it had planned two.
The group A teams that qualify for that step is Simba of Dar es Salaam and Mtibwa Sugar and Morogoro and a group B which was using Kagera station that were qualified is Azam and Stand United.
Information officer of Tanzania Football Federation (TFF), Alfred Lucas said yesterday that the finals of the competition at the end of this week.
That league was officially launched on November 15 this year being sponsored by DTB Bank where every club was given 10 million shillings and communication directly and television station of Azam.
In the center of Dar es Salaam there were JKT Ruvu teams, Simba, Ruvu Shooting, Ndanda, Majimaji of Songea, Mtibwa, Mbeya City and Prisons.
