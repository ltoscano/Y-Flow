Novemba 10, 2013, 2013.

The police force in Geita province has been successful to arrest two people with 243 cards of different people in Chato district, used to give money to the NMB Bank's ATM against the law.
The police commander in Geita province, Leonard Paulo, told journalists in his office, that the incident happened the day before yesterday at 7 o'clock, at night in Kitela village in Chato district.
He mentioned the suspects in Gidion Matiku (22) and Seleman Hassan (30) a resident of Chato at all of the traders of the cereals shop together with construction equipment.
"We are holding these people going to investigate more on what they own all the ATM cards that were given by the NMB Bank, they are working on why they have names of different customers, who are the rightful owners who are being recognized by this bank and not them," said the commander.
He said that the action of owning those bank cards, is against the law and that the army is still ongoing with more investigations where the time is complete the suspects will be arraigned in court immediately.
In another step, the army is holding two people residents of the Runzewe village in Chato district, accused of killing him to death a resident of that village for that is supposedly is a fight for a farm.
The police commander in Geita province, Leonard Paulo, mentioned the suspects that are Bw.Marco Mathias (46) and Bw.Boniphace Shija (33) all the residents of that village and that the incident happened the day before yesterday.
