It has been published: 07 September 2016.

The laws of pension payment in the Social Security Funds have been notified and now every public servant who will want to transfer its benefits to another fund or transfer jobs can do so.

The minister in the office of the prime minister, policy, parliament, labor, employment, youth and with disability, Jenista Mgahama said in parliament yesterday to be a public servant who will want to transfer his benefits is allowed according to the law and even if he will decide to transfer jobs.
He said that retired one who will want to continue contributing to the pension fund can go to speak with the fund and continue to get services while out of employment without disturbance.
Mhagama was responding to the question of the member of parliament of Korogwe town, Mary Chatanda (CCM) who asked the government has a strategy to remove the retired problems because the PSPF fund has been delaying the retired benefits.
In his reply, the Deputy Minister of Finance, Ashantu Kijaji said in dealing with the problems of paying employees benefits, the government in the period of January to August this year, has been accomplished to pay 511.86 billion shillings which is the arrears of the employer contributions.
He said in that period, the government has paid a total of 83.25 billion shillings as a payment of debt before the year 1999 together with paying arrears of claims of retirees and other benefits offered by the fund.
KIJAJU said in the period of January, this year to July 2016, the PSPF fund has paid the pension benefits of the month by 117.33 shillings and now the fund is going to pay the arrears of the bonuses for new retirees and other benefits.
