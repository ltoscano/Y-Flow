
From parliament: President Magufuli moving to Dodoma officially in the year 2020.

Today September 16, Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa reading a speech to postpone the fourth meeting for the 11 parliament that lasted for a period of two weeks, he studied the government's schedule to move to Dodoma.

"In order to make the government's commitment to Dodoma, it has already prepared the schedule that will enable the government moving to Dodoma in phases, from the month of September 2016 to 2020, without affecting our budget of the year 2016 / 2017 as follows" -

1 - the one.
September 2016 - February 2017.

The Prime Minister and all the ministers, all the chief secretaries, the deputy secretaries all will move to Dodoma in the First phase.
In addition, every ministry will be required to transfer the servants of one department or two, and that time to continue to put the procedure of taking the servants of another department moving to Dodoma,

2.
March 2017 - August 2017.

This period will give them an opportunity to the directors of different ministries to put in their budgets of the year 2017 / 2018 costs of continuing to transfer its staff to come to Dodoma.

3.
September 2017 - February 2018.

In this phase, the ministries will continue with relocating the servants of the departments that are inside the ministry.

4.
March 2018 - August 2018.

In this period, the ministries are supposed to continue to relocate his staff.

5.
September 2018 - February 2020.

In this period, the ministries are supposed to continue to relocate his staff.

6.
March 2020 - June 2020.

In this period, the office of the President led by Honorable President of the United Republic of Tanzania and the Vice President of the United Republic of Tanzania, will be moving to Dodoma.

The Prime Minister Kassim Majaliwa used this time to advise all the ministry to establish a system of electronic records instead of relocating and fafain from Dar es Salaam to come to Dodoma, we want Dodoma to be electronic, emphasized the prime minister.
