The leader of the BOHORA arrives in the country.

It was written by our writer Wetu.

It has been published: 28 September 2016.

The chief leader of the BOHORA world, Doctor Seyyidand Mohamed Bancers anuddin, has arrived in the country for leading a concert to celebrate the new Muslim year to the believers of those denominations.

The leader arrived at the Julius Nyerere International Airport and with hundreds of believers of those denominations living in the country and various leaders of the government and the Supreme Council of Muslims Tanzania (Bakwata).
In the reception, there was also the Minister of Defense and Armed Forces, Doctor Hussein Mwinyi, Shehe of Dar es Salaam Province, Alhad Mussa Salum and the secretary general of Bakwata, Suleiman Lolila.
After the reception, the convoy of that leader was gone directly at the BOHORA Mosque where the guest had attended various believers for the reception ceremony of the reception.
Speaking at that event, Doctor Mwinyi who represented the prime minister, Kassim Majaliwa said the government values the contribution of the people of BOHORA Tanzania in the way they invest and they are running their activities in peace and tranquility and promised that the government will continue to cooperate with them.
And the representative of Bakwata, Shehe Lolila, said President John Magufuli is expected to participate at the opening of the festival which will be held soon in the BOHORA Mosque.
"BAKWATA is in addition to you and I promise them for all 10 days that you make this festival we will have a cinyi hand in hand to ensure your activities that you are planning are being held in great efficiency," he said.
The leader of the BOHORA Tanzania, has been sacked Adamjee, said the believers of the BOHORA religion in the whole world will celebrate Moharam who is a new Muslim year from October.
