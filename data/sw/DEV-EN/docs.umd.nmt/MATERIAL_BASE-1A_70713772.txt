
VETA has been requested to construct a college in Bagamoyo college.

It has been written by the penalty in Emmanuel.

It has been published: 04 December 2016.

District commissioner of Bagamoyo, Alhaj Majid Mwanga has requested the Education and Vocational Training Authority (VETA) to build a technical college in that district for helping the youth to kujiendeleza themselves.

While closing the training of rearing fish business that took place the day before yesterday at the Education and Fisheries Training Agency (FETA) that is in Mbegani, Bagamoyo where the training was sponsored by VETA, Mwanga said he is ready to give the authority to the area for the purpose of the construction. "We request to Veta this region."
He said that together with education that will be given will be sure to get milk that will be produced by students who will get a chance to join that university.
In addition, he said that together with the presence of many land conflicts in the district, they will ensure that they provide the area for help the development of the nation.
On his side, the acting director of VETA region, Asanterabi Kanza, said they will ensure that they are continuing to fund the training so as to promote business issues and private incomes.
He said the African Development Bank (AFDB) has helped to sponsor the training and explained that the graduates are required to use the training to produce employment to other people in order to bring development.
