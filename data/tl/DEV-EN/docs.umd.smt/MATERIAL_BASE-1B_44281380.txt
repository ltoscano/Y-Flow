Palawan Liberation commemoration

April 25, 2016 Orlan Jabagat.

Puerto Princesa, Palawan, Abr 25 (Pia) - reminisced recently in the historic Plaza Cuartel in the city of Puerto Princesa is the-71 anniversary of the liberasyon of Palawan.
The April 22 the date which is known by the veteran Palawenyo days of paglalapaya of the province from the hands of the mapanakop that the Japanese during the time of the second Filipino pandaigdig.
The memorial was started by a parade which was spearheaded by the ranks of the Armed Forces of the Philippines with the representatives of different't organisasyong society and the private sector.
He was followed by the wreath laying Ceremony and unveiling of the marker in Plaza Cuartel.
The Plaza Cuartel considered that the camp of the gerilyang Filipinos during the time of the second Filipino pandaigdig where the matatapang woman and man Filipinos the American soldiers in the fight the Japanese during the time of war.
An estimated 100 American soldiers were also imprisoned in a entrance or tunnel in Plaza Cuartel and massacred by burning of the life of them.
More than 1,000 are gerilyang Filipinos to fight in order to achieve the said freedom from the Japanese.
