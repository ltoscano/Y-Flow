
Two thousand poor Bulakenyo, benefited from the medical mission of the US-based doctor

January 28, 2016 Vinson F. Concepcion

The city of Malolos, January 28 (Pia) -- May two thousand poor residents of Bulacan, the one who benefited from this week in the medical mission conducted by the United States based Philippine Medical Association Medical Mission Foundation of Greater St. Louis, Missouri.
40 doctor was the one who gave the free service, including the surgery, urology, plastic surgery, OB-GYN, investigation cardiology, radiology, pathology, and pediatrics.
Who turn-over the group of Surgical equipment in Bulacan Medical Center.
In a statement, heartfelt thanks, governor Wilhelmino Sy-Alvarado to the people who does not allocate time to help the needy.
The PMA Medical Mission Foundation is in the fourth year when a medical mission in Bulacan who is supporting the program of the Department of Health that adopt the foreign medical missionaries of the local hospital.
