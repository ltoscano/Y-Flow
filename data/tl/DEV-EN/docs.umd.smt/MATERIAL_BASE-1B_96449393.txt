
Romblon Provincial Hospital, give free operation in bingot and ngongo

November 01, 2016 Dinnes Manzo M.

ODIONGAN, Romblon, November 1 (Pia) --- the Romblon Provincial Hospital (RPH) will conduct a free medicine and free operation for the bingot (Cleft lip) and ngongo (Cleft Palate) on November 13-16 in the town of Odiongan.

It is the purpose of the hospital management makapaghatid of service that it is true that there is a heart and give the new smile on the remains of a child with of this kind of disability.

Based on the study, it can be considered as a global crisis the existence of the child who was born with a cleft lip and cleft palate so iniaalay in the province and the local government of Odiongan the free surgery operation for the children.

The said operation, it would take only 45 minutes and give it a new hope for the patient to face the future with a smile on her lips.

Those who are in the operation that sasailalim magpatala early because they needed to be admitted to RPH which are on the 13th of November to perform the surgery operation.

The free operation is a big help to those from Romblon that there is a cleft lip and cleft palate who want to maisayos their disability.

For the additional information and question, you may call or text 09174120914 and look for just nurses Maey Padilla. (DM / Pia-MIMAROPA / Romblon)
