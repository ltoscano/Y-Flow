Walks for Life was held for the seniors

October 05 in, 2016 Nino S. Donato

Cauayan City, Isabela, October 5 (Pia) - attended by the the Senior citizens the 'Walk for Life' in a mall here in the city as part of the celebration of the Elderly Filipino week.

Said Shiela Marie Estudillo, Mall Manager of SM City Cauayan, more than 500 Senior citizens of the city attended the said activity.

He said, aside from the fun walks, also had a Zumba dance, medical mission and PhilHealth Registration for senior citizens who are sick and there is still no PhilHealth cards.

"Nagagalak the management of SM City Cauayan because there are a lot of Senior citizens attended the very front walks for Life which was held in the said mall," Estidullo added.
(ALM / NSD / Pia-2 Isabela)
