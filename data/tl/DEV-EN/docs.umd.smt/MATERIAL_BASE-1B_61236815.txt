Proper care for the eyes discussed in N. Cotabato

August 31, 2016

Kidapawan City, North Cotabato, August.
31 (Pia) --- to strengthen the campaign in proper care of the eye, a Basic Eye care Orientation were held in the capitol Rooftop in Barangay Amas here in the city recently.

According to Integrated Provincial Health Office (IPHO) Head Dr. Eva Rabaya, together with the Department of Health (DOH) 12 in the said activity that responded by those who have taya in the sectors of the health.

Binigyang- emphasis by Doctor Rabaya that the eye is one of the most important part of the body, so't need the right care and protection to them.

Gave a reminder how to avoid early destruction of their eyes or low vision until blindness, Dr. Zandro Casado who representante of the DOH 12.

Included in his warn is to avoid it too much use or pagtutok in the cellphones, tablets and other which is.

On the other hand, hinimok of Governor Emmylou Mendoza those participating in activities to follow the advice and warn of Dr. Casado and to share their knowledge to others.
