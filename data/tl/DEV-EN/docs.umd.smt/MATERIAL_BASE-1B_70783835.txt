Fil-Am Lions Club, gave the book in Nueva Vizcaya

August 24, 2016 Ben Moses Ebreo

Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, August 24 (Pia) - gave a free books the group of San Francisco Filipino-American Lions Club (SFFALC) recently in Nueva Vizcaya as help out to those in school.

According to former Governor Ruth Padilla, the book is given to Nueva Vizcaya General Comprehensive High Schoo L (NVGCHS) in the town of Bayombong, Solano East Central School (SECS) and Bintawan National High School (BNHS) in the town of Solano and Quezon National High School (QNHS) in the town of Quezon.

The free book contains the children's lessons, reference, Christian lessons, biology, history, Science, math and geography lessons.

"We were in SFFALC because this is going to help the schools to implement the K-12 program, especially the finds it hard schools.
The our gratitude for donasyong, "this statement of Padilla.

Donation, the book considers the provincial government and the SFFALC in the administration of former Governor Ruth Padilla.
