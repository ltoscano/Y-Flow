Veterans Reg'l Hospital, launched 'Bike For Life' program

April 27, 2016 Ben Moses Ebreo

Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, April 27 (Pia) - launched by the Veterans Regional Hospital (VRH) recently the "Bike for Life" program to strengthen the relationship of the employees, to pursue the healthy living and communicating with other government Hospitals in Northern Luzon.
According to Doctor Cirilo Galindez, VRH chief, the launching of "Bike for Life" program, started with three days that bicycling caused by 200 - kilometer road from San Jose City, Central Pangasinan, San Fernando La Union, Baguio City and Ambuklao, Benguet to Kayapa, Nueva Vizcaya.
"Because of this, there was a with the health workers in other Hospital of the government which we," statement of Galindez.
The group of "Bike for Life" program, visit the medical centers of Northern Luzon like Region 1 Training and Medical Center (R1TMC) in Dagupan City, Pangasinan, Ilocos Training and Regional Medical Center (ITRMC) in San Fernando La Union and Baguio General Hospital and Medical Center (BGHMC) in Baguio City.
According to Galindez, because of the successful launching of the program, they will continue it as a regular work to encourage others to join in their advocacy about the healthy lifestyle.
