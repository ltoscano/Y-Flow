60th game nasikwat of Antique By Ramil Cruz November 25, 2016 the Department of Education ipasama the chess, 50-meter and CENTURY dashes in 20 sports for the differently-abled athletes in Antique Province that winning the host of the 60th Palarong Pambansa 2017.
Nagkakaisang this decision of regional directors with the game Management Committee which is composed of the Department of Interior and Local Government and Philippine Sports Commission.
In the election last November 18 of Palarong Pambansa Selection Committee, chosen by the San Jose de Buenavista City, Antique that nakaungos in five votes against four of Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental.
Who retreat from the Negros Occidental, Cebu and Iloilo because of the problem of funds.
This is the first time in Antique to be the host of the weeklong sports Fest for the student-athletes in elementary and secondary from 18 region of the country to serve as the 60 this edisyon.
