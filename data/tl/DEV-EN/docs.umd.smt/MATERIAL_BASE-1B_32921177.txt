Divine word College of Calapan, had of the 70-year anniversary

March 12, 2016

Calapan, Oriental Mindoro, Mar 12 (Pia) - the recently celebrated the 70 years of Divine pagkakatatag word College of Calapan (DWCC).
The Naitatag DWCC last April 1946 that first named as Mindoro Junior College that there was a 37 enrollees.
Last January 1981, replaced with the hope of Mindoro College until the Divine word College of Calapan.
As time goes by, it was proven that the high quality of education to be given to the students.
Year 1995, led Region IV and Top 10 in the national level in having a high level of board passers that published the Philippine Star last June 13, 1995.
Also tumatak in the history of the DWCC Student in free Enterprise (SIFE) who won the SIFE National Exposition held in holiday Inn, Manila last February 2002 until it became the representative of the country in the SIFE World Cup at Amsterdam, Netherlands last year that.
That is why this year, of stepping on the 70 anniversary of the pagkakatatag, aside from the annual doing for the celebration, the task will be colorful and contests that kinatampukan of Mindoro Festival Competition, search for 2016 Miss DWCC, movies Divinista short Film Festival, Variety show, and the pinakamakulay that fireworks display.
Under the leadership of Fr.

Anthony Ibarra Fabella, SVD, as President and Vice President for Academic Affairs Dr. Aleli Dugan, is expected to be achieved more success of DWCC to give quality education for the citizens of Mindoro in the future.
