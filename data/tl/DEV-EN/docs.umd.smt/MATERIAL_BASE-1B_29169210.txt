August 22, 2016 Voltaire N. Dequina

San Jose, Occidental Mindoro, August 22 (Pia) - was recently the two-day Regional blood Services Meeting of the Department of Health (DOH) Mimaropa in Sikatuna Beach Hotel, San Jose.

It is the purpose of the said meeting to discuss the issues regarding the operation of the blood service facilities in every province like the lack of blood, the method in pagimbita of people buluntaryong give blood (donor) and maseguro that there is enough supply in the region.
