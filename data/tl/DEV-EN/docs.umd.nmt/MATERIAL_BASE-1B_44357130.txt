Medical Mission 2016, will be conducted in San Jose, occupMindanao.

January 10, 2016 Voltaire N. Dequina.

San Jose, Occidental Mindoro, January 10 (PIA) -- scheduled to do a medical mission in January 22-27 will be held in San Jose District Hospital (SJDH), Barangay San Isidro, town of San Jose.
Three medical teams from other countries will give free service.
These are the destination ination Hope, which brings free ckaliwang lip and ngala-ngala operation with children who are nine years old down, MedLend Team that will look at the reproductive health of women and the Prosthesis Team that several times of return to the province to help those whose hand cut off and feet to have artificial limbs.
According to Jerina Pablo, Administrative Officer of San Jose District Hospital, those who wish to be beniciated by the medical mission should be visited immediately in the district hospital to be given initial research.
(VND / PIA 4B / Occ Mindanao).
