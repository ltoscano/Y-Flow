Dengue calamity in Tupi, South Cotabato By Betchai Julian July 23, 2016 Tuluyan when the town of Tupi in South Cotabato was placed under state of calamity after the four children who died and 300 others were suffering from dengue.
Based on the data of Tupi Mayor Reynaldo Tamayo, three barangays in their town have a high case of dengue and it is the Poblacion, Acreprimand and Bunao.
In said towns, the deceased victims came from the age of 12 years old down.
The patients have been packed in their district Hospital with dengue reasons to declare the state of calamity in order to use funds.
Also advised the barangay officials and areas of leaders to conduct cleaning to eradicate the mosquito with dengue.
