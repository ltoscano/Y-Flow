Manila, Philippines - those who have been hit by HIV / AIDS infection this year is already late to 3,000.
This was found out in the Department of Health where the AIDS Registry of the DOH National Epidemiology Center (NEC), 3,154 is getting sick of HIV / AIDS last January 2013 after they record last August 2013 of 382 bagong HIV AB sero-positive indibidwal.
Such data allegedly higher than 40 percent of the 272 cases of the similar date last year.
It was found out that 96 percent of the new case are men, most are aged 27, or the age range 17-71 years, 60 percent are at the age of 20 to 29.
341 who have been HIV / AIDS was infected because of the sexual contact, 87 percent of the victims are men who sleep with both men, while 41 are due to Needle pagbabahagi of the injecting drug users.
The most recorded cases of the disease in National Capital Region (41%), Region 7 (17%) and Region 4A (13%).
