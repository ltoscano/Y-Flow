AFP Chief already pack up.

Joy Cantos.

Manila, Philippines - outgoing Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) Chief of Staff General Emmanuel Bautista is already scheduled to go down to his position in July 18.
Bautista is the product of the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) Class 1981, fourth Chief of Staff under the administration of President Aquino.
According to the officials near Bautista, after being busy with the "exit call" to the military station that he used to be served, the general and his family are busy with the pack.
Bautista is scheduled to step down from his position in the coming Friday or 2 days before his fifth birthday, the compulsory age pagreretiro in AFP.
Included in the contenders as the burial of Bautista is the AFP Vice Chief of Staff Lt. Gen. Gregorio Pio Catapang; Philippine Air Force (PAF) Chief Lt. Gen. Jeffrey Delgado; Philippine Army Chief Lt. Gen. Heriberri Iriberri and Vice Admiral Jesus Millan, watawat officer of the Philippine Navy.

But if the bet of the presidential kapatid, Delgado is the elected Chief of Staff.
