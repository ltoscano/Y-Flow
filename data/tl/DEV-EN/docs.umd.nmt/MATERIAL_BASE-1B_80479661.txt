Planting soybean insist on the farmers.

Angie dela Cruz.

Manila, Philippines - the Department of agriculture (DA) defeated the farmers in Central Luzon to plant soybean as an additional income and even helpful to the health of the people.
According to DA National Soybean program coordinator Rosemarie Aquino, the soya gives immunity to the body of the people to fight the heart disease, cholesterol and cancer.
Aside from this, there is also a big help of soya in passing the land farm and raising the alagaing pork, chicken and fish.
Aquino adds that the government will help the farmers in planting, training, improving and in the way of eating soybean.
The DA appealed to the public to help improving and patronize the products from the soybean that can also profit from the additional business of every filipino family.
