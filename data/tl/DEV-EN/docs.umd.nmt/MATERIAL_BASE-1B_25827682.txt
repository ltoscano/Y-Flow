
PMA entrance exam will be conducted in the city of Dagupan in August 21.

August 15, 2016 April M.

City of Dagupan, August 15 (PIA) - in August 21, the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) will conduct the Philippine Military Academy (PMA) in the University of Pangasinan of the entrance examination for those who want to be the cadet.

According to PMA, such an exam is the first step in the process of choosing for the caeks ship in the premyadong pamumuno training school in the country.

Those who want to get an exam need to be natural-born Filipino citizen; there is a height of 5 talampakan; sinasaktan; there is a good behavior; single and not once married; high school graduate where the K-12 program is finished, before the K-12 program, it may be applied; and no administrative or kasong kriminal.

The pass of the said examination will be part of the PMA Class 2021 officials that will be met in the academy in a Oath-taking Ceremony at reception Rites which will be held in April 1, 2017.
