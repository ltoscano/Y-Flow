When the host of the Romblon of MIMAROPA RAA meet, it is possible that it will not push through.

Enero 18, 2016.

Romblon, Romblon, January 18 (PIA) - it is possible that the host of Romblon is possible to push through the MIMAROPA Regional athletic Association (NCCA AA) meet that it is scheduled for January 31 to February 4 this year.
According to Romblon Mayor Gerard S. Montojo, 1 percent is the possibility that the market of the region in the town of Romblon will push through because of the decision of the governor of five provinces not to continue this because of the big damage of last typhoon in Mimaropa.
"We are ready for the host of the warehouse AA meet but I don't know what happened," Montojo stated.
The MIMAROPA RAA is scheduled to be held in 2016 in the town of Romblon which will be attended by the players from Oriental Mindoro, Calapan City, Occidental Mindoro, Marinduque, Romblon, Palawan Province and Puerto Princesa City.
According to an official of DepEd Division of Romblon, the Calapan City and Marinduque said that the participants of the region and five schools Division were said to have refused to join the said sports.
While writing this news, DepEd IV-B Regional Office has no statement on how to make options from the nationalistic Pambansa (DM / PIA-IVB / Romblon).
