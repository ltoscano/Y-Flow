Veterans Reg'l Hospital, launched a Bike For Life 'program.

April 27, 2016 Ben Moses Ebreo.

Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, April 27 (PIA) launched the Veterans Regional Hospital (VRH) recently the "Bike para sa Life" program to strengthen the organization of the employees, pursue the healthy lifestyle and communicating with other government hospitals in northern Luzon.
According to Doctor Cirilo Galindez, VRH chief, the paglulunsad of the "Bike para sa Life" program started for three days quarrels of 200 - kilometer road from San Jose City, Central Pangasinan, San Fernando La Union, Baguio City and Ambuklao, Benguet to Kayapa, Nueva Vizcaya.
"Because of this, health workers had to connect with other hospitals of the government that we passed," said Galindez.
The group of "Bike para Life" program visited the medical centers of northern luzon like Region 1 Training and Medical Center (R1TMC) in Dagupan City, Pangasinan, Ilocos Training and Regional Medical Center (ITRMC) in San Fernando La Union and Baguio General Hospital (BGHMC) in Baguio City.
Ani Galindez, because of the success paglulunsad of the program, they will continue with it as a regular task to encourage others to join their advocacy regarding malusog pamumuhay.
