Indigent senior citizens in Magdiwang, Romblon received social pension.

Enero 20, 2016.

Celebrate, Romblon, January 20 (PIA) --- the DSWD Romblon distributed yesterday to the social pension of the 360 who is a senior citizen in the town of Magdiwang, Romblon, who received P3,000 payment for the 3rd and 4th quarter of the past year.
The penlesson is financial help from the national government which is showing the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) for the grandparents who are aging 65 years old and above.
This benefits is just for the older poor who are weak, sickly, disabled and no pension from GSIS, SSS, Veterans benepisyaryo or other insurance companies and no permanent source of income or support to the family member.
It is the purpose of Social pension Program to fix the living of the poor senior citizen through giving the ability to buy food, medicine and bitamina.
SocPen Program of DSWD is a big help in the indigent senior citizen who is having a hard time work due to old age or is sick in the body (DM / Lbr / PIA-IVB / Romblon).
