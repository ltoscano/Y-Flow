
Two thousand poor Bulakenyo, benefited from the medical mission of the US-base doctor.

Enero 28, 2016 Vinson F. Concepcion.

The city of Malolos, January 28 (PIA) -- May two thousand poor residents of Bulacan benefited from the medical mission conducted by the United States base Philippine Medical Association Medical Mission Foundation ng Greater St. Louis, Missouri.
40 doctors gave a free service including the surgery, urology, plastic surgery, OB-GYN, IM cardiology, Pathology, radiology, and schedulatrics.
The group of Surgical kagamitan in Bulacan Medical Center also started.
In a statement, governor thanked Wilhelmino Sy-Alvarado in the people who did not make time to allocate time to help those in need.
The PMA Medical Mission Foundation is on the fourth year when the medical mission is conducting a medical mission in Bulacan to support the program of the Department of Health to adopt foreign medical missionaries of the local hospitals.
