Record breaking in the pagyanig drill recorded By Jojo de Guzman Agosto 5, 2016 every time there is a disaster and calamities are important to ensure the safety of each citizens so in Olongapo City National High School (OCNHS), the said school recorded a new record in the 2nd quarter nationwide Simultaneous lindol Drill (NSED) recently.
From the former hour 27 minutes, more than 4,000 students will be lowered their time in 4 minutes and 12 seconds, fastest all the time compared to the drill of other schools in the city.
A partner of OCNHS Disaster Risk Reduction Management (DRRM), City Disaster Management Office is the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD), Olongapo Traffic Management and Public Safety (OTMPS) and mga magulang Teachers Association officers (PTA).
OCNHS Principal IV Leonardo De Guzman, Principal IV said, because of the good record of students many lives will be safe in case there is a disaster and calamity that needs immediate evacuation.
