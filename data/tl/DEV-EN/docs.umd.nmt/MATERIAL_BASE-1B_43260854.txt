Maglakad para sa Life isinagawa for the seniors.

October 05, 2016 Nino S. Donato S.

Cauayan City, Isabela, Oktubre 5 (PIA) - the Senior citizens attended the 'Walk para Life' in a mall here in the city as part of the celebration of Elmarahan Filipino Santa.

According to Shiela Marie Estudillo, Mall Manager of SM City Cauayan, more than 500 Senior citizens of the city attended the said activity.

According to him, aside from the fun walk, there was also Zumba dance, medical mission and PhilHealth registration for senior citizens who are sick and there is no Philhealth cards.

"Happy is the management of SM City Cauayan because many Senior citizens attended the very front walk para sa buhay held in the said mall," Estidullo added.
(Alm / NSD / PIA-2 Isabela)
