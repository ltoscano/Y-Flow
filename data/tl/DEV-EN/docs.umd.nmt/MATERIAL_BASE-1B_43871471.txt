
The case of dengue, went down in Pangasinan.

August 19, 2016 April M.

Pangasinan, August 19 (PIA) - the number of people got sick of dengue in the province of Pangasinan from January 1 to August 16 this year compared to the similar time in 2015.

According to the record of the Provincial Health Office (Phor), around 1, 486 people got sick of dengue this 2016 compared to a higher record 1,703 last year.

The Phor seven were recorded dead due to the disease dengue this 2016 while 12 were recorded last year.
