
Equipment in farming, distributed by the city Agriculture Office of San Pablo.

Agosto 23, 2016.

City of SAN Pablo, Laguna, August 23 (PIA) - a different farm will be distributed recently by the herbs organic magsasaka in Barangay San Francisco at the office of the city Agriculture office (Cao).

Aside from the farm kagamitan, there are also distributed in the farmers whose 300 sack abono (vermicast), 40 drums, and 400 seedlings of guyabano and lansones.

"All the distributed equipment in farming is that we personally asked for the Department of agriculture Region IV-A that they were given to and we did not fail," said the city hall ulturist Elizabeth Eseo.

Eseo is also very thankful for Maro Loreto Amante in continuing to support the programs of agriculture in their office.

"It's not just a transfer of technology but also helping the farmers to have the appropriate equipment in farming what our office is doing," end of Eseo.
