Soldiers are left short of food.

Joy Cantos (Filipino Star Ngayon) It will be recalled updated SSetyembre 15, 2013 - 12: 00am.

Manila, Philippines - a hunger for the lack of food is the majority of the soldiers who are joining the fighting in Zamboanga City.
According to some soldiers who refused to be identified, they usually did not have breakfast and luckily, if they were eating pandesal or soup because the supply of supply was usually delayed.
"We have a lot of supply of bullets that is why our stomach is a little complaining, because we are not going to have breakfast, here, here, a Army Sergeant," said an Army Sergeant who did not want to speak names.
The soldiers are happy and many residents of Barangay Sta.
Barbara, Santa.
Catalina, Talon, Rio Hondo and Mammore giving them breakfast like boiled egg, soup and pandesal.
In the mentioned place, the MNLF is hiding a hold of hostages.
