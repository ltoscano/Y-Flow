DOH: Fireworks biktima at 524 By Noel Abuel Enero 3, 2017 up to 524 is the total number of victims of fireworks in the previous New Year's Day.
According to Department of Health (DOH) Asec.
Eric Tayag, 521 of the victims were hit and three swallowed by paputok.
In the said data, 426 are all men or 82 percent.
Most of the firecracker-related came from Metro Manila, which reached 297.
Meanwhile, the primary kind of fireworks that was damaged in 177 victims was the piccolo.
Around 98 were damaged in the eyes or 19 percent of the total number of naputukan.
Despite this, the said number is still low as compared to 874 victims last welcoming the New Year.
Most of the victims are all children who use piccolo.
DOH appeal to the people to avoid using firecrackers in order to reduce the same ones that are going to be living in it.
