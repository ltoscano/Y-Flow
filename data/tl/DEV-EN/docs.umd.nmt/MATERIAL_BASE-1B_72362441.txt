Projects under IPSP Bayanihan will be implemented in the Dipaculao, Casiguran, Casiguran.

Marso 01, 2016 Joselthis S. Libranda.

Baler, Aurora, March 1 (PIA) -- the projects under Internal Peace or IPSP Bayanihan will be implemented in the towns of Dipaculao and Casiguran in Aurora.
This is after Memorandum of Undernakatayo or MOU its mayors in the 56th Infantry Battalion of the Philippine Army and his partners of civil lipunan organisasyon and from the private sector.
According to Battalion Commander Lt. Col. Joey Escanillas, MOU is going to give way to different civil military project in two municipalities that have been able to live in business, social services, and it is a wise ststura.
Earlier signing MOU in the 56IB and his partners are some barangays in the town of Maria Aurora and San Luis and even in Nueva Vizcaya and Quirino.
