Right care of the eye tinalakay in N. Cotabato?

Agosto 31, 2016.

Kidapawan City, North Cotabato, August.
31 (PIA) --- to strengthen the campaign in the right care of the eye, a Basic Eye Cis up to be held at Capitol rooftop in Barangay Amore here in the city recently.

According to Integrated Provincial Health Office (IPHO) Head Dr. Eva RAbaya, their partner is a partner of the Department of Health (DOH) 12 in the said activity which attended by those who have a bet on the health sector.

Dr. Rabaya emphasized that the eye is one of the most important parts of the body so the proper care and protection of them is needed.

Meanwhile, gave reminders on how to avoid an early destruction of eyes or low vision until the blindness of Doctor Zpetual Casado who is representative of DOH 12.

Included in his warning is to avoid getting too much use or focusing on the cellphone, tablet and other gadget.

On the other hand, Governor Emmylou Mendoza urged those participating in the activities to follow the advice and reminding Doctor Casado and share what they learned from others.
