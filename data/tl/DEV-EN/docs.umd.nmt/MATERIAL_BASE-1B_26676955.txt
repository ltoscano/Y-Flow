14 BIFF todas in the fight.

Joy Cantos (Filipino Star Ngayon) It will be recalled updated Marso 8, 2015 - 12: 00am.

Manila, Philippines - up to 14 members of the Bangsamoro Islamic Freedom Fighters (BIFF) was killed while 26 were wounded in the continued offensive of the government troops in the said rebel Muslim this Friday afternoon and Saturday morning in Maguindanao.
According to AFP Spokesman Brig. Gen Josel, Kakilala, encountered the operating troops of Joint Task Force Central around 2: 00 in the afternoon the BIFF rebels at the limits of Datu Saudi Ampatuan, Talayan, Datu Salibo and Datu Piang.
In the report of the field commanders, up to 14 BIFF rebels were killed until yesterday morning and 13 were wounded.
Currently, according to the official, the gunfire between both sides continues to be investigated.
On the date of AFP Public Affairs Office Chief Lt. Col. Harold Cabunoc, 13 were wounded among the soldiers including a pilot of the Philippine Air Force (PAF) who are conducting a air evacuation in the ranks of the military.
The operation is part of all out the opensiba of AFP Chief General Gregorio Pio Catapang Jr. against 300 BIFF rebels who continue to be spreading terrorism in Central Mindanao.
In the report of the Army's 601st Infantry Brigade, the troops of the Philippine Army attacked the lungga of the BIFF but with a lot of enemies, the military was conducting a "tropa nawa'y" the military using the Huey helicopter.

The official announced that the AFP will not stop the BIFF until they are not totally suffering so that the civilians who are usually victims in the bombing of the group will not be destroyed.
