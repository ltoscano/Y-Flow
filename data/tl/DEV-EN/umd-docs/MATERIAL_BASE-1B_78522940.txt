Indigent senior to celebrate, Romblon receive social pension

January 20, 2016

Celebrate, Romblon, January 20 (Pia) --- distributed yesterday the DSWD Romblon social pension of 360 that indigent senior citizens in the town of celebrate, Romblon, received P3,000 that a payment for the 3rd and 4th period of the past year.
This is the pensyong financial help from the government that a national ipinamamahagi of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) for the grandfather't grandmother with age 65 years old and above.
The benefits this is only for the elderly poor is weak, sickly, disabled and not received any pension from GSIS, SSS, Veterans beneficiaries or other insurance companies and does not have a permanent source of income or support to the members of the family.
It is the purpose of the Social pension Program to fix the lives of the poor that senior citizens by giving the ability to them to buy food, medicine and vitamins.
It is a big help to the SocPen Program of the DSWD to the indigent senior citizens who are the maghanapbuhay due to old age or illness is in the body. (DM / LBR / Pia-IVB / Romblon)
