Increase in the number of the sick of dengue in Culion, meanwhile,

August 20, 2016

Puerto Princesa, Palawan, August 20 (Pia) --- continuously being monitored by the Municial Health Office (MHO) the increase in the number of the sick of dengue in the town of Culion.

According to Doctor Charlie Tejada, Culion Municipal Health Officer, up to 49 cases of the sick of dengue their recorded from January 2016 until August 12.
This case of 39 higher compared to the number of cases of the past year in the same month that only 10.

Those who has the dengue is now being observed in Culion Sanitarium the General Hospital.
The recorded sick dengue, from the town of Libis, and Jardin of said town.

In spite of the increase of cases of dengue in the said town, was still no basis for the MHO magrekomenda in Local Chief Executive to declare dengue outbreak.

In relation to this, also launched in the town of Culion the bad-samang cleaning the place that might pamugaran by mosquitoes learned of dengue.

Dr. Tejada has also ordered that the employees of the MHO to conduct a Information Dessimination to prevent the increase of the sick of dengue.
Included here are paghihikayat to the citizens of cleaning the surroundings and the early pagpapakonsulta the doctor to be prevented the pain.

(OCJ / Pia-MIMAROPA, Palawan)
