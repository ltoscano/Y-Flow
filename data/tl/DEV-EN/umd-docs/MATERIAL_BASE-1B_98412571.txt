Chacha hearing nilangaw By Eralyn Prado January 19, 2017 Dismayado Senate President Pro-Tempore Franklin Drilon for not appearing of some inimbitahang Resource persons in the first hearing of the Senate in relation to the proposal to change the Constitution or known as charter change (chacha).
According to Drilon, chairman of the Senate Committee on constitutional amendments, instead of the start of his committee will conduct a consultation in the province, she still needs magpatawag of another hearing here in Manila.
This is caused by not appearing of their inimbitahang Resource persons.
I must express "My disappointment and the will take interest of the public.
When we had Our first hearing, we had a Good number of invitees, but the responses were lukewarm, I must say.
We wanted to have MORE Resource persons present but none appeared, so we will try again.
We will have a Second hearing in Manila to invite those WHO were not able to come, "according to Drilon.
