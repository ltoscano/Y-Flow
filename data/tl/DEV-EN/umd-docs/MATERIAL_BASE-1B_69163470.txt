Paghost of Romblon of MIMAROPA RAA Meet, it is possible that not

January 18, 2016

Romblon, Romblon, January 18 (Pia), it is not to make the pagho-host of Romblon in MIMAROPA Regional Athletic Association (MRAA) Meet palarong that was scheduled to on January 31 until February 4 of this year.
According to Romblon Mayor Gerard S. Montojo, 1 percent is the possibility that the palarong pangrehiyon in the town of Romblon due to the decision of the governor of the five provinces that do not continue because of the huge damage of the typhoon in Mimaropa.
"That we are ready to pagho-host of MRAA Meet but I do not know what happened't allegedly did not push through this," statement of Montojo.
Scheduled ganapin Mimaropa RAA Meet 2016 in the town of Romblon to be attended by the players from Oriental Mindoro, Calapan City, Occidental Mindoro, Marinduque, Romblon, Palawan Province and Puerto Princesa City.
According to an official of DepEd Division of Romblon, the Calapan City and Marinduque the stated that participants of the palarong pangrehiyon and five Schools Division Superintendent the refused to not join in the said sports.
While writing this news, there is still no statement the DepEd IV-B Regional Office how the planned choosing athletes ilalahok in Palarong Pambansa. (DM / Pia-IVB / Romblon)
