Also boasted of Bayron Pamahalaang the programs of the city for the members who, he said, surely received by them because almost everyone is registered to the office of the Senior Citizen's Affairs (OSCA).

At present, up to 8.4 percent of the residents of the city were recorded members of the OSCA, these are high in the national average wage more than six percent only.
