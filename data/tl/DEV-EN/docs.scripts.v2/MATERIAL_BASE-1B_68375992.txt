US launched an airstrike in Libya By AFP, Noel Abuel August 3, 2016 conducting an attack and airstrikes in the United States in the largest camp of terrorist Islamic State in Libya.
In the report, the said strikes targeted the port city of Sirte area filled with members of the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS).
Libyan Prime Minister Fayez al-Sarraj called the said airstrike a loss.
This is considered the first time that there was a US military intervention in the Libyan Unity government.
According to the Pentagon, the strikes have been authorised by President Barack Obama as support to the government forces fighting against ISIS.
