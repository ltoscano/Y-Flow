Nagaowa handa nang sumabak By Elech Dawa July 6, 2016
Handa nang makipag-upakan si Benguet native Jujeath Nagaowa sa World Series of Fighting Global (WSOF Global) Philippines vs The World Fight sa Hulyo 30 sa Smart Araneta Coliseum.
May 2-0 record sa Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) si Nagaowa at kasama siya sa lalaban sa magaganap na Philippines vs. The World. “I’m more than ready, I’ve been waiting to stage a comeback, buong puso ko at buong isip ko ibibigay ko sa laban na ito.” pahayag ni Nagaowa, “Gusto ko ipakita sa kababayan natin na kaya ng Pilipinas lumaban sa mga taga ibang bansa.” Makakabaldugan ni Nagaowa si Japanese MMA fighter Yuko Kiryu sa WSOF GC3’s fight card.
May training sa boxing at former titleholder sa Women’s International Boxing Association (WIBA) light flyweight division si Nagaowa, dati rin siyang WBC Asian Boxing Council Female Atomweight titleholder.
Na interview at na head line si Nagaowa sa Wall Street Journal, mababasa ang ‘Female Boxer Fights to Open Mixed Martial Arts Doors for Filipino Women’.
Isang Filipina ang owner ng WSOF GC’s, si Dunessa Hesser ay based sa Las Vegas pero ipinanganak sa Escalante, Bacolod.
“The Philippines is known very well for combat sports and Filipino fans are very passionate, despite that, very few Philippine MMA fighters have gained global recognition.” ani Hesser.
