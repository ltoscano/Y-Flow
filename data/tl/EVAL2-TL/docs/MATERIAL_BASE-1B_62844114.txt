Cignal work in progress pa By Elech Dawa October 4, 2016
Tournament favorite ang Cignal, pero sinabi ni coach Sammy Acaylar na marami pa silang hahasain sa pagsabak sa Philippine Superliga Grand Prix na sisiklab sa Sabado, Oct. 8, sa FilOil Flying V Center sa San Juan.
Hinuhubog pa ni Acaylar ang tamang timpla ng HD Spikers na nagbalasa sa offseason ng inter-club tournament taya ang isang tiket sa AVC Asian Women’s Club Championship sa Chinese Taipei sa 2017.
Maliban kina imports Olympian Lynda Morales ng Puerto Rico at Laura Schaudt ng US, pulos new recruits ang starting crew ng Cignal. Balik si dating MVP Venus Bernal, nakuha si Paneng Mercado mula F2 Logistics. Newbies din sa HD Spikers sina Lourdes Patilano, Mylene Paat, Janine Marciano, Sandra Delos Santos, at playmaker Shawna-Lei Santos na ni-recruit pa mula St. John’s University sa Division I ng US NCAA.
Kukumpletuhin nina veterans Michelle Laborte, Cherry Vibas, Len Cortel at Jheck Dionela ang roster.
“If you look closely, almost all of our players are new. We are still adjusting on the court and developing chemistry. We’re still a work in progress. It’s still a long way to go,” punto ni Acaylar.
