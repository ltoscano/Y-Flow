Betten back-to-back sa Bellevue Triathlon By Ramil Cruz November 11, 2016
Salo sina Sam Betten ng Australia at Amelia Watkonson ng New Zealand sa top honors ng pro division sa 2016 Bellevue Resort 5150 Triathlon sa Panglao Island ng Bohol nitong weekend.
Coast-to-coast win sa clocking na 2 hours, 1 minute, 40 seconds ang isinumite ni Betten para tagumpay na idepensa ang titulo sa men’s division ng 1.5-km swim, 40-k. bike, 10k run. Bumuntot sina German Henry Beck (2:10:07) at Aussie Daniel Brown (2:29:37).
Sa women’s side, pinalitan ni Watkonson (2:10:19) sa trono si Australian Dimity Lee-Duke (2:15:10) na nagkasya sa segunda. Tersera si Michelle Duffield (2:32:22). Sa elite male, top three winners sina Jorry Ycong ng Repro Optime (2:10:55), Paul Jumamil (2:11:44) at Emmanuel Commendador (2:11:56), 1-2-3 sa female sina Jenny Rose Guerrero (2:38:57), Cianyl Gonzales (2:18:54) at Amale Jopson (3:00:42).
Nanguna sa sprint men’s sina Lucian Antonio Alejo (1:11:43), Dwight Galolo (1:14:19) at Brian Borling (1:16:41), sa distaff side sina Aaliyah Mataragnon (2:24:52), Kyla Gomez (1:41:41) at Patricia Lee (1:45:39).
Ang tatlong nanguna sa relay ay ang Team Paseo de Loon (2:25:06), Team Tribuddies of Tagum (2:32:30) at Team Tri-S (3:07:29.
