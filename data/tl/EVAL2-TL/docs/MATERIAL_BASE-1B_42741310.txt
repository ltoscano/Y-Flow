Ronda Pilipinas qualifying races sa Nob. 6 By Ramil Cruz October 30, 2016
Tuloy ang paghahagilap sa future cycling stars sa bansa sa pagpadyak ng dalawang qualifying races ng 7th LBC Ronda Pilipinas 2017 sa Nob. 6 kasabayan ng LBC Giro de Pilipinas sa Subic Bay Freeport Zone grounds.
May 30 riders ang pepedal sa culminating event, na isa sa biggest cycling race sa bansa na papasinayaan sa North sa Ilocos region sa Feb. 4 at matatapos sa Iloilo City sa Mar. 4 nang susunod na taon.
“The top 30 cyclists in the LBC Ronda Pilipinas Subic qualifying race will make it to the main Ronda event in February next year,” deklara kahapon ni second-year Ronda Pilipinas race director Jingo Hervas. Hinirit niyang sa mga hindi makakapasok sa cut, pwede pang mag-final qualifiers sa Bacolod City sa Dec. 4, na sisikad kasabay ng Dans 360 Beynte bike event.
“Those who are automatically in, they should claim their slots by registering,” wakas ni Hervas.
Tumataginting na nakataya sa 12-stage main event ang P1 million cash prize para sa champion na handog ng presentor LBC at major sponsors na Mitsubishi, Petron, ASG Group, Dans360 at Donen sa event na benditado ni PhilCycling president Abraham ‘Bambol’ Tolentino.
“We believe that somewhere out there, there are gems in the rough waiting to be discovered like Ranlen Maglantay,” ani Moe Chulani, Ronda sports development head. “LBC Sports Devt. Corp. feels everyone should have the chance to join Ronda Pilipinas 2016, which is the fourth biggest race in the world in terms of distance covered, not just the elite riders.”
