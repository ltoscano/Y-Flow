NCR teachers nagsanay sa wushu By Ramil Cruz August 22, 2016
Nakumpleto ng 67 sa dumalong 90 teachers ng National Capital Region ang Wushu Coaching Course Clinic sa loob ng walong sunod na Sabado, huli nitong Agosto 20 sa Rizal Memorial Coliseum sa misyong hindi na mabokya sa medalya sa 60th Palarong Pambansa 2017.
Bumahagi ang mga dalubhasa ng sport na sina two-time world champion Samson Co (clinic director), former world champ Mark Robert Rosales (taolu), two-time World Cup titlist Jessie Aligaga (sanda), 2015 Jakarta World Championships medalists Daniel Parantaac (silver) at Francisco Solis (bronze) bilang demonstrators, at Chinese coach Majing Wei.
“We are expecting that our teachers will be a big help to our student-athletes to perform better in next year’s Palarong Pambansa” ani NCR-Department of Education-Education Support Services Division chief Jocelyn Vilanueva-Marcial. “We went empty handed in last April’s Bicol Palarong Pambansa wushu competition.”
