<2>	June 27, 2016 Dinnes M. Manzo	June 27, 2016 Dinnes M. Manzo
<3>	ODIONGAN, Romblon, Hunyo 27 (PIA) --- Nakatanggap kamakailan ang lalawigan ng Romblon ng isang unit ng Mobile Dental Bus mula sa Department of Health (DOH).	Odiongan, Romblon, June 27 (PIA) - the province of Romblon recently received from the Department of Health (DOH) a unit of the Mobile Dental Bus.
<4>	Ayon kay Dr. Ruth Cervo ng DOH Romblon Provincial Office, ang naturang bus ay may dalawang dental chair, stainless na lababo, upuang hintayan ng mga magpapabunot o magpapakonsulta, at mesa ng dentista.	according to Doctor Ruth Cervo of DOH Romblon Provincial Office, the bus has two dental chairs, a stainless steel lavatory, reception chairs for those lined to consult, and a dentist desk.
