<1>	HUMANAP KA PANGIT: Reflection for 4th Sunday of Lent Year B - March 15, 2015 - YEAR OF THE POOR	look for someone ugly: reflection for 4th Sunday of Lent year B - March 15, 2015 - year of the poor.
<2>	May isang pari na tinatawag na "Parekoy" na kuwela at mahilig biruin ang kanyang mga parokyano lalong-lalo na ang mga nag-aaply ng kasal.	there is one priest called "Parekoy", who is funny and likes to tease his parishioners, especially those who are applying for marriage.
<3>	Minsan may lumapit sa kanya at nagtanong,"Father, magkano po ang kasal sa inyong parokya?"	once, someone approached him and asked, "father, how much is it to get married in your parish?".
<4>	Sinagot siya ng pari, "Aba, depende yan sa itsura ng mapapangasawa mo."	the priest answered her, "well, it depends on what your betrothed looks like."
<5>	At dinala ng babae sa kanya ang kanyang gwapong nobyo at sabi ng pari, "Iha, sampung libo ang kasal mo dahil sa may itsura ang nobyo mo!"	then the woman brought her handsome boyfriend and the priest said, "child, your marriage costs ten thousand because your boyfriend has good looks!".
<6>	Pagkatapos ay my nagtanong uli sa kanya, "Fadz (short for father), magkano ang kasal sa simbahan ninyo?"	afterwards, someone asked him again, "Fadz (short for father), how much is it to get married in your church?".
<7>	Sumagot uli ang pari, "Aba, depende yan sa itsura ng mapapangasawa mo!"	the priest replied again, "well, it depends on what your betrothed looks like!".
<8>	At pilit na hinila ng babae ang kanyang nahihiyang nobyo.	and the woman insisted on dragging her embarrassed boyfriend.
<9>	Tiningnan siya ng pari mula ulo hanggang paa at ang sabi, "Iha, libre na lang ang kasal mo!"	he was scanned by the priest from head to toe and said, "child, your marriage is for free!".
<10>	At pabulong na sinabi ng pari sa babae, "Bakit naman siya ang napili mong pakasalan?	and the priest said in a whisper to the woman, "why is he the one you chose to marry?"
<11>	Napakalayo ng anyo ninyo.	your appearances are so different.
<12>	'Pag nakasal kayo, siya, parang nanalo sa lotto, ikaw naman, nasunugan bahay."	"when you get married, it would seem like he won in the lottery, and you, as if your house got burned down."
<13>	At sinabi ng dalaga, "Father, minahal ko siya; hindi sa kanyang anyo kundi sa kanyang puso!	and the young lady said, "father, I loved him; not for his appearance but for his heart!"
<14>	I fell in love not with his face.	I fell in love not with his face.
<15>	I fell in love... with his heart!"	I fell in love ... with his heart!
<16>	Kung minsan nga naman ay totoo ang kasabihang "love is blind!"	indeed, sometimes the saying "love is blind" is true!
<17>	Iba kasi ang pamantayan ng mundo sa pagmamahal.	the world has a different standard in love.
<18>	Kaya nga katawa-tawa ang kanta dati ni Andrew E na "Humanap ka ng pangit, ibigin mong tunay!"	that is why the song of Andrew E before, which is "look for someone ugly and love him truly!" is so funny!
<19>	Ngunit kung ating titingnan ay ito ang ginawa ng Diyos ng ibinigay Niya ang kanyang bugtong na Anak.	but if we analyze, this is what the Lord did when he gave his only Son.
<20>	Humanap Siya ng pangit at inibig Niyang tunay.	he looked for someone ugly and loved him truly.
<21>	Naging pangit tayo dahil sa ating mga kasalanan.	we became ugly because of our sins.
<22>	Ngunit sa kabila nito tayo ay lubos niyang minahal.	but in spite of this, he loved us very much.
<23>	Sa Sulat ni Apostol San Pablo sa mga taga_Efeso ay sinabi niya: "Napakasagana ang habag ng Diyos at napakadakila ng pg-ibig na iniukol niya sa atin..."	in the letter of the apostle, Saint Paul, to the Ephesians, he said: "the mercy of the Lord is so bountiful and the love that he gave us is so great..."
<24>	Sa Ebanghelyo ay inilahad ni San Juan kung paano ito pinatunayan ng Diyos: "Gayon na lamang ang pag-ibig ng Diyos sa sanlibutan, kaya ibinigay niya ang kanyang bugtong na Anak, upang ang sumampalataya sa kaanya ay hindi mapahamak, kundi magkaroon ng buhay na walang hanggan."	in the gospel, Saint John narrated how this was proven by the Lord: "the love of the Lord for humanity is just like that, so he gave his only son, so that those who believe in him will not be harmed, but have an everlasting life."
<25>	Kaya nga't sa panahon ng Kuwaresma ay nararapat lang na maunawaan natin ang malaking pagmamahal ng Diyos sa atin at sana ay maging pamantayan din natin ito sa ating pagmamahal sa kapwa.	which is why in the time of Lent, it is only right for us to understand the great love of God for us, and hopefully, this will also be our standard in loving our neighbor.
<26>	Ang pag-ibig ng Diys ay walang itinatangi.	the love of God excludes no one.
<27>	Ang pagmamahal Niya ay walang kundisyon.	his love has no condition.
<28>	Sana, tayo rin, pagkatapos nating maranasan ang pagmamahal at pagpapatawad ng Diyos ay magawa rin natin itong maipakita sa ating kapwa.	hopefully, we too can manage to show this to our fellowmen after experiencing the love and forgiveness of God.
<29>	Ngayong panahon ng Kuwaresma sa Taon ng Mga Dukha (Year of the Poor) ay sikapin nating ipadama ang pagmamahal ng Diyos sa ating mga kapwang hikahos sa pamumuhay.	during this season of Lent in the Year of the Poor (Year of the Poor), let us strive to make the love of God felt by our neighbors, who are struggling in life.
<30>	Makisa tayo sa kanilang paghihirap bilang pakikiisa sa paghihirap na dinanas ni Kristo.	let us be one with them in poverty, as an act of unity with the suffering experienced by Christ.
<31>	Matuto tayong umunawa, magpatawad at magmahal ng walang kundisyon o hinahanap na kapalit.	let us learn to understand, forgive and love without condition or looking for something in exchange.
<32>	Katulad ni Hesus, ibigin natin hindi lang ang mga kaibig-ibig.	like Jesus, let us love not only those who are lovely.
<33>	Hanapin natin ang mga "pangit at ibigin nating tunay!"	let us look for the "ugly and love them truly!".
