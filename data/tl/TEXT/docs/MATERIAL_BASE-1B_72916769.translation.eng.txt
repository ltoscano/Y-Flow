<1>	Zambales fishermen namamangka na sa WPS By Jojo Perez September 3, 2016Ikinatuwa ng mga mangingisda sa lalawigan ng Zambales ang gina­wang pagpayag ng bansang China upang sila ay makapangisda na sa Scarborough Shoal sa West Philippine Sea (WPS).	Zambales fishermen now sail in WPS by Jojo Perez September 3, 2016 fishermen of Zambales city rejoiced China's permission for them to fish in Scarborough Shoal in West Philippine Sea (WPS).
<2>	Ang pagpayag umano ng bansang China na muling pumalaot sa Pana­tag Shoal ang mga mangi­ngisda ay resulta ng ginawang pakikipagpulong ni Pangulong Duterte kay Chinese Ambassador Zhao Jianhua kahapon.	the approval of China for fishermen to sail in Panatag Shoal is the result of President Duterte's meeting with Chinese Ambassador Zhao Jianhua yesterday.
<3>	Sa panayam ng Abante sa mangingisdang si Ruben Correa na taga Masinloc, Zambales, matapos umano ang matagal na panahong pagbabawal sa kanila na makakuha ng ikinabubuhay sa dagat sa bahagi ng WPS ay lubos ang kanilang pasasalamat kay Pangulong Rodrigo Duterte.	in the interview of Abante with fisherman, Ruben Correa of Masinloc, Zambales, he said that after a long time of prohibiting them to continue their livelihood in WPS, they're very grateful to President Rodrigo Duterte.
<4>	“Ilang taon din kami nakikipaghabulan at nagtatago para makapangisda sa Panatag (Shoal) kaya lang ayaw namin sumugal noon kasi natatakot kami sa coast guard ng China,” ayon pa kay Correa.	"it has also been a couple of years of tiptoeing just to fish in Panatag (Shoal), although we didn't want to gamble before because we were afraid of the coast guard of China," according to Correa.
