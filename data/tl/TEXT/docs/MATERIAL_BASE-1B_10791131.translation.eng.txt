<3>	CALAPAN, Oriental Mindoro, Hulyo 29 (PIA) â€“ Bilang pasasalamat at pagpupugay sa kanilang naging panunungkulan bilang mga konsehal ng bayan, pinarangalan ang 31 mga 3-termer ng Sangguniang Bayan sa Oriental Mindoro sa isinagawang Philippine Councilors League-Oriental Mindoro Federation (PCL-OMF) End-Term and Recognition Activities 2016 sa Batasang Panlalawigan, Lungsod ng Calapan kamakailan.	Calapan, Oriental Mindoro, July 29 (PIA) - to thank and honor their service as municipal councilors, 31 3-termers of the municipal council of Oriental Mindoro were awarded in the Philippine Councilors League-Oriental Mindoro Federation (PCL-OMF) End-Term and Recognition Activities 2016 held at the Provincial Assembly, City of Calapan recently.
<4>	Pinangunahan ang gawain ni PCL-OMF President at Ex-Officio Member ng Sangguniang Panlalawigan (SP), Board Member Gideon B. Abuel.	the activity was headed by PCL-OMF president and ex-officio member of the provincial council (SP), Board Member Gideon B. Abuel.
<5>	Tumutok sa temang "Integrity and Social Responsibility: Commitment of the Councilors to its constituents in the Province of Oriental Mindoro", tumanggap ang mga natatanging konsehal ng bayan ng mga plake ng pagkilala at ng kani-kanilang sipi ng kapasiyahang naipasa ng SP na nagbibigay-pugay sa kanilang mga naiambag sa larangan ng lokal na lehislatura ng lalawigan.	focusing on the theme "integrity and social responsibility: commitment of the councilors to its constituents in the province of Oriental Mindoro", the outstanding councilors of the town received plaques of recognition and their own copies of the resolution passed by the SP recognizing them for their contributions to the field of local legislature in the province.
<6>	Naging panauhing pandangal sa gawain si Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) Provincial Director Ma. Victoria J. Del Rosario.	Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) Provincial Director Maria Victoria J. Del Rosario became the guest of honor of the event.
<7>	Binigyang-diin ni Del rosario sa kanyang talumpati ang pagbibigay-parangal sa mga opisyal ng bayan.	in her speech Del Rosario emphasized giving honors to the municipal officials.
<8>	Gayundin, hinikayat niya ang mga opisyal na manguna sa pagtataguyod sa mga lokal na pamahalaan at lehislatura na tumutugon sa mga tunay na pangangailangan ng kani-kanilang nasasakupan.	also, she encouraged the officials to lead in supporting the local government and legislature to answer to the true needs of their constituents.
<9>	Mula sa mga konsehal na pinarangalan, kinilala rin ang mga nahalal sa mas mataas na posisyon sa kani- kanilang mga bayan.	from the councilors honored, those in high-ranking positions in their own towns were also recognized.
