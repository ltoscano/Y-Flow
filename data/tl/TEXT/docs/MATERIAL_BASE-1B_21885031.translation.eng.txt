<1>	120 magsasaka ng Sablayan, OccMin sumailalim sa pagsasanay	120 farmers of Sablayan, OccMin undergo training
<2>	February 16, 2016 Voltaire N. Dequina	February 16, 2016 Voltaire N. Dequina
<3>	SABLAYAN, Occidental Mindoro, Peb 16 (PIA)—Sumailalaim sa Agricultral Technology Training (ATT) ang 120 mga magsasaka mula sa apat na barangay ng bayan ng Sablayan kamakailan.	Sablayan, Occidental Mindoro, February 16 (PIA) - 120 farmers from four barangays of Sablayan recently underwent the Agricultural Technology Training (ATT).
<4>	Ang mga dumalo, na nagmula sa barangay Ibud, Sto. Niño, San Francisco at San Vicente, ay tinuruan ng makabagong teknolohiya at paraan ng pagsasaka, kabilang ang rice at corn production, animal livestock at poultry pati na rin sa pangisdaan.	the attendees from Barangay Ibud, Sto. Niño, San Francisco and San Vicente, were taught modern technology and methods of farming, including rice and corn production, animal livestock and poultry as well as fishery.
<5>	Ayon kay Sofronio Baranda, Assistant Provincial Agriculturist (APA), pinangunahan ng mga magaaral mula sa Occidental Mindoro State College (OMSC) ang naturang pagsasanay.	according to Sofronio Baranda, Assistant Provincial Agriculturist (APA), students from the Occidental Mindoro State College (OMSC) led the training.
<6>	“Mga magsisipagtapos ng kursong Bachelor of Agricultural Technology ang mga batang ito, at may kakayahan na magturo sa ating mga magsasaka”, paliwanag ni Baranda.	"these students are graduating from Bachelor of Agricultural Technology course and they have the ability to teach our farmers," explained Baranda.
<7>	Hindi lamang nagbigay ng kapakinabanag ang programang ATT sa mga dumalong magsasaka, nakakuha rin ng karanasan sa pagtuturo ang mga maaaral.	the ATT program was not only useful to the farmers who attended, but also to the students who gained a teaching experience.
