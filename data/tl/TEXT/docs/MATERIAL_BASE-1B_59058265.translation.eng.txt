CBCP will open chaplaincy in Jordan by Noel Abuel August 16, 2016 the Catholic Bishops' Conference of the Philippines (CBCP) Episcopal Commission on Migrants and Itinerant People will open a chaplaincy in the Middle East particularly in Amman Jordan this year.
<2>	Ayon kay Balanga Bataan Bishop Ruperto Santos, chairman ng komisyon, ito ang resulta nang pakikipagpulong nito kay Bishop Paul Hinder, ang Vicar Apostolic sa Apostolic Vicariate of Southern Arabia noong nakaraang taon kaugnay na rin sa panawagan ng maraming Filipino na dagdagan ang mga paring Filipino doon.	according to Balanga Bataan Bishop Ruperto Santos, chairman of the commission, this is the result of their meeting with Bishop Paul Hinder, the Vicar Apostolic of the Apostolic Vicariate of Southern Arabia last year related to the plea of many Filipinos to add Filipino priests there.
