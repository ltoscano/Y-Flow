
Public Works and weekend, By Eralyn Pritil, 10, 2016.
The Road will continue to repair and improvement of the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) this weekend, so expect the busy on the streets.
According to Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA), around 10: 00 in the evening will be started at around 10: 00 in the evening and will be opened at five o'clock in the morning on Monday (June 13).
Particularly the stretch of Mindanao Avenue from Road 8 to Alley 14, 2nd lane from the island, because of this, MMDA advised motorists, to avoid affected areas and use an alternative route.
