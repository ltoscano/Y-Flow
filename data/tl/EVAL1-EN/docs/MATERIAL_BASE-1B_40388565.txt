
Koronadal LGUs, reminded the public to take care of the fire.

March 4, 2016.

Koronadal City, South Cotabato, Mar 4 (Pia) - Mar 4 (Pia) - when the Fire Protection was started, the city government of Koronadal has started to remind the citizens of the city to be careful to avoid fire.

According to Koronadal City Mayor Peter Miguel, the mayor should be prepared and be alert for the possible threats of fire especially during rainy season, but according to the data of fire in the city, it can be avoided that there will be a cause of fire in the city of Fire Protection (BFP) to avoid the investigation of the fire in the city.
