Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, July 4 (Pia) - Governor Carlos Padilla is supported by President Rodrigo Duterte about his first meeting with the employees of the provincial government, he said that he will follow the leadership of President Duterte for the first meeting with the employees of the provincial government, and also said that he will be recognized by the leaders of the provincial government and the province will still meet.

Governor Padilla also appealed to the members of every branch and department of the provincial government to dismiss the work of officials and employees of (ALM / BME / Pia 2, Nueva Vizcaya.
