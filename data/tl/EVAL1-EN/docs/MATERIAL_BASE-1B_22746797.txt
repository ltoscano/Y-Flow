
City of San Jose, being thankful is the projects received from the DTI.

February 13, 2016 Camille C. P.

Cabanatuan City, Pigcawayan, 2 (Pia) - the city government of San Jose is being thankful for the projects given by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) for those who are starting to show up in the construction of the New Generation of the city, which can be helpful to show their own products made by the Small ng mga Bayani in the city.

DTI Provincial Director Brillante Mendoza stated that the office of the local government will remain temporary if after the implementation of the BuB, they should not be ashamed to go near the space of the office while the DTI is the one who will shoulder the space and personnel.
