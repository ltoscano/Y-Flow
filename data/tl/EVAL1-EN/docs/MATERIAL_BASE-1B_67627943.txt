The Chicago-elect Ruffy Biazon will be temporarily freed after failing to collect payments from the three cases he is facing in the Sandiganbayan related to the pork barrel scam.
Yesterday, the former Commissioner of Customs (BOC) personally went to the Sandali, 7th Division for his temporary freedom.
Biazon will be placed under 20,000 pesos to bail for the graft case given to bail, 30,000 pesos for the graft case of the Ombudsman, Janet Lim-Napoles and others in relation to the involvement of them on the pork barrel scam.
