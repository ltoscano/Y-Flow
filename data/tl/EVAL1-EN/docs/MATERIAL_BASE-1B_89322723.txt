
MMDA gave warning to those who are playing "Pokemon."

July 11, 2016.

Quezon City, July 11 (Pia) - the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) gave a warning to those who are playing "Pokemon Go" to be careful.

The MMDA advised, to play "Pokemon Go" while crossing the road, and do not forget to look back to the left road, but be alert at the right side of July. (MMDA / RB / Pia-NCR).
