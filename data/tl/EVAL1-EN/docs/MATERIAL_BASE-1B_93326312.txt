More than 300 senior citizens in Palawan accepted the local pension.

July 11, 2016.

Puerto Princesa, Palawan, July 11 (Pia) - more than 100 senior citizens in Palawan was received by their local government of Palawan which is estimated to be the highest pension increase of the provincial government for the year 2015, which is currently being paid by the local government of Palawan which is based on the Saligang Batas of the entire province, and according to the National Social Welfare and Development Office (PSWDO), they are receiving additional pension from the local government of Palawan which is currently being paid by the local government of Palawan.
