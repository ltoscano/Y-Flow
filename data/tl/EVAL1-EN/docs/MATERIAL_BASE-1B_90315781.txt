New city hall in Lucena, will be finished.

July 08, 2016 Ruel Oryang.

Lucena City, July 8 (Pia) - it is close to the incoming new and big city hall in the city that can be found in the town of the city, according to Mayor Roderick Alcala, the project of the new city hall will be found on January 15, 2015 after the legislative chamber.

The construction is already in A Phase 4 and is expected to be finished this October.
