Lake Sebu was identified as Labor Law or complicated.

June 17, 2016.

City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, June 17 (Pia) - the Department of Labor Law (DOLE) - the city of Lake Sebu is recognized as the first "Labor Law Compliance (LRP), according to Social Security System (LRTMC) which is scheduled to be implemented by the Labor laws Compliance.

DOLE 12 is going to build the Labor Law or complicated aspect of Lake Sebu at the entrance of said town (Dilawan, Pia 12).
