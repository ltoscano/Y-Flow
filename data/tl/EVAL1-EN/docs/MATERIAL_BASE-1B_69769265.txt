Video: Million of March against pork barrel, from Luneta until Mendiola.

By Filipino Weeker.
August 28, 2013.

It is estimated that hundreds of thousands of Filipinos joined the million New Year against the pork barrel last August 26, Independence Day, in Rizal Park.
From Luneta, thousands have marched towards Mendiola to be held accountable for President Benigno Aquino III for the billions of peso pork barrel scam involved by the senators and declaration of his own pork barrel, including his own pork barrel that reached up to P1 million MT.
