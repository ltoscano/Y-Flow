The public is warned against dengue.

There is 26, 2016.

City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, May 26 (Pia) - Provincial Health Office Doctor Rogelio AOIL) is reminding the public to always clean the cases of dengue, especially now that rainy season will continue to increase, especially this rainy season.

It is noticeable that whatever the campaign will be done by Integrated Provincial Health Office about dengue, if the residents and their bodies will not take action, there is a higher number of dengue in the entire South Cotabato, there will be more than 1,149 in the city of Koronadal, 1 in the town of Banga, 1 in the town of Surallonza-2 in the town of Surallero and Tupi.
