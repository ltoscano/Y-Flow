FWD Life at PBA nagsanib-puwersa
June 14, 2016
Lumagda ang sumisikat ngayong insurance firm sa bansa na FWD Life Philippines ng tatlong taong kontrata sa Philippine Basketball Association para mahimok ang Filipino cagers na ibigay-todo lahat at manalo lalo’t naka-insured na sila kaya walang dapat alalahanin.
Ang FWDLP ang official insurer ng Asia’s first pay-for-play league sa taong ito hanggang 2019 at inaasahan na ang pagtatambal ay magpapabago ng takbo sa local insurance sector para suportahan din ang mga Pinoy sa kinahihilgan at pamumuhay ng mga ito, gaya sa basketball.
“We are delighted to be partnering with PBA, a prestigious Filipino sporting organization” bulalas kahapon ni FWDLP president/CEO Peter Grimes. “At FWD, we have always embraced sports, a passion for many of our customers. Joining hands with the PBA is important for us as we want to show our support and encourage our home-grown Filipino athletes to perform to their full potential.” Kinasaya naman ng liga ang pagkakapit-kamay sa kumpanya na may malapit aniya sa sports, lalo na sa basketbol.
“It is with great pride that we welcome FWD to our growing PBA family. We’re looking to strengthen our ties with FWD in the coming years,” komento naman ni PBA commissioner Andres ‘Chito’ Narvasa, Jr. “Because of this collaboration, we feel protected knowing that we have partnered with a trusted insurance brand that urges Filipinos to pursue their passions and live life without hesitation.”
Nagpahayag naman ng excitement si Grimes hinggil sa partnership sa PBA.
“We’re genuinely excited about rolling out our sports-related programs, particularly together with the PBA, which will help position our brand closer to more Filipinos,” ani Grimes. “We have always been proud in sharing our vision of changing the way people feel about insurance, and this partnership with the PBA will help reinforce this.”
