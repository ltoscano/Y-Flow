Wozniacki sumurender sa Citi Open By AP July 22, 2016
WASHINGTON — Nag-retire si dating No. 1 Caroline Wozniacki sa kasagsagan ng kanyang second-round match sa Citi Open laban kay Sam Stosur.
Hindi nakayanan ni right-handed Wozniacki ang pananakit ng kaliwang braso kaya sumurender na.
Naramdaman daw niya ang sakit nang pumalo ng two-handed backhand sa 5-all ng first set ng match nilang nag-umpisa Miyerkules ng gabi at natapos ng hatinggabi, pa-Huwebes na ng madaling-araw. Binigyan pa siya ng treatment ng trainer at naipanalo ang match 7-5, pero tumigil nang ma-break at maiwan 4-3 sa second.
“There was a point where I hit a backhand and I just felt it, and I was like ‘Oh, it’s nothing,’” ani Wozniacki. “But then I hit another backhand and I was like, ‘OK, it’s something.’ And it just got worse.”
US Open runner-up si Wozniacki noong 2009 at 2014.
Ngayong taon, wala pa siyang naipanalong Grand Slam match. Nasilat siya sa first round sa Australian Open at Wimbledon, lumiban sa French Open dahil sa injured ankle.
