Takbo Kabitenyo, UE bets wagi sa RU 2 By Ramil Cruz June 6, 2016
Naitakbo ang top honors sa 32K nina Unilever worker Jeffrey Orolfo Galicio at University of the East-Recto student Cellie Rose Jaro sa may 13,000 lumahok kahapon ng umaga na 5th Run United 2 2016 paalis at pabalik sa SM MOA grounds sa Pasay City.
May 2 hrs., 21 mins. at 59 secs. clocking ang 33-year-old ultra-marathoner na fifth placer sa half-marathon ng RU 1 noong Marso sa nasabi ring venue at Takbo Kabitenyo, Inc. bet na si Galicio sa pagprimera sa men’s division at paglagak sa segunda-tersera na lang kina Andy Toniacao (2:28:00) at 2012 London Paralympian Isidro Vildosola (2:32:19).
Naka-2:36:11 ang 21-year-old incoming junior student na si Jaro sa pamamayagpag sa women’s side laban kina Silanie Apolistar-Gutang (2:46:18) at Rosalyn Russell (3:01:12). Top three male and female winners sa 21k sina Jojie Daga-as (1:22:09), gardener Carlito Fantilaga (1:24:07) at Renato Zialcita, Jr. (1:30:47), at sina Emilaine Balatbat (1:53:24), Myzel Alcala (2:00:12) at Richell Jane Santos (2:01:44).
Humati rin sa limelight ang top 3 sa 10k na sina Joey delos Reyes (35:35), Dante Cagas (36:56) at Sam Noveros (39:31), at sina veteran internationalist Cinderella Agana-Lorenzo (44:16), Rayya Cuven Abellar (47:42) at Lauren Lim (49:35).
Gayundin ang 1-2-3 sa 5k na sina Christopher Iblan (17:27), Ralph Edison Tan (20:03) at Jan Rimwell Lopez (20:11), at sina Rishane Ashira Abellar (22:39), Annabelle Daniels (22:42) at Ashley May Oro (22:56).
