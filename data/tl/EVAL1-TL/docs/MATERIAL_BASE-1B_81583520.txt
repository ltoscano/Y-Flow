NBA player Mills magbibitbit ng bodyguard sa Rio Olympics June 25, 2016
AUSTRALIA — Plano ni Australia’s NBA player Patty Mills na mag-hire ng personal security sa 31st Summer Olympic Games 2016 sa Agosto 5-21 para matiyak ang kaligtasan ng kanyang pamilya makalipas holdapin at tutukan ng baril ang Paralympic sailor at team official ng kanyang bansa sa host city.
Binunyag nina Paralympic sailor Liesl Tesch at physiotherapist Sarah Ross na nagba-bike sila sa isang parke na malapit lang sa kanilang hotel sa Flamengo Beach noong Linggo nang dalawang lalaki, isa ang armado ang kumompronta sa kanila.
Inihayag naman ni Australia’s chef de mission Kitty Chiller na ang insidente’y “light warning” at siya’y susulat sa Rio organizing committee at mayor para magsiaksyon. “Obviously you can’t ignore that stuff.

We have got some stuff together with our family to make sure they are looked after,” wika sa News Corp. ni Mills, na naglalaro sa San Antonio Spurs sa NBA.
“It is an Olympics and you want to make sure you share that experience with your family and friends as much as you can. Definitely I am putting some focus into that to make sure they are looked after and healthy.”
