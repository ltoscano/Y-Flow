VOLLEYBALL MANDATORY SPORT NA NG NCAA By Elech Dawa June 7, 2016 Simula ngayong taon isa sa apat na mandatory sports na ng NCAA ang volleyball.
Hinayag ni NCAA Management Committee chairman Jose Mari Lacson ng Season 92 host San Beda na opisyal ng ina-probahan ng board ang volleyball bilang fourth sport branded mandatory kasama ang basketball, swimming at centerpiece athletics.
“It’s approved,” saad ni Lacson. Nabuo ang desisyon dahil sa kasikatan ng volleyball sa bansa. “The NCAA’s goal is increase participation not just in a few sports but all and the NCAA plans to make it all mandatory sports in the future,” pahayag ni Mancom member Peter Cayco ng Arellano U.
“The NCAA started with volleyball first because all schools are already active when it comes to volleyball,” dagdag ni Cayco.
Samantala, sa pagbubukas ng Season 92 sa Hunyo 25 sa Mall of Asia Arena, salpukan agad ang Mapua at Jose Rizal sa alas-dos ng hapon habang katapat ng defending champion Letran ang last year’s runner-up San Beda main game sa 4 p.m.
Magiging bongga ang opening number sa pangunguna ni Roxanne Lapus at direksyon ni Douglas Nierras ayon sa organizers.
