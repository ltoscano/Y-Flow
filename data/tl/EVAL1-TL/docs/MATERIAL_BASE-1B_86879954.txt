Tuloy ang protesta! — Bongbong
By Bernard Taguinod June 12, 2016 Philippine Senator Ferdinand Marcos Jnr., the son of late dictator Ferdinand Marcos, gestures during a press conference in Manila on June 11, 2016.

Ferdinand Marcos's son threatened legal action to reverse his narrow defeat in the recent Philippine elections, which analysts said had been the family of the late dictator's best chance to regain the presidency. / AFP PHOTO Ikinakasa na ng kampo ni Sen.

Ferdinand ‘Bongbong’ Marcos Jr., ang paghahain ng election protest bago matapos ang buwan. Hunyo 28, 2016 ang target date.
Ito ang inamin ni Marcos matapos makipagkita noong Biyernes ng gabi kay President-elect Rodrigo Duterte sa Davao City.
“As far as the merits go, I thinks it’s very very clear that (there are) many many violation of Smartmatic and by Comelec,” pahayag ni Marcos kaya maghahain ito ang election protest. Hindi matanggap ni Marcos ang umano’y ginawang pambabastos at pagbalewala sa boses ng taumbayan noong nakaraang eleksyon dahil naniniwala itong siya talaga ang inihalal ng tao bilang bise presidente.
Si Marcos ay tinalo ni Vice President-elect Leni Robredo matapos malamangan ng 263,473 votes.
