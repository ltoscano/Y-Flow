<1>	Simeone ataka kulipa kisasi cha 2013/14	Simeone wants revenge for 2013/14.
<2>	Madrid, Hispania.	Madrid, Spain.
<3>	Ndoto ya kocha wa Atletico Madrid, Diego Simeone ni pamoja na kulipa kisasi cha fainali ya msimu wa 2013/14 pamoja na kutwaa taji la Ligi ya Mabingwa Ulaya.	Atletico Madrid coach, Diego Simeone's, dream is to get revenge for the 2013/14 season finals and scoop up the award for the European Championships League.
<4>	Simeone, aliyeiongoza Atletico juzi kuiondoa Bayern Munich kwa faida ya bao la ugenini, anaaingalia kwa jicho la pili fainali hiyo ya jijini Milan, Italia, Mei 28.	Simeone, who led Atletico to remove Bayern Munich the other day by benefiting from an away goal, has his eye on the finals in Milan City, Italy, May 28th.
<5>	Atletico itaikabili Real Madrid kwenye fainali ambayo itakumbusha usiku wa jijini Lisbon, Ureno ambako Real Madrid iliipokonya tonge klabu hiyo kwa mabao ya muda wa nyongeza.	Atletico will face Real Madrid in the finals which will be reminiscent of the night in Lisbon, Portugal, where Real Madrid snatched a win for the club in extra time.
<6>	Simeone, raia wa Argentina alieleza faraja yake kukutana na vijana wa Zinedine Zidane, ambao waliiondoa Manchester City kwa bao 1-0 jijini Madrid juzi usiku.	Simeone, an Argentinian citizen, explained his comfort of meeting with Zinedine Zidane's boys, who knocked out Manchester City by 1-0 goals in Madrid the night before yesterday.
<7>	“Nina furaha,” alisema baada ya kuambiwa kuwa watakutana na majirani zao wa Madrid.	"I am happy," he said, after being told that they will meet with their neighbors from Madrid.
