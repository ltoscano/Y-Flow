
Full House Committee versus Metro Manila By Eralyn Pritil, August 2, 2016.
Different agencies of the government are now in their force to implement concrete steps so that the problem with traffic will finally be solved.
This is according to Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) Traffic Engineering Center Director Noguavio.
According to Recio, the government is allegedly serious and is determined now that within six-years there will be good to happen in transportation particularly the response to traffic problem.
MMDA hopes that within six-years, there are good things to happen in transportation.
So now, the discussion of other agencies of the government is said to be almost everyday, which is included in the Department of Transportation and Communication (DOTC); Land Transportation Office (LTO), Land Transportation Office (LTO), Highway Patrol Group (HPG) and MMDA).
