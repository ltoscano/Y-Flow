VP Leni, on the SONA By Bernard Taguba, July 26, 2016.
Vice President Leni Robredo is contented and happy in the State of the Nation Address (SONA) by President Rodrigo Duterte because he allegedly put in detail of the direction of our government, "what happened to the HUDCC, he said in his direction," said Robredo.
In his speech that reached more than half an hour, Duterte stressed that they will not allow the houses of the Inbiological family if they will not be able to leave this problem in order for the people to stay in illegal drugs and ensure the peace and order in the country.
"I am happy there.

"We will be happy that the campaign against it will be happy, and he said that the campaign will be exported," that's what should be, "according to the Vice President.
