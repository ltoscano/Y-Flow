DOH-Trillanes IV warned against dengue this rainy season.

July 21, 2016.

Cotabato CITY, July 21 (Pia) - the Department of Health (DOH-AMM) once again reminded the public to avoid the increase of dengue in the first three months, based on the report of dengue, and even in schools and community.

The symptoms or signs of dengue are high and continued to have a high fever which contains two to seven days; redness and vomiting of the skin which is usually increasing during rainy season (Oka, hepatitis A, and diseases of skin which are usually increasing during rainy season.
