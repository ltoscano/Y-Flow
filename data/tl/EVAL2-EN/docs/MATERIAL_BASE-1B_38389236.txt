SONA 2014: "Aquino, he should be fired!

Monday, July 28, 2014 - armed men who were calling for President Noynoy Aquino in his position were blocked by President Noynoy Aquino, on the fifth SONA (State of the Nation Address), that's why they decided to have a program for a kilometer before yesterday in Commonwealth Avenue.

Brought by a big der, which is a wheel that has a call "Aquino, oust them!

Covetous and Eladversary!

Together with the programs were the groups under the Islamic Liberation Front (FDC) and Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PPHP).

The SONA is during the fourth Monday of July.
