Health official in South Cotabato, in favor of the total ban of fireworks.

January 07, 2016 city of Koronadal, South Cotabato, January 7 (Pia) - the members of the Integrated Provincial Health Office of South Cotabato said that there are still many who have been victimized by fireworks, 8 percent of the K-2-year-old, due to fireworks that were recorded dead due to fireworks that were recorded to have been victims of firecrackers this past Christmas and New Year.
