1M was able to enroll to Senior High School in Angeles June 11, 2016.
Around 1 million grade 11 students were able to enroll for public and private schools in the whole country, this is the report of the Department of Education (DepEd) along with the opening of classes on June 13.
According to (DepEd) Secretary Arlus said, this is the opposite of the poor people who are going to study in grade 11 in the year 2016-2017 by the said data of enrollees.
As of now, half of more than eight public and the Brigada Eskwela will be submitted by Senior High School which reaches up to 432,000 students, DepEd is expected to have 1.5 million students who are going to grade 11 this coming June 13.
