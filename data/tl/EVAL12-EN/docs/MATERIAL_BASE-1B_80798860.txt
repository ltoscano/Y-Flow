The construction of the new City Hall in Malolos, started.

June 27, 2016 Shane F. Velasco.

City of Malolos, June 27 (Pia) - the construction of the new City Hall of Malolos City, according to Mayor Christian Natividad, is now in the midst of a foundation of the five-hectare land which was held in MacArthur Highway in Barangay Buliran, at the height of 1.8 million pesos.
