The church raised.

Joy Cantos (Filipino Star Ngayon), PO 2 44; UPP-March 1, 2015 - 12: 00am ed.12: 00.

Manila, Philippines - the suspected members of the bandits Abu Sayyaf Group (ASG) were arrested by a bomb in the spread of terrorism in Lamitan City, Basilan two nights ago.
In the report of Joint Task Group Basilan Commander Superintendent Rolando Espinosa, it was around 9: 25 in the evening when the explosion happened in Cathedral Church and Central School in Poblacion in the city.
The bombing happened in the middle of the all out offensive offensive by the military troops to protect the bandits in the province.
No one was reported injured in the explosion but it created a severe fear in the ranks of the catholic devotees.
