606 hectares of corn and rice were devastated by the rat.

January 14, 2016.

Kabacan, North Cotabato, January 14 (Pia) - up to 606 hectares of corn and rice here in the town were devastated by the rats from the report of Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (David Saure, nine barangays in the town continued to be affected by the attacks of rats.

After a few days that it is not included in the affected areas, the state of calamity has been declared recently in Kabacan, according to Saure, purpose of the declaration to give funds to the affected farmers and the families of the affected farmers and their families.
