Bato: I am ready to resign!
I am ready to resign.
This is the hard time yesterday by the Philippine National Police (PNP) chief Director General Ronald dela Rosa in front of the police during his visit to the office of the police in Isabela and Cagayan before going directly to Ilocos Norte.
According to dela Rosa, he is ready to resign from his position if he failed in the campaign against illegal drugs in the country, after this statement, dela Rosa encouraged the provincial police to take the campaign of the PNP against illegal drugs and crime in their jurisdiction.
Dela Rosa warned those who are going to resign from their duties if they are involved in illegal drug trade.
