Helping the indigenous people to return to Kidapawan City.

August 25, 2016.

City of Kidapawan, North Cotabato, August 25 (Pia) - up to the city will be able to support the indigenous people here in the city.

This is after city mayor Joseph Evangelista assured that the indigenous people will be given a help in the field and livelihood.

Evangelista insisted, the priority of the city government to help the victims (IP) through the help given by the Department of Agriculture (DA) recently.

It was determined that the local government received more than 6 million farms to receive from the DA.

Aside from this, the mayor will also focus on helping the children IP that he wants, that at the young age, the youth will be able to show their culture and identity.
