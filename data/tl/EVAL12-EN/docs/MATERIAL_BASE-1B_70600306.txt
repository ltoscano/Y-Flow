Free rides in LRT and MRT this Independence Day.

Mer Layson (Filipino Star Ngayon), Nunber 1255; UPP-12, 2014 - 12: 00am editing.

Manila, Philippines - the Light Rail Transit (LRT) and Metro Rail Transit (MRT) and Metro Rail Transit (MRT 3) will give free passengers on the Independence Day, June 12.
According to Atty. Hernaez or Cabrera, spokesperson of LRT Authority (LRTA) and of MRT 3, this is as the same in the celebration of Independence Day.
It was found out that the free rides in LRT 1 and 2 and MRT 3 can be released from 7: 00 in the morning and from 9: 00 in the morning and from 5: 00 in the afternoon and from 5: 00 in the afternoon until 7: 00 in the afternoon and from 5: 00 in the afternoon.
Meanwhile, the MRT 3 is traveling in the stretch of Eve io delos Santos Avenue (EDSA) from North Avenue in Quezon City until Taft Avenue in Pasay City.
