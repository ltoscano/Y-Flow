Protest by the farmers in Koronadal, finished.

April 26, 2016 city Koronadal, South Cotabato, April 27 (Pia) -- the protesters were able to leave the area in exchange of rice to their ranks, including the DSWD office, including those who were first left in the dungeon and they went home to their barangay.

They were also given 2,000 pesos and 500 boxes of the DSWD 12, accompanied by some employees of the DSWD-12, according to them, the distribution of DSWD 12 to the farmers destructed by the drought in relation to the local government.
