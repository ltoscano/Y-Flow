Bulacan Jail is negative of drugs By Jun Borbe an example of September 25, 2016.
The Oplan Galugad conducted by the Provincial Public Safety Company (PPSC) of Bulacan Police Provincial Office (BPPO) in Bulacan Provincial Jail (Bastes) in capitol compound in Malolos City was negative of drugs.
P / Supt. Frankie Arelutat, Russian Commander of Bulacan PPSC is headed to the city jail that there are more than 3,000 prisoners who were successful because of some cellphone and gadgets were recovered and negative of drugs.
The operation of Bulacan PNP in the Provincial Jail is part of the severe campaign of the police to ensure that it is clean in any kind of illegal drugs and illegal contraband.
