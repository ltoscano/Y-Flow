The city of Tarlac, January 20 (Pia) -- the 61 students in the city of Tarlac have already obtained the beneficiary of the Special Program for global warming or PPES.

According to DOLE Provincial Director Efren Reyes, they received 400 hundred pesos from them from working in the city government within 20 days after the remaining 60 percent is from their employers, company or local government.
