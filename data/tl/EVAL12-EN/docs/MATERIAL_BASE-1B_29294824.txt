Oplan Rody Duterte, against the drug programs rushed by BJMP to Puerto Princesa.

September 29, 2016 Leila B. Dom.

Puerto Princesa, Palawan, September 4 (Pia), conducted a "Oplan Galugad conducted in jail (Oplan Salas)" the Bureau of Jail Management and Penterm (BJMP) Puerto Princesa.

Through this, the discipline will be fully implemented and ensure that these will be controlled by the management of the prison.

In Oplan Rody Duterte, to verified, the prisoners are already separated dormitories to prisoners, based on the case they are facing.

It is the priority of the BJMP to be imprisoned for violation of Republic Acting-9165 (Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002) so that the programs for them will be easily fulfilled.

BJMP Puerto Princesa City Warden, Jail Chief Inspector Lino Soriano led the said initiative to response to President Rodrigo Duterte's mandate which is being implemented by the BJMP that's being removed from the BJMP.

Meanwhile, the programs of the City Jail for prisoners like the Islamic State Warriors are now in thorough program who have the goal to help each other in a positive attitude, as well as a regular sports.

The anti-drug strategy is already being implemented on the campaign and medical program to focus the reforms for prisoners.
