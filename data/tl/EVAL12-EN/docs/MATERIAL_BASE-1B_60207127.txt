The plunder case of Gloria Arroyo will decide on the case of former President Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo to show his decision on the case of former President Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo who will be facing the plunder case of the Sandiganbayan in relation to the filing of the Supreme Court.
The said petition of Arroyo is asking to dismiss the case against him because of his mother-known evidence, the Supreme Court (SC) will be heard today on the implementation of the Supreme Court (SC) today.
It can be recalled that because of the petition of Arroyo, the SC filed an intensive order against the Sandiganbayan wherein he stopped the hearing on the case while a few magistrates responded in favor of the case of Arroyo when he was not going to pass through an entrapment operation.
