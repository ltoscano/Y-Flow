March 1, 2016.

City of Koronadal, South Cotabato, March 1 (Pia) - starting this year, it will lower to the barangays to know the projects that will be funded under the Bailable Program of the national government.

This means, according to DILG-South Cotabato OPLAN), the people and the leaders of the barangay will decide on what is the project that they should be implemented in Barangay Assembly Day on the 25th of March, there will be better for the national government to implement the proper projects in Barangay Baay Day on the 25th of March.
