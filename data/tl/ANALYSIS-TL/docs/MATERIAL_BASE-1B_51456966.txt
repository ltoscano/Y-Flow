Duterte naki-join sa Feast of Ramadan By Dindo Matining July 7, 2016 Nakiisa si Pangulong Rodrigo Duterte sa mga Pilipinong Muslim sa pagdiriwang ng Feast of Ramadan.
“I extend my warm greetings and felicitations to the members of the Muslim Ummah (communities), as they celebrate today Eid’l Fitr or the culmination of the month-long observance of fasting for the Holy Month of Ramadan,” sabi ni Duterte sa isang statement.
Aniya, ‘timely’ at ‘relevant’ diumano ang pagdiriwang ng Ramadan sa taong ito lalo na’t nagsisimula ang bansa sa ilalim ng bagong administrasyong Duterte. “This year’s celebration is indeed timely and relevant, as the Filipino nation starts a fresh move towards peace, stability, development, and progress under a new leadership,” sabi ni Duterte.
“Definitely, we view the new administration with high hope and sustained enthusiasm, that, with the support and cooperation of every Filipino, it will propel our country to greater heights,” dagdag pa nito.
Umaasa rin ang Pangulo na ang ‘sense of discipline’ na natutunan sa banal na buwan ng Ramadan ay magiging daan ng pagkakaisa patungo sa tunay na pagbabago ng bansa.
