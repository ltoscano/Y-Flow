4 teams maghahasa para sa next season By Elech Dawa SSeptember 22, 2016
Tigok na sa Final Four ang Jose Rizal U, San Sebastian, Letran at Emilio Aguinaldo College pero target pa rin nilang manalo sa kanilang huling laro ngayong araw sa 92nd NCAA basketball tournament sa The Arena sa San Juan. Maghaharap ang Heavy Bombers at Stags sa unang laro, barugan ang Knights at Generals sa main game.
Nasa fifth place ang JRU (9-8), puwesto naman ang titleholder Letran (8-9) sa No. 6, nasa ibaba ang San Sebastian (7-10) at EAC (6-11).
Madagdagan na lang ang kanilang experience ang hangad ng apat na teams para umpisahan ang preparasyon sa susunod na season.
“This is still a young team and we are looking forward to adding a few more players to fill in for things that we’re missing this year,” ani Letran coach Jeff Napa.
Para kay EAC mentor Ariel Sison, maganda ang hinaharap ng kanyang koponan sa susunod na taon.
“Remy Morad and Igee King are on their last year so we have an almost intact line up next season,” saad ni Sison.
Balik sina team captain Francis Munsayac, Sidney Onwubere at Cameroonian Hamadou Laminou sa Generals sa 93rd season.

Masaya naman si San Sebastian coach Egay Macaraya sa ipinakita ng kanyang mga bataan.
“We had the misfortune of seeing some of our top players getting injured at the start of the season,” wika ni Macaraya. “But we’re happy we made a big leap in the second round, which we could bring into the next season.”
