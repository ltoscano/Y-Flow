Untitled teen nakibuhol sa tuktok By Ramil Cruz June 23, 2016
Sinorpresa ng Mindanao teenager at 14th seed untitled John Marvin Miciano si 13th seed International Master Michael Gotel para sumalo sa tuktok ng men’s division pagkaraan ng third round ng Battle of the Grandmasters 2016 National Chess Championships-Grand Finals sa Philippine Sports Commission National Athletes Dining Hall sa Rizal Memorial Sports Complex.
Umiskor ng back-to-back wins si comebacking ninth seed Woman International Master Beverly Mendoza para gumitgit din sa tuktok sa women’s.
Inakbayan ni 16-years-old Davao City native Miciano, bunso sa tatlong anak ng businessman at housewife at Grade 11 sa FEU-Diliman, sa liderato sina second seed GM Rogelio Antonio, Jr., seventh seed GM Jayson Gonzales, eighth seed IM Paulo Bersamina, 12th seed IM Chito Garma at 10th seed NM Hamed Nouri sa 4.0 points sa men’s. May 3.5 points si GM Eugene Torre kasama ang naka-stalemate sa kanyang si ninth seed FM Narquinden Reyes sa round four.
Ang nagbabalik mula dalawang taong lay-off na si Mendoza ay umiskor ng panalo kina kina WFM Allaney Jia Doroy at sixth seed WFM Marie Antoinette San Diego para makaapat na puntos na rin at sumalo sa tuktok.
May kagaya niyang iskor sina top seed WIM Janelle Mae Frayna, fourth seed WIM Bernadette Galas, fifth seed WIM Jan Jodilyn Fronda at Bernales.
