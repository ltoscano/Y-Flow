I KNOW BETTER!: Sunshine Dizon, NALOSYANG, NAHIWALAY, NAGPAGANDA! By Jun Nardo December 1, 2016
Hindi inurungan ni Sunshine Dizon ang role niya sa Ika-6 Na Utos nang i-offer ito ng GMA. Hawig sa dinaraanang problema sa married life ang role niya.
Asawa niya si Gabby Concepcion sa afternoon drama na iniwan siya’t kumabit sa mas bata sa kanya na pinapelan ni Ryza Cenon.
“Actually, to be honest, alam naman ng GMA eversince that I’ve been interested in doing out of the box characters. It just so happened that unfortunately, something happened, you know, in my life. I won’t deny the fact na it’s quite similar to my story. “But the story is very beautiful. The characters are very nice. It’s not one for me to turn down. Sabi ko nga kina Kuya RJ, Kuya Done (creative writers of the series), hindi naman siguro ako ang nag-iisang artista sa mundo na may ginampanang character na nagkakataon may kapareho sa tunay na buhay.
“I just treated as it is. It’s work. I try to separate my personal life with work. That’s what I do,” pahayag ni Sunshine sa grand presscon ng movie.
May eksena ba siyang kapareha ng nangyari sa buhay niya?
“Ahhh, actually, hindi naman po! Ha! Ha! Ha! Hindi naman po. If ever there is, I’m sorry, I cannot properly discuss it. There is an ongoing case. Siguro, ang masasabi ko lang, kung may similarity, siyempre, lahat ng pinagdadaanan ng mga babaeng pinagdaanan nitong character. You know, you go through the pain, all of the emotions that you go through as a wife, as a sister, as a mother.
“‘Yun po siguro ‘yung similarities,” rason ng aktres.
Hindi naman siya losyang, ‘di ba?

Sa series, nalosyang kasi ang byuti niya, iniwan ng asawa, nagpaganda at lumaban.
“There was a time that I was quite big after two kids and I got so busy taking care of the family. Actually, everybody thought na kaya ako pumayat, is because what happened to me and my husband.

“That’s not actually ang totoo. Kasi actually, maayos naman po kami ng asawa ko when we closed the deal with Belo endorsement. Nagkataon lang talaga ang lahat. You know, I am not the one to explain na kaya ako pumayat. Kasi some people are taking na, ‘Hindi kaya pumayat ‘yan, iniwan ng asawa. May nangyari sa buhay.’
“No, that’s not it. It was a conscious effort and decision, you know, it’s really time already to look my best. You have to be your best,” paliwanag ni Sunshine.
Ryza, halos maduling sa lakas ng sampal ni Sunshine!
Sa series, ang sumalo sa galit ni Shine ay si Ryza Cenon na hindi pa naman ginagawa ang engkuwentro nila eh inihanda na ang sarili at pisngi sa mabibigat na sampal ng aktres.
Kaya naman nu’ng sinampal na siya ni Sunshine, umikot daw ang paningin niya, o halos maduling siya!
“Ang lakas niyang sumampal! Ha! Ha!

Ha!” sey ni Ryza.
“May daya pa ‘yon, ha!” hirit naman ni Sunshine sa sampal niya kay Ryza.
