Battle of GMs susulong By Ramil Cruz June 21, 2016 Magpapang-abot agad sina Grandmaster Rogelio Antonio, Jr. at US-based GM Rogelio Barcenilla, Jr. sa opening round ng 2016 Battle of the Grandmasters-National Chess Championships-Grand Finals Open men’s division sa Philippine Sports Commission National Athlete’s Dining Hall ng Rizal Memorial Sports Complex.
Taya rito ang tigatlong silya na bubuo sa Philippine national men’s and women’s team na isasabak ng National Chess Federation of the Philippines sa 42nd World Chess Olympiad 2016 sa Sept. 1-14 sa Baku, Azerbaijan. Susulong sa torneo ang 14 sa men’s at 12 sa women’s.
Makakasalpukan ni International Master Paulo Bersamina si FIDE Master Narquinden Reyes, makakatapat ni GM Eugene Torre si National Master Emmanuel Emperado. Banggaan sina NM Hamed Nouri-GM John Paul Gomez, IM Chito Garma-IM Haridas Pascua, GM Darwin Laylo-NM Michael Gotel, at NCFP executive director GM Jayson Gonzales-John Marvin Miciano.
Maghahamok sa women’s sina Woman IM Mikee Charlene Suede-WIM Janelle Mae Frayna, WIM Catherine Secopito-Lucelle Bermundo, W FIDE Master Shania Mae Mendoza-WIM Jan Jodilyn Fronda; untitled Judith Pineda-Marie Antoinette San Diego; WIM Bernadette Galas-WFM Allaney Jia Doroy, WIM Berverly Mendoza-WNM Christy Lamiel Bernales at WFM Samantha Glo Revita-Arvie Lozano.
Ang NCFP na ang magpapasya sa tigalawa pang puwesto na kukumpleto sa Azerbaijan-bound OLympic squad.
