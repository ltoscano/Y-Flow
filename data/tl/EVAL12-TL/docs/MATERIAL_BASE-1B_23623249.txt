Utang target din sa China visit By Aries Cano October 21, 2016
Naging prangka si Pangulong Rodrigo Duterte sa kanyang pagbisita sa China at ito ay ang paghingi ng tulong-pinansyal sa anyo ng utang.
“Yes, frankly, actually… I’m going to say I’m not in the kind of really to… I hate to admit it but there’s so many things that we need. We will be asking for the help of China. I do not know if they will — your government will give it to us,” pahayag ng Pangulo sa press briefing kasama ang mga foreign media sa Grand Hyatt Hotel sa Beijing.
“…but along the way, if they would ask me other things which I need and they can help and I would … also the simple ones, not impositions, not financial burdens. We would appreciate if we are given loans, soft loans. We would rather borrow and pay it in a very liberal schedule,” dagdag ng Pangulo.

Bukod pa rito, nakapagsara rin ng 15 memorandum of understanding (MOU) at mga kasunduan ang dalawang bansa.
Bunga rin ng pinainit na bilateral relations ng Pilipinas at China, inanunsyo kahapon ng huli na tinatanggal na nila ang travel warning laban sa Pilipinas kaugnay ng sigalot sa West Philippine Sea (WPS).
Isa umano ito sa napagkasunduan sa pag-uusap nina Pangulong Duterte at Chinese President Xi Jinping.
