Maling-mali si Duterte By Boyet Jadulco, Eralyn Prado SSeptember 7, 201610370 Nangangamba ang ilang senador sa epekto ng pagkansela ng miting ni US President Barack Obama kay Pangulong Rodrigo Duterte dahil sa panlilibak ng huli sa presidente ng Amerika.
Ayon kay Sen. Antonio Trillanes IV, maling-mali si Pangulong Duterte na murahin ang isa sa powerful world leader na kumakatawan sa bansang may matagal nang magandang relasyon sa Pilipinas.
“President Duterte’s vulgar language directed against President Obama is wrong on so many levels and will definitely have detrimental effects on our diplomatic relationship and alliance with the US,” ani Trillanes. “I’m afraid his recent statements vs President Obama will have consequences on our bilateral relations,” pangamba naman ni Sen. Leila de Lima.
Nanghihinayang naman si Senate President Pro-tempore Franklin Drilon sa nakanselang bilateral meeting.
“The scheduled meeting between President Rodrigo Duterte and US President Barack Obama would have been an opportunity for the two heads of states to know each other on a personal level,” ani Drilon.
