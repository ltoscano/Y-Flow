KOBE MUNTIK I-TRADE NG LAKERS
SSeptember 4, 2016
Pangkaraniwang pumasok sa kukote ni Kobe Bryant ang pagpupumilit na i-trade siya ng Los Angeles dahil sa bagsak na tambalan nila ni Shaquille O’ Neal at isa pa uli sa masaklap na taon ng Lakers’ nang umalis si Big Diesel pa-Miami Heat. Ayon sa bagong report, nag-demand si Mamba ng trade sa maaga nyang pamamalagi.
Pahayag ni Lakers coach kay Charley Rosen ng Today’s Fastbreak sa kanyang ongoing series “The Phil Jackson Chronicles.” Hat tip to B/R for the find: “Kobe was only averaging about 19 points per game,” tugon ni Jackson. “So Kobe called Jerry West and wanted to know how Jerry and Elgin Baylor both averaged 30 points.

Kobe also said that he wanted to be traded.
Of course, Jerry told me about the conversation. And, for a few minutes I thought about taking the Pistons up on an offer they made to trade Kobe for Grant Hill. Make that a few seconds.”
Syempre natapos si Kobe, na isa sa bibihira sa hanay ng professional athletes na ginugol ang kanyang entire careers sa nasabing team, sa kabila na may ilang ulit ding nawalan siya ng gana sa maraming okasyon.
Pero nilunok ni Jackson ang bala sa isang ito, lalo’t ang pasibol na career noon ni Hill ay nabatbat ng mga injuries pagkaraan.
