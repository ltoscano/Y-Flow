May madudungisan By Elech Dawa November 8, 2016
Hindi makakalaro si middle blocker Jaja Santiago pero tiwala si Foton coach Moro Branislav na kaya nilang sabayan ang Petron ngayong araw sa Asics Philippine Superliga Grand Prix sa FilOil Flying V Center sa San Juan.
Parehong malinis ang karta ng reigning champion Tornadoes (3-0) at Tri-Activ Spikers (4-0), isa sa kanila ang madudungisan. Sasama si Santiago sa NU squad sa Japan para sa 12-day buildup.
“Jaja will not play in the next 12 days because she needs to go to Japan for her university commitment,” anang Serbian coach. “But it’s okay. I know my players would step up. I’ll prepare them for the next three to four matches.”
Babalikatin nina imports Lindsay Stalzer at Ariel Usher ang Foton katuwang sina locals Angeli Araneta, Maika Ortiz at ang ate ni Santiago na si Dindin Manabat.
Itatapat ng Tri-Activ Spikers sina Frances Molina, Jen Reyes Aiza Maizo-Pontillas, Cherry Nunag at CJ Rosario at reinforcements Stephanie Niemer at Serena Warner.
