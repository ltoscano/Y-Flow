Tagos hanggang puso ang eksena nina Jodi at Kathryn

Posted by: Pete Ampoloquio Jr.
on August 27, 2015
in Showbiz

Isang factor siguro kung bakit well-followed ang Pangako Sa ‘Yo nina Daniel Padilla at Kathryn Bernardo ay ang fabulous presence ni Jodi Sta. Ma-ria who’s delineating the formidable character of Amor Powers.
Sa totoo, na-approximate ni Jodi ang powerful at riveting presence some fifteen years ago ni Eula Valdez. Walang kaduda-dudang kung magaling na aktres si Ms. Eula, hindi rin magpapa-kabog si Jodi.
To be honest about it, nabigyan niya ng millennium coloring ang Amor Po-wers character na tunay namang nakaaaliw.
Anyway, lately, trending talaga ang eksena nina Ina at Amor Powers na with all her sophisti-cation ay may natutunan siya sa simpleng cha-racter ni Kathryn about moving on and letting go.
Kaya if you want some vital lessons in life, keep on watching Pangako Sa ‘Yo nightly right after
Nathaniel.
