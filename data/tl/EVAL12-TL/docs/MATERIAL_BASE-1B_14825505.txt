Borja, Noble umariba sa TriKids Triathlon By Ramil Cruz October 20, 2016
Hindi naging sagabal ang masamang panahon kina Zedrick James Borja at Alison Ann Noble para pangibabawan ang Super TriKids 11-12 boys/girls contest ng 9th Anvaya Cove Invitational Triathlon 2016 nitong weekend.
Nahuli lang ng ilang segundo ang national team member na si Borja sa 300-meter swim sa maalong karagatan ng Anvaya Cove Beach & Nature Club sa Morong, Bataan, pero nakabawi siya sa 4-kilometer bike at 2km run para itakbo ang gold medal sa clocking na 18 minutes, 38 seconds. Nagkasya sa silver-bronze sina Daniel Nazarro (20:23) at Juan Miguell Tayag (21:11).
Sinandalan naman ni Noble ang may kalakihang bentahe sa bike part tungo sa coast-to-coast victory sa 21:46. Second si Katrina Salazar (22:23) at third si Finela Bianca Silamor (36:28). Sa 9-10 age group (200M swim – 2K bike – 1K run) male 1-2-3 sina Dayshaun Karl Ramos (13:37), Connor Thomas Hodges (14:27) at Mark Grist (16:03), sa female ay nanguna sina Aubrey Tom (15:23) at Calista Felisa Silamor (18:53).
Sa aquathlon (25m swim, 200m run), kampeon ang tandem nina Evan Arrow Ramos at Maria Sabine Magsanoc (1:21). Bumuntot sina Sky Guevarra at Angel Cenizal (1:28), at Ener Antonio Regis at Jazmine Fay Borja (1:35).
