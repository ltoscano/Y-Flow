
TECHNOLOGICAL INSTITUTE OF THE PHILIPPINES ACCREDITED NG US – BASED ABET

ANG Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP) ay may State of the Art laboratory equipment, may mahuhusay na mga guro, may de kalidad na management system at may sertipiko mula sa ISO 9001 standards na nagbibigay-kasiguruhan ng isang matatag na proseso, kapaligirang markado ng simple subalit tunay na maaasahan.
Ayon kay (TIP) President Elizabeth Quirino-Lahoz, nagsimula noong 2009 ang Outcomes–Based Education (OBE) at Outcomes – Based Teaching and Learning (OBTL). Nailunsad naman ang TIP’s BID for ABET accreditation noong 2011.
Ang implementasyon ng TIP sa OBE State – of-the-Art laboratories, gayundin ang kalidad ng management system, at ang pagpapatuloy ng quality movement ay nakapagbigay ng pagkakataon sa panawagan para sa international accreditation mula sa US – based ABET, na dating kilala bilang Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology.
Itinuturing ang ABET (www.abet.org) bilang “The Global Gold Standard in Engineering and Computing Education Accreditation.”
Para sa TIP Manila, ang engineering programs accredited ng EAC ay Chemical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Industrial Engineering at Mechanical Engineering. Ang mga computing programs na accredited ng CAC ay Computer Science, Information Systems at Information Technology para sa TIP Manila.
Para naman sa TIP Quezon City, ang mga engineering programs na accredited ng EAC ay ang mga sumusunod, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Mechanical Engineering at Sanitary Engineering.
Na accredited ang computing programs ng TIP Quezon City ng CAC tulad ng Computer Science, Information Systems at Information Technology.
Ang Information Systems programs ng TIP ay tangi at kauna-unahang IS programs accredited ng ABET sa South East Asia.
