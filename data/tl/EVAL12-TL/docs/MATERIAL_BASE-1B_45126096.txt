CIGNAL KUMPLETOS REKADOS By Elech Dawa October 7, 2016
Maliban sa isa, kumpleto na ang imports ng mga teams na maglalabu-labo sa 2016 Asics Philippine Superliga Grand Prix presented by PLDT Home Ultera na sisiklab bukas sa FilOil Flying V Center sa San Juan.
Si Lynda Morales, skipper ng Puerto Rico sa Rio Olympics, ang pinaka-astig sa imports na paparada sa inter-club tournament na suportado ng Mikasa, Mueller, Grand Sport, Senoh at official broadcast partner TV5.
Makakatuwang ni Morales si 6-foot-5 Laura Schaudt na gigiya sa Cignal. Ganito kaaga, team to beat ang HD Spikers na kumpletos rekados dahil kina former MVP Venus Bernal, Paneng Mercado, Janine Marciano at Fil-Am Shawna-Lei Santos. Si high-flying Stephanie Niemer kasama si Serena Warner ng US ang isasagupa ng Petron sa event na binak-apan ng Focus Athletics, Klab Cyscorpions, Foton, Petron at F2 Logistics. Sina veterans Lindsay Stalzer at Ariel Usher ang tinapik ng Foton.
Babanderahan nina Trinidad and Tobago national team member Darlene Ramdin at Polina Liutikova ng Ukraine ang Generika, sina dating US juniors national team star Kierra Host ang Hailie Ripley sa RC Cola-Army.
Kinuha ng F2 Logistics sina Sydney Kemper at dating US juniors national team member Hayley Dora Spelman. Pero wala pa si Spelman na may tinatapos pang international assignment.
“Except for her, all imports are ready to play,” ani PSL president Ramon Suzara.
