Sharon, tagos sa puso at pagkababae By Alwin Ignacio October 18, 2016
WELL-ATTENDED at tila pulos sosyalin ang mga nanood sa first night ng Sharon, ang comeback concert after four years of not being on the legitimate stage ni Megastar Sharon Cuneta sa Theatre at Solaire.
Simple lang ang production values ng concert. May two staircases. May dalawang live bands sa left and right. May LED screen sa gitna.
Panalo ang lighting design. Nakatulong ang mahusay na disenyong pang-ilaw para mas umigting at umapaw ang mga emosyong nakapaloob sa mga kanta ni Mega Shawie.
What made the concert a memorable night, siyempre pa, ay dahil kay Cuneta.
Umaakyat pa lang ang hydraulics at sa entablado, one hears the majestic score of Bituin Walang Ningning, all she did was to say, “Good evening, Solaire!” ang Sharonians were on the palm of her hands.
Lahat were hooked on her charisma at sa katotohanang she was at home and reigning supremely sa Solaire stage that night.
Madaldal, chika kete chika si Cuneta. Ang daming big reveals on life, love, relationships and the wisdom and power of God all through out the two-hour concert.
Lahat ng numbers niya, instantaneous ang applauses. Her voice was in perfect form, filled with so much passion and vibrancy.
Opening numbers niya ang Something’s Coming mula sa West Side Story at mash up ng Fever at Rumors.
Bentang-benta si Shawie lalo na pag pinaglalaruan niya ang kanyang tila never ending battle with the bulge.
Palakpakan ang lahat sa sinabi niyang wala siyang suot na girdle to prove the point na she has successfuly curbed her weight problem.
May updates on her career tatlong pelikula ang kanyang gagawin at may studio album.
Isa sa mga obvious crowd favorites ay ang Something New With My Life, Barbra Streisand arrangement.
Isa sa panalong big reveal ni Ate Shawie ay ‘yung muntik na pala siyang maging Mrs. Robin Padilla.
Para sa kanya, “Two months of beautiful memories ‘yun. I just came from a break up, tapos sinuyo niya ako. Then, he got somebody pregnant, at hindi ako ‘yun. Hahahaha!”
After the spiel, glorious na glorious ang areglo sa Maging Sino Ka Man.
Panalo rin ang recollection niya tungkol sa Mr. DJ, “Si God talaga ang source of my staying power. Imagine, it all started when I was 12, now its 38th years since Mr. DJ happened.
“The song changed my life dramatically.”
Para sa Megastar, “Deeper at full circle moment,” ang sabayang pag-awit nila ni Frankie Pangalinan.
Svelte si Frankie, naka-white top and black long skirt with a front kaya in full display ang kanyang long legs.
Mother Shawie gave her daughter Frankie her moment. Humuhuni lang si Ate Shawie during the Mr. DJ number. Kitang-kita how much they adored each other.
May fast number pa ang mag-ina kung saan may dramarama si Sharon na parang back up dancer siya ni Frankie, na umamin that night na, “Mom, you know that I cannot dance.”
Kaaliw ang dance moves ni Cuneta, very pang-throwback 80s, huh?!
Isa pang heart-tugging moment, ang pag-amin ni Sharon, “My life is an open book. Most of you know that when it comes to matters of the heart, I really keep it inside.
“Matagal akong magdala dito sa loob. This next song is one of my favorites, and one of the most difficult to sing,” at ‘yun ang cue niya para sa Losing My Mind mula sa musical na Follies.
Grabe ang truth at emotional commitment ni Cuneta sa awit. Para nang monologue ang number, at it was almost like an operatic aria kasi.
Every word, every line of the song, ‘yung emotions that she placed in them, tagos sa puso at pagkababae niya.
Ang tears ng diva that you love, plok kete plok habang inaawit niya ito.
Isa pang kwela sa part one of the concert ay ang tribute ni Ate Shawie sa Aegis.
Sabi niya, “From another planet ang bandang ito dahil kakaiba talaga ang boses.”
Sharon belting Aegis? Ay, dito lutang-lutang ang mastery ni Louie Ocampo sa boses at kakayahan ni Cuneta bilang singer.
‘Yung areglo niya, swak na swak at pak na pak sa vocal range ng Megastar.
At Sharon, being the actress that she is, may acting elements ang kanyang renditions of the Aegis classic ditties kaya masigabong palakpakan ang iginawad sa kanya.
Pagkatapos ng ten-minute interval, ang pambungad na awit ni Sharon ay ang a few bars of Ikaw ni Yeng Constantino. Kasunod nito siempre, ang national anthem of mag-sing irog at laging kanta sa kasal, ang Ikaw.
Kinanta rin ni Ate Shawie ang You na mula sa The Carpenters. What Sharon did to these songs, lalo niyang pinatingkad at pinalutang ang kilig at romansa na napakaloob sa mga kanta.
Siempre pa, as promised kumanta ang 24 strong Team Sharon mula sa The Voice Kids.
May solo spot numbers ang kanyang top three na sina Antoinette Tismo, Alvin Dahan at JC Tan.
Ang highlight of the night, ang passing of the torch number kung saan Sharon shared two of her most iconic songs with the twenty four kids she loved dearly, tear inducing talaga ang Bituing Walang Ningning at Pangarap na Bituin mash up.
Standing ovation ang ibinigay kay Sharon Cuneta and her kids after their number.
Bago ang kanyang finale song at encore, Cuneta declared I love you’s sa kanyang mga fans at supporters.
Ang kanyang line, “38th years, ang daming mga young stars na, and you chose to be with me…. salamat.”
Cry kete cry na si Ate Shawie at ang most fitting song to cap the night ay Kahit Maputi Na ang Buhok Ko at ang encore na Sana’y Wala Nang Wakas.
Maliban sa kanyang pamilya na sina Senador Kiko Pangilinan, daughter Miel at son Miguel.
Kuya Ched Cuneta, present sina Ms. Annabelle Rama, Donita Rose at Gary Valenciano. May isa pang performance ang Sharon sa October 22, sa Theatre at Solaire.
