Pilipinas HD, suportado nina Paeng, Toto, atbp.
By Ruel Mendoza June 22, 20161691 Ni-launch noong June 12 ang pinakabagong cable channel na magke-cater sa nationalistic tone ng ating bansa na Pilipinas HD. Ito ay mula sa idea ng veteran sports writer and broadcaster na si Chino Trinidad na ang tanging hangad ay ang i-revolutionize ang programang Pinoy sa pamamagitan ng rich history at culture ng ating bansa.
“I personally think this is very timely with Filipinos wanting change, maybe, a clear and more factual education of our history and culture can address and redefine how we should, as a nation, move forward.
“As a treat for cable operators, from June 12 until the end of the month, Pilipinas HD will be free for subscription. “After that, I hope that cable operators in the country will carry the channel so that it can somehow help how our nation, specially the youth, in learning more about our history,” diin pa ni Chino.
Nakuha nga ni Chino ang suporta ng marami na naniniwala sa paghatid niya ng bagong mga programa para sa mga kabataan ngayon. Kabilang dito sina Paeng Nepomuceno, Eddie Ilarde, MTRCB chairman Atty. Toto Villareal, Mandaluyong Mayor Benhur Abalos at ang kanyang ama na si Recah Trinidad.
“Pilipinas HD will be the medium where stories of unsung heroes will be retold and highlighted. Pilipinas HD levels up Filipino viewing,” dagdag pa ni Chino.
Kabilang sa mga shows na mapapanood sa Pilipinas HD ay ang mga docu-series na Decoding Duterte, Duyan ka Magiting (Cradle of Noble Heroes), Pamana, Filipiknow, Grid, Panatang Pilipino, Ugat ng Lahi and Storya ng Bayan.
