<1>	6 inatado ng ‘baliw’	6 attacked by a 'lunatic'
<2>	Ni Joy Cantos	by Joy Cantos
<3>	MANILA, Philippines – Anim-katao ang malubhang nasugatan matapos na pagtatagain ng kanilang kapitbahay na may diperensya sa pag-iisip sa Barangay Dumalaging, bayan ng Impasugong, Bukidnon kamakalawa.	Manila, Philippines - six people were severely injured after getting hacked by their neighbor with a mental disorder at Barangay Dumalaging, town of Impasugong, Bukidnon yesterday.
<4>	Kasalukuyang ginagamot sa Bukidnon Provincial Hospital ang mga sugatang sina Neil Boy Ayocan, 21; Alejandro Dialong, Fernando Dungo, 60; Mentel Aguiyan,12, Ecsel Aguiyan, 16; at si Pio Tumales, 50.	currently being treated at Bukidnon Provincial Hospital are the injured Neil Boy Ayocan, 21; Alejandro Dialong, Fernando Dungo, 60; Mentel Aguiyan, 12, Ecsel Aguiyan, 16; and Pio Tumales, 50.
<5>	Sa police report na isi­numite sa Camp Crame, lumitaw na sinumpong ng kaniyang mental disorder ang 31-anyos na suspek na si Bryan Okinlay kung saan unang pinagta­taga ang kaniyang mga kapitbahay na sina Ayocan, Dialong, at Dungo.	in the police report submitted at Camp Crame, it showed the mental disorder of the 31-year old suspect, Bryan Okinlay was triggered when he first started hacking off his neighbors Ayocan, Dialong, and Dungo.
<6>	Samantalang sumunod naman nitong pinagtataga sina Aguiyan at Tumales.	next, he then hacked Aguiyan and Tumales.
<7>	Matapos ito ay mabilis na tumakas ang suspek patungo sa hindi pa malamang destinasyon.	after which, the suspect hastily fled to an unknown destination.
