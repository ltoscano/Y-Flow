<7>	Sinimulan kahapon ng Seaoil, ang pinakamalaking independent fuel player­ sa bansa, ang pagbibigay ng P2 rebate sa gasoline at P1 naman sa diesel sa lahat ng mga drivers at mga emplyedo ng GRAB.	Seaoil, the biggest independent fuel player in the country, started giving yesterday a 2 peso rebate for gasoline and 1 peso for diesel to all drivers and employees of GRAB.
<8>	Puwedeng pumunta sa lahat ng Seaoil stations sa buong Metro Manila ang gustong sumali sa Grab Promo­ na ito.	anyone who wants to join this Grab promo can go to all Seaoil stations in the whole Metro Manila.
<9>	Magpakita lang ng Grab ID at Grab driver app sa mga station crew ang mga Grab drivers­, habang Grab ID lang ang ipakikita ng mga Grab ­employees para mabigyan ng nasabing rebate.	Grab drivers only need to show their Grab ID and the Grab driver app to station crews while Grab employees only need to show their Grab ID to get the rebate.
<10>	Malaking tulong ito sa mga Grab drivers dahil kahit na matrapik ng husto, tiyak na may murang ­gasoline silang mabibili sa mga Seaoil stations.	this is a big help to Grab drivers because even if they get stuck in traffic, they can certainly buy cheap gasoline in Seaoil stations.
