<2>	Ni Angie dela Cruz (Pilipino Star Ngayon) | Updated May 10, 2015 - 12:00am	by Angie dela Cruz (Pilipino Star Ngayon) | updated May 10, 2015 - 12:00am
<3>	MANILA, Philippines – Tatlong sundalo ang nasugatan nang maki­pagbarilan sa armadong grupo sa Shariff Aguak, Maguindanao kahapon ng umaga.	Manila, Philippines - three soldiers were injured after exchanging fire with an armed group in Shariff Aguak, Maguindanao yesterday morning.
<4>	Ang mga nasugatang sundalo ay sakay ng isang M35 truck 40th Infantry Battalion at binabagtas ang highway ng Barangay Timbangan nang sumabog ang landmine sa kanilang daraanan.	the wounded soldiers were aboard an M35 truck 40th Infantry Battalion and traversing the highway of Barangay Timbangan when a landmine exploded in their path.
<5>	Dahil dito ay nagkaroon ng barilan sa pagitan ng mga sundalo at mga hinihinalang miyembro ng Bangsamoro Islamic Freedom Fighters (BIFF).	because of this there was an exchange of fire between the soldiers and suspected members of the Bangsamoro Islamic Freedom Fighters (BIFF).
<6>	Pansamantalang na­isara ang kalsadang pa­punta sa bayan ng Mamasapano kayat madami ang nai-stranded na sasakyan na naroon din sa lugar.	the road toward Mamasapano is temporarily closed so many vehicles in that area get stranded.
<7>	Nagpapagaling na sa os­pital ang tatlong sundalo na nasugatan na hindi pina­ngalanan.	the three wounded soldiers who were not named are recovering in a hospital.
