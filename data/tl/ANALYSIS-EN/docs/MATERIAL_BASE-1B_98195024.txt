<1>	Castañeto, Cardona umeksena sa Dagupan leg By Ramil Cruz July 18, 2016Tinakbo ni Cesar Castañeto ang se­cond straight men’s half-mara­thon title, samantalang nakaapat ng korona sa wo­men’s side si Lany Cardona sa kapana-pana­bik na 40th Natio­nal MILO Mara­thon 21K Dagupan City opening leg kahapon ng umaga na nilahukan ng 9,300 kasama na ang sa mga side events na 10k, 5k at 3k.	Castañeto, Cardona took the stage in Dagupan leg by Ramil Cruz July 18, 2016 Cesar Castañeto ran the second men's half-marathon title, while Lany Cardona got four crowns in the women's side in the exciting 40th national milo marathon 21k Dagupan city opening leg yesterday morning participated by 9,300 members including the side events 10k, 5k and 3k.
<2>	Naghulma ang 27-year-old at 2015 Lingayen Philippine Natio­nal Games double silver medalist ng one hour, 11 minutes at 23 seconds sa pagbulaga kay 2006 NMM Finals titlist Julius Sermona na sumegunda sa 1:13:38.	the 27-year-old and 2015 Lingayen Philippine National Games double silver medalist molded one hour, 11 minutes and 23 seconds which surprised 2006 NMM Finals titlist Julius Sermona that came in second at 1:13:38.

<4>	Pinasalamatan ni Castañeto na tumatakbo na sa MILO Marathon short distances sapul nang nasa elementary pa lang, si dating NMM queen Cristabel Martes na nagtiyagang mag-training sa kanya para sa matagum­pay na pagdedepensa ng titulo.	Castañeto, who has been running in the Milo Marathon short distances since he was in elementary, thanked former NMM queen Cristabel Martes who persevered in training him for his successful title defense.
<5>	Pumrimera si Cardona, 25, at tubong Linga­yen, sa women’s side sa 1:31:48 clockings at pagdaig kina Shane Acosta (1:42:27) at Eunice Lata-an (1:44:11) sa 4-in-1 footrace na mga inaayudahan ng Asics, Salonpas, TIMEX, 2XU, Endurance, Hisense, Maynilad at PhilamLife.	Cardona, 25, from Lingayen, came first in the women's side at 1:31:48 clocking and overpowering Shane Acosta (1:42:27) and Eunice Lata-an (1:44:11) in the 4-in-1 footrace that is sponsored by Asics, Salonpas, TIMEX, 2XU, Endurance, Hisense, Maynilad and PhilamLife.
<6>	Nagrasyahan sina Castañeto at Cardona ng tig-P10,000 cash prize bukod pa sa nakaabante sa NMM Finals sa Disyembre 4 sa Iloilo City kung saan paglalabanan ang magiging bet ng Pilipinas para sa 11th Tokyo Marathon 2017 sa Japan sa darating na Pebrero 17.	Castañeto at Cardona were graced with P10,000 cash prize each along with the advantage in the NMM Finals in December 4 in Iloilo City where the bet of the Philippines for the 11th Tokyo Marathon 2017 in Japan this coming February 17 is at stake.
