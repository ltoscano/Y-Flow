<8>	Kumain ng mga malusog na pagkain para bu­maba ang blood pressure.	eat healthy foods to lower the blood pressure.
<9>	Sundin ang Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension o DASH diet na nagbibigay halaga sa prutas, gulay, whole grains, manok, isda at low-fat dairy foods.	follow the Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension or DASH diet that gives importance to fruits, vegetables, whole grains, chicken and low-fat dairy foods.
<10>	Bawasan ang pagkain ng saturated fat, cholesterol at trans fats.	lessen the consumption of saturated fat, cholesterol and trans fats.
<11>	Bawasan din ang asin, asukal at red meats sa diet.	also lessen salt, sugar and red meat in the diet.
<12>	Kumain ng maraming potassium na makikita sa kalabasa, patatas, yogurt, broccoli, saging, gatas, salmon, manok at tuna..	eat a lot of potassium found in squash, potato, yogurt, broccoli, banana, salmon, chicken and tuna.
<13>	Sa mga taong may hypertension o may edad na 51 pataas, bawasan ang paggamit ng asin sa 1,500 milligrams (mg) o 2/3 tablespoon kada araw.	for people with hypertension or with age 51 and up, lessen the use of salt to 1,500 milligrams (mg) or 2/3 tablespoon every day.
<14>	Alamin ang dami ng asin sa mga kinakain lalo na sa de lata para hindi tumaas ang blood pressure.	know how much salt intake especially in canned foods for the blood pressure not to rise.
<15>	Kapag sinunod ang DASH diet, bababa ang blood pressure sa loob ng dalawang linggo.	if DASH diet is followed, blood pressure will be lower within two weeks.

<18>	Ok din ang prutas na de lata basta hindi masyadong matamis.	canned fruit is fine as long as it is not too sweet.
<19>	Uminom ng low-fat o skim dairy products sa almusal o hapunan.	drink low-fat or skim dairy products for breakfast or dinner.
<20>	Dagdagan ang gulay sa ulam.	add vegetable in the viand.
<21>	Sa halip na kumain ng chips, cake o ice cream, maghanap ng pasas, mani, popcorn na walang asin at walang mantikilya o hilaw na gulay.	instead of eating chips, cake or ice cream, search for raisins, nuts, popcorn without salt and oil or raw vegetables.
