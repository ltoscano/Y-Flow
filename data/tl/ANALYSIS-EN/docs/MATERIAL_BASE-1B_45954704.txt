<2>	June 22, 2016	June 22, 2016
<3>	Bunsod nang tumataas na bilang ng mga nagpopositibo sa Human Immunodefiency Virus (HIV) sa Cagayan de Oro City, hinikayat ng isang self-confessed HIV counselor ang mga kalalakihang nakikipagtalik sa kapwa lalaki na magpa­sailalim sa libreng eksaminasyon.	due to the rising number of positive cases of Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) in Cagayan de Oro City, a self-confessed HIV counselor encouraged men who have sex with men to undergo the free examination.
<4>	Sa pinakahuling talaan, umaabot na sa 227 indibidwal na positibo sa HIV ang nagparehistro sa treatment hub ng Northern Mindanal Medical Center (NMMC) sa Cagayan de Oro City.	in the latest record, more than 227 HIV-positive individuals have registered with the Northern Mindanao Medical Center (NMMC) treatment hub in Cagayan de Oro City.
<5>	Ayon sa HIV-AIDS Core Team (HACT), ang naturang bilang ang nakapag-avail ng Anti-retoviral Treatment.	according to HIV-AIDS Core Team (HACT), the said number has availed of the antiretroviral treatment.
<6>	Nag-abiso rin ang City Health Office na magpa-test ang mga indibid­wal na duda sa kanilang health condition, sa darating Hulyo 10 na gaganapin sa Capitol University ng nasabing lungsod.	the City Health Office has also advised individuals who are doubtful of their health condition to undergo testing on July 10 to be held at the Capitol University of the said province.
